We're searching for friendly and engaged moderators who will help us keeping order on Retrospring, making sure that everything is going fine.

KEEP IN MIND THAT THIS IS A VOLUNTEER POSITION AND YOU WILL NOT BE PAID!

Retrospring is an open-source project that aims to be a question and answer platform with social elements. With over 6000 users (as of the writing of this job opening) there often are people that do not behave correctly, and/or according to our Terms of Service. To make the usage and browsing of the site as enjoyable as possible for everyone, we need people that manage in-site reports and handle all kinds of situations, as well as investigate on report claims, so the site's overall environment stays friendly.

Retrospring's moderation should not consist of reporting posts and instantly deleting them. We will (and want you to) only delete and take care of posts that are against our rules or harassingly directed towards another user, behaviour that we don't want to see on our site.

## You should have experience with:

*   Moderation (like on forums, blogs, etc.)
*   Being able to work with all kinds of situations coming towards you, Retrospring is a community with many different individuals you might have to deal with.
*   Judging reports in a fair way, also thinking of the reasoning behind it, looking through on-site conversations to find out if the reported content is not just a reaction to the reporter trying to provocate users.

Being able to work in a team and discussing reports and various other claims is also a key aspect of our moderation workflow, as you are never going to approve a post/user deletion on your own.

## What we can offer:

We can't pay you, since we only get donations to cover our server cost. Retrospring in its current scale does not meet the requirements to get enough money from displaying ads (also, it would be a pain to implement them),

with current web techniques most of the ads would get blocked anyway. But we can hand you official documents for later job applications that verify that you worked with us on Retrospring, showing that you helped us managing an evergrowing community including people from all over the world.
