## About BibliU:

BibliU is changing the future of the multi-billion dollar textbook industry by transforming the way students and universities engage with learning materials. We’re looking for driven, creative candidates with a background in consulting or high-growth tech businesses. You will have the opportunity to work in a startup environment, directly contributing to the success of the company, which recently raised $10M in Series A funding. If you’re looking for an opportunity to accelerate your career at a high growth company with a high level of autonomy, we would love to hear from you.

You will be part of a team that pushes boundaries to provide the most efficient learning experience for academic content worldwide. BibliU has partnered with over 150 universities worldwide and works with more than 1,000 publisher partners to ensure students are receiving the content they need on day one of class. We are a growing team with over 50 full time employees based all around the globe.

## The Role

We are seeking a self-starting, proactive, results-oriented Sales Development Manager to drive the results of our sales development team, through recruiting, training, organizing, coordinating, measuring, coaching, problem-solving, and leading. This is a player-coach position. As Sales Development Manager, you will execute on business strategies and uphold standards to ensure that sales goals are met or exceeded, while also demonstrating best practices by achieving your own targets.

As Sales Development Manager, you will have influence on strategy and tactics, expanding and coaching your team to success, as well as the opportunity to grow your own career as a leader. You will have access to leaders for visibility, mentorship, and to ensure that SDR voices are heard at the top of the organization.

Your responsibilities will include:

*   Running the day-to-day activities of the US- and UK-based Sales Development team and maintaining Outreach processes
*   Achieving quotas, including your own
*   Identifying, recruiting, interviewing, and hiring sales talent and continually “raising the bar” with each new hire
*   Designing and presenting training curriculum, courses, supporting documentation and certifications from on-boarding to continued message congruency and everything in between
*   Coaching Sales Development Representatives to optimum performance
*   Aligning with Marketing on Account-based Marketing campaigns and related outreach
*   Maximizing workflow within the department
*   Mentoring your team members to create clear paths toward their own career goals
*   Identifying problems and proactively creating comprehensive plans of action to present to senior management
*   Coordinating inter-departmentally to drive initiatives tied to sales development
*   Collaborating with the Account Executive department to ensure a smooth hand-off of new opportunities, and reviewing the quality of sales opportunities on a regular basis
*   Creating sales development resources, such as call scripts and prospecting emails to ensure the consistency and effectiveness of our messaging
*   Measuring and reporting against KPIs, as well as creating reports as needed or requested by senior management
*   Preparing for and contributing to status or ad hoc meetings

## Job requirements

*   2 years minimum of sales experience as a top performer, including time as a team lead, coach, or manager, preferably in a sales development role at a fast-paced, SaaS company
*   Thrive in a goal-oriented environment
*   Be flexible and adaptable to changing conditions at a fast-paced, growing company
*   Have a demonstrated ability to engineer and optimize systems and processes
*   Ability to learn quickly and you are always searching for ways to expand your knowledge
*   Helping your employees grow and move to their next challenge excites you
*   Familiarity with tools such as Outreach, SalesForce, ZoomInfo, 6sense, Gong/Chorus, LinkedIn Navigator
