## PhD and Masters University Grad Position-Health Applications Software Engineer

_Our company vision is to amplify human potential. Our mission is to deliver enterprise a powerful tool for transformation—an augmented reality platform of great utility and simplicity. Achieving our goals requires passion and dedication. That’s why we’re committed to building and empowering a diverse team of incredibly talented people and fostering an inclusive culture through our values of unity, innovation, and user centricity._

Magic Leap is looking for PhD and Masters students to join our team for a 3 month Summer Program for Underrepresented Students in Engineering.  In partnership with STEM departments at Historically Black Colleges and Universities (HBCUs), Hispanic Serving Institutions (HSIs), Women In Engineering Institutions, and broader universities we are committed to empowering and elevating diversity of thought, experience, and background in our workforce and in our pipeline of future researchers!

## Job Description

During this program, you will work on the development of an advanced app prototype designed to showcase the use of digital biomarkers using spatial computing. Under the guidance of the Health software technical leads, you will work within an agile development environment to understand and implement software requirements and develop appropriate unit tests. At the end of the program, you will have created a self-contained prototype of a visualization app that will be used to explore novel ways to collect and present health information for both physicians and patients.

## Responsibilities

*   Implement a self-contained Unity prototype for digital biomarker data visualization.
*   Develop with a strong focus on user experience and interface design.
*   Develop software, perform code review, and create and maintain comprehensive design documentation, specifications and tests that fulfill the requirements for regulated code development.
*   Troubleshoot and resolve software defects and other technical issues.
*   Write elegant, maintainable, reusable code; leveraging test driven principles to develop high quality applications, algorithms, and services.

## Must-Haves 

*   Experience coding & debugging of C, C++ and/or C#.
*   Experience performing high-level design and architecture of front-end applications
*   Interest and academic background in medical technology and/or health-related software applications.
*   Experience with game engines, especially Unity, a strong plus.

## Education

Currently working towards a PhD or Masters in Biomedical Engineering, Software Engineering, Computer Science, or related fields.
