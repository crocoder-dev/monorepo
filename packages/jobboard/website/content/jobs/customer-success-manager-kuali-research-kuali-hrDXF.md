## Description

**Who are we?**

Kuali builds software solutions for higher education. We help our customers — colleges & universities — focus on providing a fantastic education to students by decreasing their administrative costs. We work in a competitive space, ripe for innovation, with users ready to be delighted.

**Our culture**

As a company, we are guided by our [cultural values](https://www.kuali.co/jobs#values):

*   Iterate to evolve
*   Cultivate openness
*   Act with accountability
*   Assume the best
*   Practice humility
*   Deliver amazing experiences
*   Make mistakes

As team members at Kuali, we learn from and teach each other, we practice transparency and empathy, and we delight in delivering value to our customers.

We work remotely, and have for years. Distributed work is in our bones, with a history of institutions working across state lines on open-source software for more than ten years. Our employees each work in the environment where they’re happiest, from Massachusetts to Hawaii. We work consciously to create a collaborative and healthy remote work culture, and we travel to meet in person a few times each year.

Everyone should love their work.

Kuali has been voted a [top place to work](https://topworkplaces.com/publication/saltlake/kuali/) for 3 years in a row by the Salt Lake Tribune. We also made [Forbes' list of America's Best Startup Employers](https://www.forbes.com/americas-best-startup-employers/#1100e1e65275) for 2020. Not too shabby.

Your team

You will be part of the Kuali Research Customer Success team, working closely with Customer Success and Product colleagues, who are focused on helping customers get the most value from their investment in our software, and the team-members who design and build our products. Customers use our Research product to efficiently and effectively manage the complex research administration and compliance needs.

At Kuali, a Customer Success Manager is responsible for developing customer relationships that promote retention and loyalty including providing project management, training and professional services. The Customer Success Manager works closely with customers to ensure they are satisfied and partners with customers to solve problems. The Customer Success Manager owns the account relationship after the sales process is complete and remains the primary customer contact throughout the customer lifecycle. Customer Success Managers also play an active role in sales demonstrations and are expected to be experts in both, Kuali Research product and the business domains it supports.

## Requirements

*   Oversee multiple, simultaneous customer implementations and provide other onboarding assistance as needed (e.g. project managements, functional / business domain consulting, business process analysis, system configurations, training, etc.).
*   Become a trusted advisor to multiple people at the customer school and become the customer’s voice within the company.
*   Monitor the health of the customer relationship.
*   Solve problems that arise during the customer lifecycle to ensure continued satisfaction.
*   Communicate with customers on a regular basis to share best practices and provide information about new features that can support their strategic goals.
*   Share customer feedback with the Product team.
*   Share customer success stories with the Marketing team.
*   Manage the customer renewal process.
*   Participate in Kuali Research sales demonstrations and help with RFP responses.
*   Stay up to date our continuously delivered products, features and integration capabilities.

**Skills and Experience (Required):**

*   Excellent interpersonal skills; highly invested in developing strong relationships both with customers and colleagues.
*   Technical aptitude with the ability to understand and communicate technical concepts in a clear and simplistic way.
*   Proactive and sensitive to customer needs.
*   Strong communication skills, both spoken and written.
*   Comfortable presenting information to various levels of stakeholders.
*   3-5 years of experience in research administration, compliance, and/or experience in the consulting field.
*   1-3 years of experience implementing or supporting an enterprise-class system at an institution of higher education or similar institution.
*   The ability to multi-task effectively in a fast-paced environment.

Skills and Experience (Desirable):

*   Project Management certification
*   Business Analysis certification
*   You’ve used Kuali Research
*   You’ve worked in a Higher Education Research Administration or Compliance office/department

Other things you should know:

*   You’ll have the opportunity to travel. The travel requirement could be 50%
*   This team is (and has always been) fully remote. You’d be expected to have a suitable home working environment or alternative. We try to get together in person as a team or company 2-4 times a year.
