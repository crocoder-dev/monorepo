## Requirements

#### **In this role**

We are looking for an experienced B2B SaaS high-tech Growth Marketing Manager to join our team and make an immediate impact across all of our Demand Generation programs, activities and campaigns as we continue our rapid growth. As part of a small team, this role will give the right candidate the opportunity to dive deep into all aspects of proactive integrated campaigns to a well-defined set of personas and companies through paid and earned channels and activities focusing on creating engagement converting into measurable pipeline as we accelerate our growth and trajectory.

This role will have company-wide visibility and impact as you’ll collaborate closely with Product Marketing, DevRel, Sales, Customer Success, and the broader Marketing team.

#### **Day to day, you will:**

Along with your team...

*   Plan, build, execute, follow-up, optimise and report on campaigns and activities to contribute to pipeline creation through paid and earned tactics to well defined targeted personas and accounts
*   Oversee Marketing content editorial calendar to align to new capabilities being launched and new themes or topics relevant to our key audiences through integrated campaign execution
*   Develop strategies for both acquiring and re-engaging leads that drive awareness, consideration and purchase intent across all channels including paid media, email nurturing, webinars, paid programs and virtual events
*   Virtual and field demand gen events to raise company and solution awareness and generate engaged qualified leads from sponsoring, speaking and marketing opportunities
*   Full responsibility for digital marketing: display, prospecting, retargeting, remarketing, Search Marketing, syndication and peer reviews with agency support where needed
*   Have a strong understanding and focus around ABM campaigns to proactively land in high intent and right profile accounts
*   Proactively partner with our sales teams and sales development managers to ensure seamless interactions for lead follow-up, lead development and account penetration
*   Align and manage Marketing’s frugal budget with overall Sales and Marketing models and constantly identify new approaches, optimization tests, tactics and growth opportunities for efficient growth, better lifetime value and lower CAC across Marketing Qualified Leads MQL production and conversation

#### **The ideal candidate will:**

*   Have 5 years of B2B demand generation or campaigns marketing with demonstrated lead, pipeline and revenue growth, preferably in enterprise high-tech SaaS
*   Deep demand gen functional knowledge and experience with modern MarTech stack methods and tooling, including ABM strategies, experience with Hubspot is ideal
*   Analytically-driven decision maker, strong attention to detail with the ability to capture and share key data and insights and use data to drive key marketing strategies and decisions
*   Strong written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills focussed including engaging email and digital ad copy
*   Extremely organized with excellent project and time management skills
*   A self-starter that can work with minimal direction to get the job done perfectly as an individual contributor while leading and managing a small team

#### **What’s it like to work at Ably?**

Ably is entering a new phase of growth led by an experienced senior team. You’ll be part of a successful, highly motivated, and extremely collaborative team.

To provide the infrastructure required for the next generation of the internet, to tell the world about it, and to support everyone inside Ably to do so we hire people who are ready and capable of taking on challenging problems in a high-growth environment.

We want to work in a company of inclusive people who treat each other well. To really thrive at Ably, you’ll need to believe in and be prepared to do your work in line with our values, which are:

*   **Open for all.** Open is a mindset. When open, we’re better collaborators, communicators and creators. Whether open source, standards, or protocols, open hearts, minds, and eyes. It’s better when it’s open.
*   **Bias for action.** We avoid analysis paralysis at all costs. We lean into doing in order to move us forward. When we have enough of what we need to act, we act.
*   **Even the best do better.** We want to be the best - but that doesn’t mean we rest when we are. Improvement and growth - true betterment - is an ongoing journey. One fuelled by curiosity and taking risks.
*   **Technology needs humanity.** Our technology means little without people - whether that’s how we treat our colleagues, or how we design developer experiences. Being human matters.
*   **Dependable people make dependable things.** Dependability is integral to our mission and technology, but being dependable starts with our people. When we care enough to take real ownership of our products and problems - all else will follow.
*   **Bold creativity leads to the greatest rewards.** When viewed through a different lense, what stands in the way can become the way. The bigger the challenge the easier the choice: bold creativity will always move us forward, create better outcomes, and set us apart.
