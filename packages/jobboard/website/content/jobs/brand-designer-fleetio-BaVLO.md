## Description

Fleetio is a modern software platform that helps thousands of organizations around the world manage their fleet operations. Transportation technology is a hot market and we’re leading the charge, with raving fans and new customers signing up every day. We're looking for an innovative and collaborative designer to create assets for use across a wide range of digital marketing channels.

Design is an integral part of the Fleetio culture and informs how we build products and communicate value to our market. In fact, one of our core beliefs as a company is “Design is an important part of everything we do.”

We are looking for a Brand Designer who not only has prolific visual design abilities but also has a self-motivated creative spirit and strong collaborative DNA.

As a part of our growing Marketing Team, you’ll spend a significant amount of time working with the our content and digital marketers. You’ll handle design work to support our content marketing efforts (think case studies, infographics, email, social media graphics, video and more), web, events (large format banners, etc.) and other marketing activities. This also means testing new design concepts, ensuring brand consistency and helping the brand grow. Be sure to mention your favorite font in your application so we know you actually read this.

## What you'll be doing

*   Drive visual design projects such as marketing content (case studies, infographics, video, etc.) sales collateral, presentations and trade show signage
*   Provide assets to help strengthen and maintain our digital presence including our marketing website, emails, blog, social media, etc.
*   Assist with front-end website work across our marketing site
*   Bring new ideas for design and content creation to the team using your expertise and eye for great design
*   Raise the bar for design quality across all assets
*   Compellingly communicate and present design solutions, across a range of mediums, to stakeholders and other designers
*   Collaborate with multi-disciplinary team members to develop and maintain brand guidelines and assist our team in creating new assets
*   Get to know our market and create dynamic visuals that fit their needs
*   Actively contribute to and grow our brand's design system

## **What’s in it for you**

*   Be a part of an incredible team of “A” players who go above and beyond to make Fleetio a successful company. We’re a customer-centric team with a great product, excellent support, and countless happy customers.
*   Use the latest tools and techniques to grow a massive audience in a global marketplace (we already have paying customers in over 70 countries).
*   Work remotely (within the United States), or at our Birmingham, AL HQ. We strive to promote a strong remote working culture and have done so since the beginning.

## Requirements

*   1-3 years of experience as a professional designer, preferably within a fast-paced SaaS environment
*   A world-class design portfolio, showcasing a strong foundation in Typography, Color Theory and Visual Design
*   Expertise in Adobe Creative Suite (Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign), Sketch and/or Figma
*   Comfortable working not only with pixels, but print as well
*   Excellent organizational skills to work independently and efficiently, managing projects with many moving parts
*   Exceptional collaboration skills to work with the marketing team and other teams to concept, iterate on and complete projects
*   Personal interest in staying up-to-date on the latest trends and emerging best practices in the design community
*   Technical experience working in front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
*   Experience in Cinema4D and/or After Effects a huge plus
