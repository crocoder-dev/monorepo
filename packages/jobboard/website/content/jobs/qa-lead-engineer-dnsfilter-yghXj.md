QA Lead Engineer:

DNSFilter was born out of necessity. Our founders realized that the existing solutions for DNS security out there were clunky, convoluted and over priced. In 2015, they set out on a simple yet crucial mission—to revolutionize DNS security. We’ve come a long way since our humble beginnings. We support nearly 14,000 organizations, including Fortune 500 customers like NVIDIA, and provide service to millions of end users. Our infrastructure is made up of two anycast networks with over 300 servers in 53 datacenters. At the start of 2020, our software was processing 1B queries/day. One year later, we’re now processing 12B queries/day! In the middle of a global pandemic, our team (and product) thrived. We doubled the size of our team, and we’re looking to do it all over again in 2021.

To ensure the highest level of software quality, we need a remote QA Lead Engineer who has a track record of hunting down and squashing bugs, developing and executing strategic test plans, and is independent and intrinsically motivated. You can take control and lead the team while contributing directly to manual and automated tests. We are looking for someone who can work with at least a 5hr overlap with EST hours. Unfortunately at this time we are unable to provide visas to applicants.

You will:

*   Coordinate and oversee the QA department
*   Manage Testing Schedules to meet project deadlines and expectations
*   Perform manual tests against our web app, API and Android and iOS Apps
*   Expand test cases covered by our automated testing
*   Research potential bugs brought up by customers
*   Communicate with stakeholders to estimate, report progress and blockers and provide feedback for testing new features or adding to coverage of existing features
*   Use your initiative to move the business forward
*   Maintain our internal documentation
*   Work with Development manager to develop QA Processes and Procedures

You have:

*   Native English speaking ability. Testing will also need to include verifying UI text
*   Proven experience with software testing including developing test strategies and plans and executing them
*   Some experience with JavaScript and front end technologies such as HTML and CSS to contribute to automated tests written in frameworks such as Nightwatch, Cypress or Cucumber Gherkin
*   Effective verbal and written communication; this role as QA team leads provide reports to developers and engineers to improve software and systems
*   Live within 5 hours of Eastern Standard Time, and are able to work close to a standard work day in Eastern Standard Time

Bonus points for:

*   Experience working with an automation framework tool such as Nightwatch, Cypress or Cucumber Gherkin
*   Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering or similar field, or extensive experience in the software industry
*   Experience testing content for web apps, Android and iOS
*   Knowledge or experience of agile development (e.g. SCRUM, Kanban) and continuous integration
*   Have experience working remotely

We Offer:

*   A 100% work-from-home position with a company that values and fosters personal and professional growth
*   Passionate and intelligent colleagues who work hard and have a good time doing it
*   Competitive salary
*   Flexible Vacation Policy
*   Stock Options
*   Full medical, dental, and vision benefits for US Based Employees
*   Full short-term disability and life benefits; available long-term disability
