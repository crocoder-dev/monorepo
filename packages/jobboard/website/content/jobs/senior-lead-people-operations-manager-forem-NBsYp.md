## Job description

As Forem’s Lead People Operations Manager, you will lead and grow our HR operations, team happiness initiatives, and recruiting strategy. You will embody our company values and will foster a high-performing, anti-racist and inclusive culture of transparency, trust, and collaboration. You are a strategic thinker that is excited about designing, deploying, and optimizing our internal processes

You will oversee a wide range of responsibilities from payroll administration/benefits to conflict resolution to employee hiring and offboarding. You will be an integral part of our leadership team and will report directly to our Chief Operations Officer.

**Primary responsibilities**

*   Own, manage, and enhance implementation around benefits, and compensation, including regular updates against market comparable and new best practices
*   Ensure the People Operations department can provide the best possible service to all teammates to enable their best work - generally via fast (24 hour SLA or better) response times to teammate questions
*   Regularly reevaluate our benefit offerings and experience around benefits - including monitoring utilization and other statistics
*   In partnership with our legal team, own all workforce-related compliance and policy matters, including consulting/employment agreements and compliance trainings
*   Oversee and enhance our entire hiring process from start to finish, including talent outreach, compensation discussions, and new team member onboarding/offboarding
*   Work with managers to oversee and execute performance reviews and promotion evaluations
*   Manage people programs to foster team member fulfillment and team camaraderie
*   Develop and execute DEI initiatives
*   Collect feedback and monitor team morale to suggest cultural improvements
*   Lead all conflict resolution needs and act as an advocate for, and resource to, all team members to ensure their perspectives are heard and considered
*   Manage, mentor, and coach your people operations teammates.

## Requirements

**Skills or Experience We’d Like To See**

*   Comfortable “managing up” to the executive team
*   High emotional intelligence and excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to generate enthusiasm and confidence from others
*   Ability to work with highly sensitive and confidential materials
*   Exceptional planning and technical skills with the ability to manage multiple high-visibility projects simultaneously
*   Proven ability to deal with conflict and diffuse difficult situations
*   Demonstrates alignment in the value of building diverse, inclusive teams
*   Experience working asynchronously and in a remote environment
*   Deep understanding and commitment to our company values and principles
*   A high degree of empathy
*   Thoughtful understanding of the challenges and opportunities involved in growing a globally distributed team
