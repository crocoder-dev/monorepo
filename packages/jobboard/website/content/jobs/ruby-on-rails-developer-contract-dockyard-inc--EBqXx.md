## Job description

## WHO WE ARE

DockYard is looking for a **Ruby on Rails Developer (Contract)**. We are a 100% remote digital product consultancy. Our in-house team of product strategists, product designers, and software engineers specialize in delivering web applications with a user-driven approach and mindset. We also provide expert staff augmentation and consulting to help clients build fast, scalable, and reliable products and services.

## WHAT WE DO

We help ambitious companies transform new ideas into fully deployed web applications. Our clients range from seed funded and early stage startups, who depend on us to define and deliver business-critical workflows, to established enterprise companies seeking fresh perspective and deep expertise on new projects.

The ideal candidate is self-driven and passionate about web technologies and industry best practices, and has a strong understanding of how to interface with clients.

## LEVEL

Mid to Senior-level

## LOCATION

Remote (U.S. based only)

## EMPLOYMENT TYPE

Contract (expected 32 hours/week)

## RESPONSIBILITIES

*   Work independently or with other engineers to maintain a large-scale Ruby on Rails legacy codebase.
*   Collaborate with client stakeholders and other engineering teams to understand business goals, analyze requirements, and partner on solutions.
*   Assist in scoping, estimating, and planning of projects.
*   Participate in project planning, standups, code review, improvement suggestions, and other team activities.

## Job requirements

## MUST HAVES

*   3-5 years of experience in Ruby on Rails development
*   Experience with relational databases such as PostgreSQL
*   Understands best practices for web development and software design, and relishes opportunities to transform complex, legacy codebases into robust, scalable, and maintainable systems
*   Excellent communication and collaboration skills

## NICE TO HAVES

*   Basic familiarity with using Docker
*   Familiarity with building APIs with Grape
*   Prior client services, consulting, or freelancing experience
*   Experience working in a remote team
