## Description

As a Customer Success Specialist focused on Fleetio’s [Maintenance Shop Integration](https://www.fleetio.com/manage/features/fleet-maintenance-shop) (MSI) onboarding, your job is to engage customers and ensure adoption of our software for their outsourced fleet maintenance workflows. You’ll achieve this by establishing a strong rapport with key customer and repair shop stakeholders. You will educate customers on both sides of the marketplace on how the integration works and its benefits, and help them fully implement the MSI feature.

Fleetio is a modern software platform that helps thousands of organizations around the world manage their fleet operations. Transportation technology is a hot market and we’re leading the charge, with raving fans and new customers signing up every day.

As a member of the Customer Success team, you will manage a portfolio of customers and prospects for our Maintenance Shop Integration, as well as help to expand our Shop Vendor Network. You will be in constant communication with your portfolio sharing your knowledge and bringing back important insights. You will be a critical player in driving long-term customer success at Fleetio.

We’re looking for a personable, detail-oriented, and driven team member who excels at helping customers adopt new technology to develop more successful habits. Someone who can leverage their existing automotive expertise to easily relate to, empathize with and educate our marketplace. We take customer service seriously and consider this an essential role to the future success of our Maintenance Shop Integration. Be sure to mention coffee in your application so we know you actually read this.

## WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING

*   Be an expert on fleet management, vehicle maintenance, and our shop network; connecting our software’s value to the goals and objectives of the marketplace
*   Engage with new customers with unique, value-driven communication and tenacious follow-up
*   Demonstrate the power of embracing MSI to independent maintenance shop operators and help them onboard
*   Measure, track and analyze results to optimize MSI onboarding programs
*   Re-engage customers with low activity trends to increase adoption
*   Track tasks against a schedule, execute onboarding efforts and coordinate with internal staff to ensure milestones are met on a timely basis
*   Strive for 100% customer satisfaction and happiness during the onboarding process
*   Bring the voice of the customer to the team to deliver a best-in-class customer experience
*   Help identify and build great customer success practices across the team

## Requirements

*   Strong customer service and communication skills and derive satisfaction from helping others.
*   At least 2 years experience working a customer-facing role in the transportation, automotive, or fleet sector (e.g. experience working with or in a lube shop, tire shop, repair facility, or dealership).
*   A technical knowledge of automotive maintenance is a plus.
*   Your work is meticulous and precise when executing a task. When it comes to details, you are painstakingly accurate and consistently thorough.
*   You are passionate about organization and can adhere to and improve organizational systems. If there’s a way to reduce redundant work, you’re on it.
*   The ability to collaborate and thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic, and evolving environment.
*   Comfortable with change, with experimenting, and with trying new ideas.
*   Prior experience as a SaaS customer support specialist, business analyst, onboarding specialist, or training specialist is a huge plus, but not necessary.
*   Prefer experience working with CRM systems (e.g., Salesforce), online project management tools (e.g., Asana), internal communication tools (e.g., Slack), Google Sheets, Google Docs, etc.
