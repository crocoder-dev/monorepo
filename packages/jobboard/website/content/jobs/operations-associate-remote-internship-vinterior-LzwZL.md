## Description

You love numbers and want to work in a startup because change and challenges drive you?

You might be our next Operations Associate!

**Who are we?**

We are a fast-growing UK- based marketplace who sells Vintage furniture and Antiques from dealers all around Europe.

We help professionals and consumers to find products that match the values that matter to us: character, a passion for craftsmanship and an obsession for delivering exceptional customer service.

We’re only five years old, but we’re already disrupting the furniture industry: since starting, our sales have increased a hundredfold. We are a passionate team who want to offer sustainable furniture and share the love of unique antiques and vintage pieces!

We're looking for a determined and tenacious Operation Associate to join our growing start-up - Vinterior. You will be part of our Customer Experience team and will also have the opportunity to work closely with our head of CX and our COO. This is a great chance for someone who is looking to develop their career within an exciting environment.

**What you will do:**

*   Finance: Book-keeping, end of month reporting, cash-flow reconciliation.
*   Sellers relation: Seller payouts, payment support, invoicing.
*   B2B support: Creating proformas, checking payments flows, requesting invoices, payment support.
*   Clients relation: Be the point of contact for our buyers and sellers with regards to their payments, answer questions and support them through their selling/purchasing journey.
*   Analytics: Assisting the Head of CX and the COO with reporting.
*   And get involved with other projects that excite you!

## Requirements

*   Bachelor's Degree or equivalent with 1-3 years office management and/or administrative assistance experience
*   General MS Office / Google Suite experience - good command of Excel or equivalent is essential
*   Experience with Looker, Tableau or SQL is a plus
*   Organised and detail-oriented
*   Strong time-management skills
*   Quick and independent learner
*   Excellent communication skills
*   Positive and approachable personality
*   Reliable
*   Team player
*   Embrace change and feedback
*   Eligibility to work in the UK
*   Available asap

## Benefits

*   23 days holiday (on top of public holidays)

*   Employee discount on products.
