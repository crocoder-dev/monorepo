Collabora is looking for a junior engineer with passion for free/libre open-source software (FLOSS) to join our sysadmin team developing, maintaining, upgrading and growing our fully open-source Debian-based infrastructure.

You are going to be the go-to person for all our Cambridge IT-related matters (laptops, ISP/Fiber, local server room, WLAN, IP phones, printer). Your day-to-day will consist in supporting both non-technical and technical people, in person and via a ticketing system across multiple time-zones, while having the opportunity to learn and contribute to our Linux infrastructure systems.

## What you will do:

*   Support of non-technical people on general Linux troubleshooting
*   Triage reported issues according to impact and severity
*   Work collaboratively with the rest of the team
*   Typical day-to-day support:
*   Creation and provisioning of new user accounts and deployment into different systems
*   Deal with access requests to various services/systems, mailing lists management and password refreshes
*   Build and maintain Debian-based Linux images to setup laptops whenever required
*   Troubleshoot and support a variety of open source applications, servers and services
*   Participate in Linux Infrastructure projects (debug, maintenance, update/upgrade...)

## Some of the technologies we use and you will be exposed to:

*   Debian-based Linux servers and services
*   Virtual Machine infrastructure (oVirt, libvirt, kvm...)
*   Docker containers
*   Monitoring system (Zabbix)
*   Cloud / Hybrid infrastructure deployment and operation 
*   Scripting / automation, eg Shell, Python, Chef, Ansible
*   General networking - VPN, Wifi, VLANs, Firewalls, DHCP, DNS
*   Server and client collaboration tools - Email/Calendaring (Dovecot, Postfix, SoGo), File sharing, Git, ticketing systems, CollaboraOnline
*   CI, Agile, DevOps, Infrastructure as Code

## What we are looking for:

*   1-3 years of related experience in the following areas:
*   _Linux Administration (Debian, CentOS, Ubuntu...)_
*   _Network Administration_
*   _System / Network Troubleshooting experience_

*   Customer support mindset
*   Owning a problem end to end and working through to resolution, putting the customer first
*   Strong appetite to learn
*   Flexible and comfortable with changes
*   Highly communicative
*   Excellent documentation skills
*   Fluent in English

Collabora is a leading software consultancy specialising in bringing companies and the open source software community together. We combine years of open source software leadership with an understanding of the challenges that businesses, non-profits, and governments face. Collabora brings deep technical expertise in system integration, architecture, graphics, multimedia, web engines and communications to a number of market verticals, including mobile communications, IVI / automotive, set top box/smart TV, and a range of other specialised embedded applications.

Collabora has the unique business model of enabling enterprises to leverage Open Source software in their solutions. Having worked with notable industry leading companies in the automotive, aerospace and handset mobile verticals amongst many others, Collabora has established a broad customer portfolio.
