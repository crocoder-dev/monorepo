## Requirements

#### **In this role**

We are looking for a Financial Analyst, to support the Finance and Operations Lead, by improving processes and efficiencies to support a scaling business.

Your responsibilities will range from advising on financial controls, monitoring and reporting on key performance indicators, leading the month end & assisting on financial reporting.

#### **Day to day, you will:**

*   Assist with the day to day operations of the finance function
*   Track and process the appropriate key performance indicators to highlight trends, explain variances and maintain a core financial dashboard
*   Own the month end reporting cycle by ensuring invoices have been accounted for, revenue recognition has been achieved and analysing actuals vs budget & forecast
*   Analyse financial information and provide real-time reports to support management with decision-making
*   Prepare investment-related documents for internal and external audiences
*   Work on process improvement and efficiencies to support the business as we scale
    *   Identify areas of inefficiencies within our reporting systems
    *   Lead projects to reduce these inefficiencies and build scalable solutions

#### **The ideal candidate will:**

*   BePart-qualified or qualified CIMA/ACCA/ACA
*   Have aBachelors / graduate degree in Economics, Maths, Finance or equivalent
*   Have advanced computer skills with proficiency in Google Sheets, Excel, Xero and project management software
*   Be a commercially focussed finance professional with a proactive approach and a willingness to get things done
*   Have strong analytical skills with the ability to understand complex data and key performance indicators
*   Be ahighly organised individual with strong attention to detail
*   Have the ability to work well within a team and across a range of business functions
