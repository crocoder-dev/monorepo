## Job Brief

We’re looking for a dynamic and creative content creator to join our editorial team and assist with researching and writing blog posts, web copy and marketing copy.

There is no ‘typical day’ at Invisible, and every day will bring something new to the table. You could be doing anything from conducting thorough research on industry-related topics such as AI or automation, generating ideas for new content types and proofreading articles before publication.

The ability to meet deadlines is essential. You will be a master of adapting your writing style to suit different audience segments and identifying customer personas.

Ideally, you’ll be a self-confessed technology geek with a portfolio of published articles from either your own blog or from companies you have worked with.

## As a Content Writer On The Invisible Team You Will:

*   Be passionate about all things tech! Perhaps you’re an AI nerd? Or an automation pro? If so, let’s talk!
*   Be a master curator - research and aggregate the latest news and views on outsourcing, machine learning, AI, etc
*   Prepare well-structured drafts using digital publishing platforms
*   Have a keen eye for detail - as part of our eagle-eyed team you will edit and proofread written pieces before publication
*   Conduct keyword research and use SEO guidelines to turbo-charge content
*   Identify target audiences and recommend exciting new topics
*   Coordinate with marketing and design teams to illustrate articles
*   Measure web traffic to content (e.g. conversion and bounce rates)
*   Update our websites as needed
*   Coordinate with our awesome marketing team
*   Ensure all-around consistency (style, fonts, images and tone)

## Requirements

*   Proven work experience as a Content Creator, Copywriter or similar role
*   Portfolio of published articles
*   Hands-on experience with Content Management Systems (e.g. WordPress)
*   Excellent writing and editing skills in English
*   An ability to fact-check long-form content pieces
*   Time-management skills
*   Familiarity with SEO
*   BS in English, Journalism, Marketing or relevant field
