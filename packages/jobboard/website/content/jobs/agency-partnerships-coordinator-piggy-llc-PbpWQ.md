[JoinPiggy](https://www.joinpiggy.com/) is looking for an Agency Partnership Coordinator US-based to join our Partnership Team and support us in hitting 100% target revenues and consolidate great partnerships with our agencies and merchants.

## Location: Remote - USJob Description:

Initial need would be to assist with the footwork for agencies:

*   Onboarding new advertisers
*   Updating internal Advertiser list quarterly to keep Quickisght reporting up to date

Second phase of job responsibilities would be to work on growing underperforming advertisers:

*   Pulling performance data from top 6 agencies and locating opportunities to pitch higher CB levels (to better compete with competition) and placement packages based on a offers to promote or high CR/AOV.
*   Monitoring advertiser performance to watch for unexpected up or down trends

Future responsibilities would include:

*   Setting up and attending conference meetings with Agency representatives and/or their clients
*   Updating and distributing Agency Media Kits quarterly to all agencies
*   Translating into European languages for international distribution
*   Mid Tier Optimization
*   Take the advertisers that make $2-5,000 in commission a month (not enough to be managed by the Partnership team but still worthy of attention) and optimize with them in terms of offers, cashback rates, and then placements.

Who we're looking for:

*   1-2 years working experience in Partnership Development or Sales field
*   Proactive and hands-on approach
*   Details-orientation and precision
*   Excellent English skills -both oral and written

Benefits

*   Work remotely from home (also after COVID-19 crisis)
*   Competitive salary
*   Join a great team ready to expand

## Why joining JoinPiggy

JoinPiggy is an International money-saving destination that allows its users to acquire incentives when shopping online, in the form of cashback and/or Discount codes. We offer our users our services via a browser extension, localized websites, email, and social marketing.

We work fully remotely in a fast-paced environment. Flexibility, proactivity, and getting things done are our keywords.
