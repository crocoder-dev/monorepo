## About BibliU

BibliU is a digital learning platform that is changing the future of the multi-billion-dollar textbook industry. Our mission is to shake up higher education and make reading materials more accessible and learning more effective. We do this by making textbooks and monographs more affordable for students, so that in most cases, they are included in tuition costs. We have incorporated accessibility features to accommodate multiple learning styles and the different needs and preferences of each student.

BibliU has grown rapidly, and during this time we have created Europe's largest digital education content programme, in partnership with Coventry University. Upon entering the US market, we have secured the largest digital textbook programmes in the US with Phoenix and Grand Canyon Universities.

We are working with over 60% of universities in the United Kingdom, and are steadily growing in the US . We have recently raised $10 million in a Series A investment round (you can read more about this on [Sky News](https://news.sky.com/story/innovation-agency-nesta-backs-uk-edtech-start-up-bibliu-11974089) and [EdSurge](https://www.edsurge.com/news/2020-04-17-bibliu-raises-10-million-to-scale-online-textbooks)) and have ambitious plans for continued growth and expansion.

We are seeking a data-driven, revenue-centric Growth Marketing Manager with experience in inbound and outbound marketing, including account-based marketing. Working from home with a diverse team across North America and Europe, you will play a key role in growing our North American markets, while also collaborating on global marketing initiatives, including overseeing the effectiveness of the BibliU website.

## Position Overview

Reporting to the Growth Marketing Associate, you will create and orchestrate demand generation campaigns that grow BibliU’s US sales pipeline. You will work collaboratively and cross-functionally, aligning closely with marketing and sales team members to market our learning enablement platform to higher education institutions.

## Areas of Responsibility:

*   Collaborate on the execution of the growth marketing plan, optimizing the use of email marketing
*   Conceive, develop, and execute on multi-touch marketing campaigns to drive qualified leads, in alignment with company goals
*   Collaborate closely with marketing counterparts, such as content marketing, product marketing, and sales development, to increase marketing success rates
*   Develop and implement email nurture strategies, using a mix of helpful content and other calls-to-action
*   Regularly report program/campaign performance
*   Manage technical aspects of key marketing systems (marketing automation, Salesforce, Outreach, etc) used to generate, distribute, and report on leads
