## Job description

**Location**:Work from home (some European travel possible)

**Duration**: 3 Months

**Start Date**: November 2020

Our client is a mid-size European outdoor consumer products group that have multiple brands through acquisition. We have been engaged to support development of their “direct to user” capabilities and wider digital transformation. Within this we are also identifying synergy opportunities (better, more efficient ways of working) across the brands primarily in the areas of Marketing and Online. This work is underway and the next phase will be to develop the target operating model for these areas of the business covering key processes, organisation design, function and role level responsibilities.

## Responsibilities Include:

Supporting the operating model project lead including:

*   Client workshops to understand “as-is” processes
*   Development of high-level structural options
*   Organisation design down to role level
*   Role level FTE calculations and investments case
