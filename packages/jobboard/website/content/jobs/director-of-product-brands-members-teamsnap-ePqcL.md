**About us**

TeamSnap is a sports and communication platform dedicated to empowering play in youth sports. We value trust, communication, and fun more than big company policies. We empower our people to bring big ideas and tiny egos which lands us on Outside Magazine’s list of **“Best Places to Work”** on the regular.

TeamSnap is looking for an experienced product manager with a passion for building awesome product experiences to accelerate the growth of our advertising and consumer businesses. You will live and breathe the TeamSnap consumer product by owning the ideation and implementation of highly engaging product and advertising experiences for over 23 million users.  You will partner closely with engineering, ad sales, marketing, finance, customer success, and analytics to ensure business success.  If you are someone who leads by deeply understanding customers’ behaviors and needs, enables and encourages the creativity of others, and is passionate about driving high-impact business results with unparalleled product experiences, then you’re exactly who we’re looking for!

## What you'll be doing

*   Partner with the VP of Product, VP of Brands, and VP of Members to develop a product strategy and roadmap to accelerate TeamSnap’s business growth, with a specific focus on advertising revenue, user engagement, acquisition, and retention.
*   Oversee and manage the business performance, investments, and priorities of our B2C mobile and web products.
*   Deeply understand digital media products, customers, the competitive landscape, market opportunities, and product differentiators to establish a market advantage.
*   Develop, manage, and analyze high-impact product features and own the daily execution of strategic and tactical initiatives.
*   Promote and enhance a highly-effective, efficient, and innovative agile product culture that executes initiatives on-time, on-budget, with high quality.
*   Direct management of a Senior Product Manager with the ownership of building-out a team of product managers.
*   Partner with other product managers, advertising operations, and growth marketing to create a cohesive product vision and customer experience across all product offerings.
*   Work collaboratively with design, product development, QA, marketing, and sales to ensure the end-to-end delivery of product features.
*   Be the voice and brand of the TeamSnap product. Proactively engage with internal and external stakeholders to bring to life the product vision, roadmap, and alignment to our company goals.

## Things that are important

*   BA/BS degree required; MBA preferred.
*   Hands-on experience with advertising revenue-generating consumer products and being responsible for business results to inform key strategic and investment decisions.
*   Strong understanding of consumer marketing, attribution models, funnel optimization, A/B campaign testing.
*   Passion for great product experiences - constantly seeking to learn best practices in web and mobile products and apply those to our own products.
*   Energized by collaborating with other functional leaders and effectively manages through influence.
*   Experience using and implementing human-centered design and lean experimentation practices.
*   Ability to do quantitative market research and use data to back your plans and recommendations.
*   Something vaguely weird in your Spotify playlist—maybe a Chipmunks cover band.
*   Excellent communication and presentation skills.
*   Comfort with the pace and ambiguity that comes with working in a fast-paced environment across multiple time zones and remote teams.

## Bonus Points

*   Fluency with Jira and Confluence.
*   Working knowledge of Google Analytics, Big Query and SQL.
*   Ability to juggle and chew gum at the same time - we have a high bar for talent.
*   Passionate about youth sports as a parent, player, or fan.
*   A TeamSnap user.

**Location**

We are headquartered in Boulder, Colorado, but this job is remote (unless you happen to live near Boulder, in which case you’re welcome to come to the office). TeamSnap is a mostly-distributed company, so you must be very comfortable working with people who aren’t in the same physical location as you or each other. While we love all parts of the world, we can only hire permanent US residents at this time.
