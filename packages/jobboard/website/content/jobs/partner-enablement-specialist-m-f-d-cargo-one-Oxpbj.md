**Join us in changing an entire industry**

We need you to help us shape the future of air freight! At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) we believe that we can only win as a team, and we want you to make an impact with us!

**🏆** **What’s the opportunity?**

This role offers the opportunity to drive the supply side (the airlines) of our booking platform. As a Partner Enablement Specialist (PES), you will have the chance to support our airline partners such as Lufthansa Cargo, Etihad Cargo and many more in a certain geographic region operationally. As such, you will help to build operational relationships in a region, educate stakeholders how best to work with [cargo.one](http://cargo.one) and help resolve operational challenges. This role is crucial, as it forms the critical connection between our demand- (the freight forwarder) and supply side (the airline) to ensure both stakeholders have a great experience on the booking platform. Having recently raised our series A, we are now onboarding more and more airline partners, while also rolling out new products - this role offers the chance to provide on the ground support to our partners in their digital transformation process. 

Join us now and transform the way airlines sell cargo!

**⚠️** **This position is remote**. The caveat is that you must be based within one of the listed countries [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1sG5ppO5OKJseIOjzHIm0jUbdpvTBmeQ8/view?usp=sharing) for us to be able to employ you remotely.

## 🕵🏼‍♀️ What you’ll be doing?

*   Act as a key touch point with our operational airline stakeholders (airline sales teams and general sales agents) and build lasting relationships
*   Investigate and resolve operational issues (via a ticketing system) that require communication and investigation on our demand and supply side
*   Investigate operational issues and find creative solutions to improve the service level of our airline partners and the experience using [cargo.one](http://cargo.one)
*   Conduct onboarding and ongoing training sessions with the general sales agents and local airline teams in your region to highlight the advantages they gain by using [cargo.one](http://cargo.one)
*   Act as a conduit for feedback from our airline stakeholders product and other internal stakeholders 
*   Monitor the operational performance of our partner airlines in your region and proactively escalate operational challenges
*   Constantly strive to find improvement areas for our partner airlines to successfully sell digitally on [cargo.one](http://cargo.one)

## 🙋🏽‍♀️ What you’ll need

*   2+ years of experience in a customer facing role such as customer support / customer success (Experience working in a SaaS or transaction based environment is a plus, but not a must)
*   You have a passion for making customers successful and have a track record of onboarding and training customers
*   You have demonstrated the ability to build trust with both internal and external stakeholders
*   You are characterized by excellent communication and presentation skills and enjoy interacting with various stakeholders
*   You possess an entrepreneurial drive combined with ability to be creative, take initiative and take risks
*   You thrive in fast-paced and challenging environments
*   You are native in German and fluent in English
