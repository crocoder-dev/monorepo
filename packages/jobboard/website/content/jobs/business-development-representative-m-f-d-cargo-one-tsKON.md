**Join us in changing an entire industry**

We need you to help us shape the future of air freight! At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) we believe that we can only win as a team, and we want you to make an impact with us!

🏆 What’s the opportunity?

Winning new airlines is an exciting challenge and critical to [cargo.one](http://cargo.one)’s success in [changing our industry](https://techcrunch.com/2020/07/27/cargo-one-gets-18-6m-to-take-its-air-freight-booking-platform-over-the-pond/?guccounter=1&guce_referrer=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbS8&guce_referrer_sig=AQAAAJ6QY2-UxIKIfnH3j9OcgovHU4U4Beufc7-vYsMymJwM4WqV_ozug43AffVt_ter8P4rfbnz1Tn-JXMmhGl6zMtTQK3NNMnHWfYJy9rw92YxjJ-M_hO3e_kcaN5_oiJtG2jwyn-6icArZUwU_JOMCw5NZnWJbV44jnpPCFXqfwH-). In the role of Business Development Representative, you have a direct influence on intensifying our presence at airlines and in accelerating our growth. Working with airlines to understand their inner workings and needs, you will spend your time trailblazing [cargo.one](http://cargo.one)’s presence with your target accounts. You will be a core part of our sales team and with you we will be onboarding new airlines faster than ever.

⚠️ This position is remote. The caveat is that you must be based within one of the listed countries [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1sG5ppO5OKJseIOjzHIm0jUbdpvTBmeQ8/view?usp=sharing) for us to be able to employ you remotely.

## 🕵🏼‍♀️ What you’ll be doing?

*   Contact airlines through outbound prospecting/cold calls, emails and other touchpoints.
*   Use different touchpoints and channels to identify needs and pain points of the airline and qualify their interest
*   Lead demos and virtual meetings with relevant stakeholders
*   Deliver qualified sales opportunities to a Business Development Manager
*   Track and maintain your work in HubSpot (we can train you on this!)
*   Work with our Marketing team to steer account based marketing to your chosen airlines
*   Actively manage and track your targets to achieve your weekly and monthly KPIs 

## 🙋🏽‍♀️ What you’ll need

*   You are English native or Mandarine native or Arabic native speaker
*   You have a background in support, sales, or marketing communications and want to be coached to become a sales superstar
*   Strong communication and presentation skills to interact effectively with airlines
*   You are proactive, outgoing and are not intimidated by the prospect of cold calling - these will be the bulk of your day-to-day priorities
*   You are a metric-driven individual, you want to grow your career in sales and are motivated by success individually and as part of a team

## What you can expect from cargo.one? We think you’ll enjoy working at cargo.one. Here are just a few of the things that we think make it a great place to work:

*   The chance to contribute from day 1 with plenty of room for career progression.
*   The opportunity to have a serious impact on a growing organization that’s changing an industry.
*   An extremely steep learning curve working alongside an ambitious and international team
*   MacBook or ThinkPad, the choice is yours – whatever helps you get your job done, we’ve got you covered.
*   Work within a fully-remote setup
