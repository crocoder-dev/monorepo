## About Hometime:

Hometime is the largest and fastest growing platform for short term rental operators in the APAC region. 

We partner with local hosting businesses and empower them with industry leading technology, supporting infrastructure and scale normally reserved for large companies. 

After successfully scaling our business locally we’ve released our software as a SaaS product for the global market of short term rental operators. Our ambition is to be the software partner of choice for small operators and to help them grow successful businesses through our amazing product and industry expertise. 

We are empowering the little guys, the micro-entrepreneurs who deliver amazing experiences to their guests and property owners because they are hyper local. We believe small and local coupled with the best technology & industry expertise is the recipe for success.

## About the role:

With the recent launch of our consumer-facing SaaS application, we need to build a support capability to ensure paying users are getting value from the product.

Our ideal candidate is a great communicator with technical support experience across web-based applications. Someone who has worked as a front-line support team member and been promoted into a supervisory/managerial role overseeing a team of support agents and being responsible for process improvement & training. As this role is the first technical support team member to join Hometime the successful candidate has an opportunity to help establish support processes and workflows before eventually growing the team.

Surprising and delighting our users with outstanding support is the goal which will be measured through user retention, churn, and NPS. Happy users will result in an engaged community and organic word of mouth growth which is directly impacted through this role.

In addition, the Technical Support Specialist will systematically record feedback and help prioritize feature development based on the impact on the end-user. This dialogue between support, product, and engineering is crucial to the long-term success of the business and provides the applicant with exposure to strategic business decisions.

## Key Responsibilities:

*   Front line technical support via email & chat to app users. Always striving for inbox zero.
*   Establish technical support processes & workflows
*   Systematically record user feedback
*   Work with the product team to prioritise feedback and feature development
*   Assist with the creation of supporting materials to help users self serve
*   Help build the team and train new staff when the user base has grown
*   Most importantly - surprise & delight our users with outstanding support  
    

## Qualifications:

*   3+ years of relevant technical support experience 
*   Great communication skills (English) 
*   Genuine curiosity and an ability to ask effective questions and understand customer needs;
*   Experience in high growth, fast-paced company such as a startup is a plus
*   Experience at a modern SaaS business is an advantage
