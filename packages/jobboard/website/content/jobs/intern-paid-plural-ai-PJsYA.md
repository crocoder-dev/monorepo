## Job description

###### ABOUT YOU

You're currently studying or recently graduated from one of the top schools, and want to get involved with an exciting start-up. You want to work with cutting-edge tech and understand what AI is all about.

You’re inquisitive, self-motivated, and excited by the prospect of working with ground-breaking technology. You love a challenge and teaching yourself new skills on the fly.

You enjoy thinking strategically and future-gazing, but you’re also practical and not above getting stuck in to help the team.

###### ABOUT THE ROLE

We're a small team, so this is a very hands-on role with a broad remit, and a great opportunity to get operational experience at an AI start-up. You will get closely involved in all commercial and operational aspects of the business, working directly with the CEO. You will also have direct involvement with the product and engineering team, working with them to improve the product and design new features.

###### Main tasks

*   Data analysis (research and extract data to populate our database, review accuracy of data model and mappings, etc.)
*   Customer success (new customer onboarding, client delivery and support, etc.)
*   Business development / sales (research prospects, maintain CRM, help prepare client materials and presentations, etc.)
*   Market research and intelligence (research competitors and landscape, review product offering and positioning)
*   Content marketing / social media (research+write blog posts, maintain social media profiles e.g. Twitter, etc.)
*   Miscellaneous tasks (help on fundraising, operations, special projects, etc.)

###### ABOUT US

Our founders met through deep-tech incubator Entrepreneur First, and have a mix of in-depth industry experience and deep technical expertise.

We’re tackling some ambitious, complex, deep-tech problems at the core of modern work. We aim to redefine human-computer interaction and set the standard in intelligence augmentation.

You’ll get to work with some of the most innovative technologies out there, including knowledge graphs, natural language processing, and machine learning.

‘Ask more questions’ is our tagline. It is at the heart of our product (we enable our users to ask more questions, and also think of the right questions to ask) and at the heart of our company too. It is at the root of many other well-known principles that we support - from first-principle thinking to ‘strong opinions, weakly held’.

Our other, related founding axiom is ‘look for the desire paths’. We look for the shortcuts, the ways to do things better. Unconstrained by existing preconceptions, we have a healthy disregard for rules, and we don’t hesitate to question and circumvent them if it leads to better outcomes.

## Job requirements

The most important thing to us is raw motivation and ability to get stuff done to a high standard. That being said, successful candidates may possess a combination of the following skills.

###### Required skills

*   Smart and hungry to learn - this is growth mindset at its best!
*   Interest in finance, fintech, AI, and our mission
*   Very familiar with Excel/Google Sheets and typical spreadsheet-based tasks
*   Experience / knowledge of corporate finance (e.g. PE / M&A / VC) or other related disciplines (via degree and/or prior work experience)

###### Bonus points for

*   Start-up / scale-up experience or similar fast-paced environment
*   Growth / marketing / sales expertise (digital marketing, direct outreach, etc. - especially in a B2B SaaS context)
*   Data science / analytics / programming skills (particularly with regards to marketing automation)
*   User research / product development skills
*   Strong academics

###### LOGISTICS

*   We're fully-remote but the team is mostly UK-based - please only apply if you live within a 2h time difference to the UK
*   Start date: ASAP
*   Duration: 8-12 weeks (flexible)
*   Part-time or full-time
