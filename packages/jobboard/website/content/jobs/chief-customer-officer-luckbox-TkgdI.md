## Job description

We have a genuinely once in a life time opportunity to join Luckbox as Chief Customer Officer; this role will be the second executive level report into the Group CEO, Quentin Martin, and will take responsibility for the operational and P&L leadership of the B2C business. The opportunity for growth in this role is unparalleled, as you will be joining us at a crucial point in our journey, as we list on the TSX and plan (with you in a key role) to grow revenue by 2500% over the next 24 months, while building a world class team to deliver this vision.

Luckbox is an esports betting platform built around the ethos of ‘Esports Betting Done Right’. The international group will be listed on the Toronto Venture Stock Exchange in December and, we immediately require a qualified candidate to fill the role of Chief Customer Officer to be a critical member of our executive leadership team with strong career growth possibilities for the right candidate. The group has recently closed CAD $6 million in financing and is expected to grow revenue by 2500% in the next 24 months.

What else is in it for you? You will be working within a globally dynamic and fast-paced environment where esports betting is done right. We are united by the mission to improve the gaming and esports scene by introducing a fully regulated and safe betting product. The group has recently closed CAD $6 million in financing and is expected to grow revenue by 2500% in the next 24 months.

What makes us different from other esports betting start-ups is our approach - we are building a product specifically for the competitive gaming audience in a fully regulated manner. Luckbox are breaking new grounds; with the future of gambling being very much in our capable hands and the world is starting to take note; we were very proud to be the recipient of the EGR 2020 Rising Star award.

## Role & Responsibilities:

*   Responsible for the Luckbox B2C P&L
*   Responsible for product, marketing, operations, sportsbook, payments, and technology
*   Responsible for all day-to-day operations
*   Working closely with the CEO to set (and smash) KPI targets and OKRs
*   To banish any _“it is what it is”_ culture and instead instil with a _“move fast and break things”_ attitude
*   Review existing processes, tools, and talent; to present and (execute upon) a gap-analysis
*   Help model and establish the group’s B2B strategy
*   Work closely with the CEO to determine market and licence strategy
*   To act as a mentor to your reports and their teams and ensure that we hire only superstars

## Job requirements

## Essential:

*   Demonstrable experience of owning and successfully delivering on a P&L
*   An MBA or similar commercially focused qualification
*   Be an ambitious, intelligent, autonomous team player
*   Experience working within a high-growth, technologically focused SME - comfortable being incredibly hands on initially and scaling the group from ~50 to ~150 employees across multiple companies in multiple jurisdictions
*   Experience in leadership role in either product or marketing
*   Driven by analytics; data syndication, business intelligence, analytical modelling and business foresight.

## Desired:

*   A passion for esports and computer games
*   Seasoned experience working in the highly regulated gambling industry

## Location:

This full-time position can be based on an EU time zone, ideally in the Isle of Man or Bulgaria, although a fully remote position may be considered. COVID-depending, this role may require extensive travelling.

Inclusion comes naturally to us, we are obsessive about finding the best talent; regardless of race, gender, age, religion, identity, and experience. We are committed to creating a working environment where our people can be themselves and flourish.
