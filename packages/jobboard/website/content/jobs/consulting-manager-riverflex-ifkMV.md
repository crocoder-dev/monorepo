## Job description

## A little bit about us

Based in the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, and Spain, we are a fast-growing start-up in the digital consulting industry. We help businesses solve tough digital challenges through the power of open talent ecosystems. This means that we work predominantly with independent industry experts and ex-consultants from tier 1 brands, enabling us to form top specialist teams with a combination of backgrounds and skillsets that really fit the needs of our clients. To date, we have built a community of [~1000 top-quality digital professionals](https://riverflex.com/people/our-people/) [](https://riverflex.com/people/our-people/) and 15+ serve world-class businesses.

## What we are looking for

As we are working with highly flexible teams, we need a strong consulting project manager that will ensure that everything comes together and with the right quality. We typically do high-stakes strategic assignments – and you will be the focal point. This means that you are structured in how you work, have good communication skills, and really enjoy building teams as you will work with diverse sets of people in a fast-paced environment.

Being part of our start-up also means that you will be building the company with us. We are looking for someone who brings ideas on how we can improve the way we work and do business, and is excited to head up key internal projects or new capabilities that we want to build.

Ideally, you have about 5-8 years of consulting experience and are looking for fast growth and responsibility.

**What we offer**

We offer you an exceptional experience to work for a fast-growing start-up, with a fun, dynamic, and stable international environment. Our management team is ambitious and innovative while bringing a professional approach from their previous experience as partners at big 4 consulting companies. We offer a competitive salary and practical benefits such as flexible and virtual working. When with the team, you will be working from a start-up hub at the heart of London or Amsterdam.

**What you will do as Consulting Manager**

*   Be responsible for the successful delivery of projects ensuring effective resource planning and team composition
*   Work with our portfolio of clients to manage and coordinate projects and develop new business opportunities
*   Gather and analyse information to identify clients’ business problems, draw conclusions, and formulate actionable recommendations
*   Generate strategies, roadmaps, and business cases, and deliver data-driven solutions
*   Present results and implement recommendations in collaboration with client team members
*   Coach the junior consultant team to enable their growth and development while delivering excellent outcomes.

## Job requirements

**What you need to succeed**

*   At least 5 years of experience at a Tier 1 consulting firm
*   At least 2 years as a Senior Consultant/Associate or another relevant role
*   A Degree in Business, Computer Sciences, or a technology-related field
*   Experience in delivering technology-based solutions to clients
*   An analytical mindset oriented on tackling complex business problems and generating value for our clients
*   Experience developing and managing relationships with clients and key stakeholders
*   A comprehensive understanding of the industry trends and the digital space
*   You know how to motivate yourself and have a proactive mindset
*   You know how to get things done; you are able to handle multiple projects at the same time and meet deadlines as agreed
*   Experience in working with international and virtual teams
*   Fluency in English and Dutch
