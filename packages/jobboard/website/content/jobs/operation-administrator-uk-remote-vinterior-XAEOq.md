## Description

## Who are we?

Our reason for being is simple - to champion the importance and relevance of vintage in a world that is dominated by mass production.

We created Vinterior to bring the best independent vintage furniture sellers together in an online marketplace and connect them with our community of vintage fans and design professionals. Today, our platform is the leading vintage marketplace in the UK - with over 1,800 vintage sellers that power 200,000+ products on our platform.

## The Customer Experience team

The CX team is home to providing excellent end-to-end services to all Vinterior clients: Trade buyers, Consumers and Sellers. As our Operations Associate, you'll be tasked with ensuring that all the payment flows are running smoothly and supporting clients with all their payment requests. In recent months, we've assembled a team of ambitious and passionate team members from some of the leading dot.coms (Depop, Made and Google to name a few). We're now looking for an Operation Associate to join us. Our ambition? To position Vinterior as the most loved and progressive brand for vintage furniture out there.

## What we’re looking for:

You love numbers, thrive on Excel and want to work in a startup because change and challenges drive you? You might be our next Operations Administrator! You will be part of our Customer Experience team and will also have the opportunity to work closely with our head of CX and our COO. This is a great chance for someone who is looking to develop their career within an exciting environment.

**What you will do:**

*   Finance: Book-keeping, end of month reporting, cash-flow reconciliation.
*   B2B support: Creating proformas, checking payments flows, requesting invoices, payment support.
*   Reporting: Assisting the Head of CX and the COO with reporting.
*   Clients relation: Be the point of contact for our buyers and sellers with regards to their payments, answer questions and support them through their selling/purchasing journey.
*   And get involved with other projects that excite you!

## Requirements

*   Bachelor's Degree or equivalent with 1-3 years of administrative assistance experience
*   Excellent command of Excel/Google Sheet is required
*   Organised and detail-oriented

*   Positive and approachable personality
