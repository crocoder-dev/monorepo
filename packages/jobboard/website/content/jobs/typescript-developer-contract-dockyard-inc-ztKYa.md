## Job description

DockYard is looking for a **TypeScript Developer (Contract)**.

## Who We Are

DockYard is a digital product consultancy specializing in production-ready apps. DockYard offers a range of consulting services with capabilities in product planning, design, user experience (UX), full-stack engineering, and QA. Over the last decade, the company has solved complex product challenges for visionary brands like Netflix, Apple, Nasdaq, and Harvard.

We’re honored to have been included on guides ranging from _Inc 5000_ (2020, 2019) to _Fast Company’s Best Places to Work for Innovators_ (2020) amongst others.

## The Role

The ideal candidate is self-driven and passionate about web technologies and industry best practices, and has a strong understanding of how to interface with clients. Candidates should feel comfortable and empowered to thrive in environments with open direction and loosely defined requirements.

## Responsibilities

*   Work independently or with other engineers to build and maintain growing libraries, APIs, and dependencies.
*   Collaborate with client stakeholders and other engineering teams to understand business goals, analyze requirements, and partner on solutions.
*   Investigate and help define feature requests from various client team members.
*   Participate in project planning, standups, code review, improvement suggestions, and other team activities.

## Job requirements

## Must Haves

*   Strong TypeScript and JavaScript experience
*   Understands best practices for web development and software design, and relishes opportunities to transform complex, legacy codebases into robust, scalable, and maintainable systems
*   Excellent communication and collaboration skills
*   Willing to learn and adopt new technologies guided by client requirements

## Nice To Haves

*   Lerna experience
*   Prior client services, consulting, or freelancing experience
*   Experience working in a remote team
