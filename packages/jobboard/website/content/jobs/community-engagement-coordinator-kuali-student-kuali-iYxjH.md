## Description

Kuali Student includes the solution for curriculum and academic catalog management for colleges and universities. As a member of the team, you’ll be helping us build our prospective customer community. Through account-based outreach activities, you’ll coordinate early-stage pipeline engagement by nurturing prospective customer interest, qualifying leads, previewing the Kuali Student solutions with prospect, and transitioning the opportunity to our sales team.

Please Note: the Community Engagement Coordinator (CEC) role is similar to a Sales Development Representation (SDR) role.

**About the company:**

Kuali builds software solutions for higher education. We help our customers — colleges & universities — focus on providing a fantastic education to students by decreasing their administrative costs. We work in a competitive space, ripe for innovation, with users ready to be delighted.

**Everyone should love their work.**

Kuali has been voted a [top place to work](https://topworkplaces.com/publication/saltlake/kuali/) for 3 years in a row by the Salt Lake Tribune. We also made [Forbes' list of America's Best Startup Employers](https://www.forbes.com/americas-best-startup-employers/#1100e1e65275) for 2020.

**WHAT YOU'LL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR**

*   Qualifying prospects based on their current catalog and curriculum management needs through educational, supportive engagement
*   Learning the higher education curriculum and catalog management market, trends, thought leaders, related events and conferences, covered media and professional associations
*   Creating prospect engagement content using marketing assets and communication delivery tools
*   Scheduling discovery calls and responding to Kuali Student demo requests
*   Using a sales development platform to execute the outreach process consisting of phone, email, and social media
*   Meeting monthly objectives for sales qualified leads, discovery calls, and demos
*   Continuously improving your knowledge around higher education, Kuali Student’s buyers, sales development process, and demonstrating a willingness to learn and implement best practices
*   Representing and demonstrating Kuali [values](https://www.kuali.co/about), brand, and mission

About You

*   Excellent skills and competencies in the following: written & verbal communication, listening, problem solving, and customer service skills
*   Ability to seek prospects and ask key questions followed by valuable probing questions to understand if there’s a need and if Kuali is a potential solution
*   Motivated self-starter who is focused on meeting goals and invested in learning how to improve how to strategically reach them.
*   Strong individual and team contributor who enjoys a fast paced environment and is eager to adapt and adjust to seek success in performance

Spent time (after reading this) to review Kuali’s [company values](https://www.kuali.co/about) and are on board with them

## Requirements

*   This position is fully remote and requires a space suitable for an effective working environment.

**If you have some of these too we’ll be delighted**

*   Familiarity with the general organizational structure of Higher Ed institutions
*   Knowledge of curriculum and catalog management at an institution(s) and/or in the market space
*   Prior lead generation and/or sales prospecting experience in Ed-Tech
*   Quick learner and general working knowledge of tools like Google Suite, Salesforce, Zoom, ZoomInfo; or similar platforms.
