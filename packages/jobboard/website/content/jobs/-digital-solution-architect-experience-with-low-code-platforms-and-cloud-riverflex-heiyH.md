## Job description

**Location**: Remote - Client based inAmsterdam

**Start Date**: 1 February2021 (for 3-6 months)

Our client in the medicalcare industry. Their target is todigitize their business processes which is based upon core legacy applications (ERP) and highly offline/paper-based. We are looking for a solution architect that can develop a modern low-code, cloud-based, target architecture and the roadmap to get there.This role reports to the Applications Manager but will work closelywith technology and businessleads to identify the HL business and tech requirements for the future.

## Key Responsibilities

*   Understand thefuture businessrequirements of the organisation and translate this into a target architecture for the organisation
*   Ensure a modern future target architecture (low-code - potentially utilising Mendix or Microsoft Power Platform, and cloud-based (Azure))
*   Identify integration and datarequirements across systems to support the future business process
*   Develop a roadmap for the rollout of the digital strategy
*   Develop architecture standards and principles
*   Ensure regulatory compliance of the future architecture (healthcare / medical experience of such processes would be a bonus (e.g. hospitals))
*   Act as thetechnical design authority for the implementation.
*   Drive and own the technical solutions through project life cycle phases

## Job requirements

**Essential qualifications/skills/competencies:**

*   10+ years experience in digital system architecture design
*   Fluent in English or Dutch
*   Experience of leading the solution design and implementation of low-code platforms
*   Experience architectures based upon Azure cloud infrastructure
*   Attention to detail and good analytical skills
*   Ability to problem solve complex IT issues

**Preferred****qualifications/skills/competencies:**

*   Experience in regulated environment is preferred(preferably pharmaceuticals/medical)
*   Experience withMendix / Microsoft Power Platforms
