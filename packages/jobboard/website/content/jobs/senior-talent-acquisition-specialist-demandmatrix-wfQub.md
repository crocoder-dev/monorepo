## Job Description

## What are we looking for?

We are looking for a Senior Talent Acquisition Specialist. This position is responsible for identifying top tier talent across India supporting our delivery, RnD, GTM, and product development teams based out remotely all over India.

## Role and responsibilities:

*   Drive full-cycle recruitment activities for both technical and non-technical positions, including scoping, sourcing, screening, assessing/interviewing, and negotiating offers.
    
*   Implement creative recruiting strategies to create a qualified and diverse candidate pool.
    
*   Engage with the external talent pool network to attract talent from passive sources, LinkedIn Network, platforms like GitHub, stack overflow, hackathons, etc. Act as a brand ambassador for DemandMatrix in these networks to engage with the talent pool
    
*   Champion a positive candidate experience, ensuring each candidate feels positive in their interaction in applying for one of our roles
    
*   Identify opportunities to increase productivity and efficiency by adopting standards using tools and processes that accelerate the speed and effectiveness of recruiting.
    
*   Use data to drive outcomes - gather and share market information and trends with the Talent Acquisition team and business stakeholders, allowing your group to make more informed decisions
    
*   Coach and guide Hiring Managers to draft competencies and make well-considered diligent hiring decisions, 
    

## Mandatory skills:

*   Expertise in technical hiring for fast pace product-based start-up companies.
    
*   Expertise in the end to end recruitment cycle.
    

*   Experience using and adapting multiple sourcing strategies to meet the needs of complex recruitment activities
    
*   Experience with various sourcing methods and tools.
    
*   Coordinate seamlessly with managers, interviewers, and team members throughout the hiring process.
    
*   Take ownership, follow-through, be self-sufficient and deliver quality within timelines.
    
*   Communication, communication, communication - you strive to be concise so your audience is on the same page as you
