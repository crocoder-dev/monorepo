Aircall is on a mission to revolutionize the business phone industry!

We exist to empower every professional to have richer conversations. We provide an entirely cloud-based voice solution, that integrates seamlessly with popular productivity and helpdesk tools that workplaces are already using. We have raised more than $100 Million since 2015, and we care about our growing base of 6000+ customers.

Behind our product are the amazing teams driving it, split between Paris, New York, Sydney, Madrid, and remote locations.

**About the role:**

We’re looking for an experienced product marketer to join our global team of collaborative, creative, and talented PMMs. You will report to the NY-based Director of Product Marketing, and you will be a key member of a team that sits at the intersection of Marketing, Product, Sales, and Customer Success. 

At Aircall, product marketers are strategic partners with product managers, from discovery through launch and beyond. You will be the product marketing lead for our core products, working closely with PMs to identify business opportunities, understand customer needs, and launch new products and features. We’ll also count on you to bring your expertise to help us take our global launch processes to the next level. 

This is an exciting time for Aircall and the Product Marketing team is right in the center of it all. 

## What you'll do:

*   Become an expert in our product, our market, and especially our customers and buyers, using industry reports and analysts, surveys, interviews, customer feedback, product usage data, etc.
*   Act as the voice of the customer, understanding their goals and the problems they are trying to solve
*   Influence roadmap decisions using buyer, customer and market knowledge
*   Own go-to-market strategy, including pricing and packaging, and launch execution for a set of product topics
*   Develop product messaging that resonates with buyers and highlights Aircall’s unique value proposition
*   Set product launch goals and partner with marketing colleagues (field / channel / content & customer marketing) as subject matter expert for campaigns to meet those goals
*   Stay flexible, positive, solution-oriented and creative

## What you'll bring:

*   Ability to hit the ground running, drawing upon 5+ years relevant experience for a B2B technology product, including product GTM experience - bonus points for experience with communication technology
*   Strong experience with pricing and packaging is a major plus 
*   Flexibility to adapt and take on new challenges in a dynamic environment
*   A proven ability to lead a cross-functional team to achieve business goals
*   Proactive communication and a drive to take initiative
*   A balance of left- and right-brain thinking - you are both creative and analytical (that’s why you love product marketing, right?)
*   Storytelling chops -  you have a way with words and you understand how to tailor a message to meet an audience’s needs
*   A global mindset - you enjoy working with a diverse group of international colleagues 
*   Yourself! What unique skills and perspective will you add to the team?
