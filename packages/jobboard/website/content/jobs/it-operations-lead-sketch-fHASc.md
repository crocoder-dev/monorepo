Do you want to help us build and scale the original disruptor in digital design? Sketch is where 1000s of design teams, in 100+ countries across the globe, create their best products, every day. We're looking for an **IT Operations Lead** to join our growing team.

In this position:

\- We need a leader that will administer and govern our evolving set of internal applications, manage our hardware and device footprint, and provide support to our globally distributed workforce.

\- You will help us run better and smarter - by building automated, low-friction, reliable process that allows our workforce to be maximally productive while ensuring good governance.

\- You will work closely with Infrastructure Engineering and Information Security to ensure our internal architecture scales and performs to the requirements of our fast-growing business.

## Responsibilities:

*   Collaborate effectively with business and delivery partners to define requirements and success criteria
*   Administer within and across key applications while controlling access, checking privileges, managing configurations, and owning our VPN
*   Streamline the process and own the management of account entitlements (onboarding, offboarding, role changes)
*   Manage mobile device and asset footprint
*   Help us support and meet our governance, risk and compliance objectives, including by operationalizing (for example) ISO27001 requirements

## Requirements:

*   Experience managing IT
*   Good knowledge of internet security and data privacy principles
*   Experience with device management setup
*   Experience with Apple devices in corp environment (Mac+iOS)
*   Experience with Google Workspace as SSO-IDP provider (and AWS IAM)
*   Previous startup experience
*   Proven team-player and cross-functional collaborator
*   Degree in Computer Science, engineering or relevant field
*   Open for challenges

## Nice to have:

*   ISO 27001 ISMS experience
*   Experience in remote working and managing remote IT
*   Based in UTC or CET time zone

**Even if you're not able to tick all of these boxes**, we would still love to hear from you.
