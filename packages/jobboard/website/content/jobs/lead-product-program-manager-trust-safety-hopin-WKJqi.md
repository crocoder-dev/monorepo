Whether it’s a 50-person meetup, or a 50,000-person conference—any type of event organizer can host a Hopin event for their attendees, speakers, sponsors, and vendors to convene, learn, and interact face-to-face online. The Role

Hopin’s products and services offer our community of users the ability to connect, share, contribute and participate in vibrant, positive experiences. The Trust & Safety team at Hopin works to ensure that our fast-growing community is protected from bad experiences by developing innovative processes, tools, product enhancements and review mechanisms that function at scale.

You will hold primary responsibility for collaborating with our Product and Engineering teams to ensure that Trust & Safety considerations are built into our strategies and roadmaps across our portfolio of products (Hopin, StreamYard, Streamable, Jamm). This is an exciting opportunity to influence the direction of our products at an early stage and to combine your technical prowess with your passion for protecting online communities. 

Responsibilities

*   Define and own the Trust & Safety product integration program, acting as a Trust & Safety expert advisor to passionate, cross-functional teams of engineers, user researchers and designers
*   Review roadmaps, assist with scoping product requirements, define use cases, and ensure a consistent and principled approach to Trust & Safety across our products
*   Work with cross-functional partners to drive proactive, scalable moderation solutions, and drive development efforts to create internal tools and evaluate third-party offerings;
*   Define Trust & Safety measurement frameworks to track and report product successes and risks 
*   Build user awareness and transparency in partnership with legal, marketing, communications and other cross-functional partners.
*   Proactively identify considerations for internationalization of our products as we grow into new markets, defining and mitigating local and global risks 

About You

*   4+ years of experience in Trust & Safety as a product program manager or similar role
*   Strong technical skills, with experience collaborating cross-functionally across engineering, product, design and other key stakeholders.
*   Ability to organize and communicate vast amounts of complexity
*   Obsessed with user and community safety, and focused on proactive issue identification consistent with these principles
*   Strong analytical skills and mindset. Experience with Excel, BI tooling and SQL are a must. 

Communication

*   Fluent in English
*   Confident communicating complex product challenges.
*   Excellent communication skills and an ability to work closely with creative, technical, and business stakeholders.
