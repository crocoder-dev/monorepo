## Company Overview

Imagine being part of a nimble team where your ideas and input have the potential to impact millions of lives. IRL aims to make an impact on collective loneliness by bringing people together in real/remote life. Our goal is to help our users engage more with friends in-person to boost their overall happiness, encourage curiosity and make the task of organizing hang outs a breeze.

Our team is focused on building an event and group discovery network. We're looking for self-motivated engineers who have a passion for building high-quality consumer-facing applications.

## You Will:

*   Be responsible for delivering new features and bug fixes as part of a small, driven team.
*   Participate in code reviews to help everyone move together and contribute quality code.
*   Manage your work by setting your own development goals, executing them, and owning delivery of projects.
*   Collaborate with the entire company via Slack, Github, and Zoom.

## The Ideal Candidate Should:

*   Have some Objective C experience and at least 3 years of Swift experience.
*   Be located somewhere that's 3 hours +/- of Pacific Time.
*   Have in-depth experience with object-oriented programming and data modeling.
*   Have a real passion for quality and attention to detail.
*   Be familiar with Cocoapods, Git, Fastlane, Travis-CI, GitHub, and LLDB.Please note that you can be a great fit even if not everything above applies to you. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to ask.

## We Offer:

*   A remote-first organization that allows you to set up working hours that best fit your daily routine.
*   Room for personal and professional development through regular 1-on-1s and mentoring.
*   A motivated team that enables you to do your best.
*   Full coverage health insurance, dental.
*   Paid holidays.
