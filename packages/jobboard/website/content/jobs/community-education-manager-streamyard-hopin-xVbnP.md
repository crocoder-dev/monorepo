_In January 2021, Hopin announced it's acquirement of StreamYard, the leading live streaming studio which allows content creators to create professional-quality video streaming experiences. In addition to eventually integrating its features into the Hopin platform, StreamYard will continue to also offer its live streaming studio tools to independent content creators._ 

## The Role: 

StreamYard is looking for a team-member to create and manage a video resource section on our website. We are in need of a producer of great educational video content that will engage and delight our community.

We want you if you are a great producer with a keen eye for creating a unique and exciting branded learning experience. You are an educator at heart and understand our community and how to create outstanding educational content.

## Responsibilities:

*   Develop original educational content designed to allow live-show creators to discover our platform through learning about live-streaming best practices
*   Create course curriculums and lesson briefs
*   Partner closely with our brand ambassadors and partners to create videos that educate and inspire
*   Understand the needs and motivations of our Customers in order to build trainings tailored to them and new prospects
*   Closely coordinate with the Marketing team and Customer Success to ensure that the content you produce is aligned with our offering
*   Become a Live-show creation and StreamYard product expert
*   Manage all video production aspects, editing and more
*   Create and manage our educational resource center on our website
*   Develop supporting materials for each lesson
*   Coordinate course promotion with the marketing team

## Skills and Qualifications:

*   You are an experienced video producer and can initiate, generate and edit excellent video content
*   You have at least 3 years experience in customer education, course creation, or similar field
*   You have experience in Inbound marketing and Youtube channel building
*   You are very comfortable working with influencers, creators and actors as part of the (remote) production
*   You like being involved in our community and understanding our audience's needs, goals and challenges
*   You have experience working working with other video editors and graphic designers
*   You like to move fast and get stuff done in accordance with the project vision
