## About Hire Space

Hire Space provides everything needed for businesses to host world-class virtual, hybrid, or in-person events, both in the UK and globally. As long-standing EventTech leaders, we are at the forefront of the fast-growing world of virtual events. To help us on this journey we are seeking Virtual Event Managers to join our passionate and collaborative team.

We operate the UK’s largest venue marketplace, which hosts over 6,000 venues and is used by over 2 million prospective event planners each year. Our clients include Google, Facebook, Red Bull, MAC Cosmetics, UNICEF, and Dr. Martens. We have built a reputation for delivering world-class virtual events and are the force behind the largest virtual Xmas party of 2020.

Ultimately, we're here to make events a joy.

## Overview

We’re looking for an outstanding event manager to deliver virtual and hybrid events for our clients on a 4-month fixed-term employment contract. You’ll be responsible for end-to-end event delivery and for developing a professional relationships with clients you work with. You’ll manage all aspects of on-the-day delivery, from speaker and delegate management, to management of live streams (alongside Live Stream Producers).

We’d love it if you have experience in delivering world-class events on behalf of demanding clients. You should also be highly versed in the fast-growing world of virtual events and have deep knowledge of the technologies, platforms, and emerging best practices. Bonus points are given to multi-lingual applicants, especially those who speak German.

You’ll be working as the core of our fast-growing virtual events team in a role with a huge opportunity for professional growth and progression. You’ll also liaise with our sales team to help generate incremental virtual event sales, and work closely with our Live Stream Producers and Designers.

## The role

## You’ll be responsible for:

*   End-to-end management of virtual and hybrid events for our clients
    *   Consult and collaborate to develop creative solutions to achieve our client’s objectives through virtual and hybrid events
    *   Gather, organize and develop assets including the writing of presentation slide copy and entry of copy/design assets provided by the client
    *   Identify, secure and manage keynote speakers, or other talent, as required
    *   Setting up and configuring virtual event platforms to meet the design requirements of the event
    *   Work closely with a designer to create static and video design assets for events you are managing
    *   Managing your own time, and the time of others, to support the execution of multiple virtual events
    *   Management of budgets, staffing, event design, technology selection
    *   Manage and ensure SLA’s (Service Level Agreements) are monitored and achieved throughout the event development process
    *   Occasional management of our own internal events
*   Client relationship and account management
    *   Deep understanding of the client’s business objectives and brand values
    *   Maintaining transparent channels of communication with the client to ensure no miscommunication of event objectives
    *   Accurate adherence to the company’s time tracking policies and systems
    *   Occasional travel to client site for
    *   Collation of post-event feedback to continuously improve your delivery of events to the client
    *   Client relationship management at all levels including senior executives
*   All elements of the on-the-day delivery
    *   Managing live streams, webinar, and video calls (e.g. Zoom or in-browser solutions) to deliver flawless live streams as part of events you are managing
    *   Working closely with our Live Stream Production team to ensure that more sophisticated live streams run flawlessly
    *   Troubleshooting live issues on event days, including technical issues, delegate management, and communications
    *   Risk management, including both technical risks and on-site health and safety (inc. COVID)
    *   Occasional travel to set and on-site management
*   Internal best practice and knowledge sharing:
    *   Development and maintenance of internal best practice guidelines in the rapidly advancing field of virtual events
    *   Acting as an internal thought leader on the differences, strengths, and limitations of various virtual event platforms
    *   Supporting our sales team with platform-specific virtual event queries and training to allow them to be highly knowledgeable and close more sales
    *   Liaising with the Product Manager to assist their understanding and prioritisation of our own virtual event technology
    *   Staying aware of all relevant development within the industry, and sharing this knowledge internally with the team
    *   Working with the sales team to help generate an accurate estimate of time and costs for virtual events
