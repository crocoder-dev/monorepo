**About our Internship Program**

As a thredUP summer intern, you will be given a firsthand glimpse at what it’s like to work at the world’s largest fashion resale platform. During the 11 week program, you will tackle projects alongside our world-class team, with the opportunity to make a real impact on thredUP’s business. Our paid internships are ideal for ambitious students or recent grads who thrive in ambiguity, like a good challenge, and want a jump start on their career development. Oh, and who want to help inspire a new generation of consumers to think secondhand first.

**About the Data Engineer Internship** 

As an intern on our Data Engineering team, you will work alongside thredUP's data warehouse team to build data driven products that will be used internally across several teams. You will get an opportunity to work on varied data sets and projects in our production environment that have a _direct_ impact on the business. Your time will focus on one main project, with several side projects to insure the growth of several areas of skill.

## About You:

*   3 years of experience in SQL and Python development
*   Currently enrolled in a Master's Program in Computer Science or MIS
*   Relevant coursework in Big Data Technologies and Business Analytics
*   Strong Data Analysis skills and an eagerness to learn in a business setting
*   Bonus: hands-on experience building data processing pipelines in Apache Spark

**What We Offer**

At thredUP, we value **infinite learning**. As an intern you’ll have the opportunity to learn about our forward-thinking business and collaborate with our talented team.

We also believe that each person should help drive the business. As an intern, you’ll be encouraged to **speakUP**, **think big**, ask questions and **seek the truth**, and help **influence outcomes** for our business and customers.

You’ll have the opportunity to work with passionate and supportive team members who encourage new ideas, feedback and collaboration.

We have a talented leadership team that encourages **transparency** and they will support you in maximizing your internship experience.
