## Job description

As a Product Designer at Forem, you will help drive design execution to empower open-source communities on our platform. The ideal candidate is a generalist product designer who enjoys tackling complex problems, demonstrates strong craftsmanship in visual and interaction design across multiple platforms, and drives product execution through an iterative and collaborative process.

**Primary responsibilities**

What you’ll do

*   You will transform broad, conceptual ideas into excellent product and user experience for our growing Forem community.
*   You embrace an end-to-end design process that allows you to wear many hats (strategy, research, concept, interaction design & prototyping, QA / building with engineers).
*   You design across platforms (native iOS, Android, web).
*   You root your product decisions in company goals and user needs, often stating the tradeoffs to consider. You can state clearly what problem you are solving.
*   You facilitate brainstorms and discussions with cross-functional peers to create a collaborative environment.
*   You plan, scope, and create work that contributes to a larger, long-term plan.

## Requirements

## Skills or Experience We’d Like to See

*   A portfolio of mobile-first, consumer work that you’ve shipped as the primary designer.
*   Solid, iterative visual and interaction design process demonstrating a range of explorations and intentionality in your choices.
*   Good product-thinking rooted in connecting people problems with product & business opportunities.
*   Experience across native platforms (iOS, Android) and web
*   Experience working collaboratively with cross-functional peers, but also an ability to wear autonomously.
*   An iterative process that embraces prototyping to communicate work.
