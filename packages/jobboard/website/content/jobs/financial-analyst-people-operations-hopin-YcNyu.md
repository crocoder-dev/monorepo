## The Role

Hopin is seeking a Financial Analyst to join the Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) team focusing on company operations. In this position you will be responsible for the planning of headcount and related expenses across the entire company. You will partner directly with leaders of the people team to drive the long-term headcount planning, annual budgeting, and monthly/quarterly forecasting processes. This role plays an important and visible part in crafting the global headcount strategy of a hyper-growth company, where talent will continue to be the key element to our long-term success.

## Responsibility

*   Nurture deep, positive relationships with the Recruiting and the Total Rewards teams
*   Be the go-to finance partner for all things headcount
*   Drive the headcount allocation and expense forecasting processes during the quarterly and annual planning cycles
*   Engage with leaders across all functions to align Hopin's long-term strategy
*   Partner with Accounting to deliver employment expense variance analyses on a monthly basis

## Experience

*   Bachelor’s degree in a quantitative field (Finance, Accounting, Economics, or a related field)
*   Experience with budgeting and forecasting processes, accounting and accruals, and interpreting and reporting results
*   Strong technical fundamentals and proficiency in financial modeling using Excel/Google Sheets
*   Prior software sector experience is preferred, Cloud/SaaS is a strong plus
