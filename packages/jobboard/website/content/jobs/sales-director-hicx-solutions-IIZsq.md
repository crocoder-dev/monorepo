As part of our exciting growth plans for 2021 and beyond, HICX is looking to hire an experienced, ambitious and energetic Sales Director to support our activity in US market. As a Sales Director with HICX you will play a key role in taking advantage of our market opportunity as we look to achieve hyper growth. Last year we trebled revenues and with your help will be looking to do the same again in the coming 12 months. Experience in selling ProcureTech solutions is a must. This role reports directly to the Chief Commercial Officer and you will form a key part of the US sales leadership team.

## Our Company

HICX is the world’s leading solution provider for supplier data and information management software. We enable some of the world most complex, and innovative, organisations to find, re-use and maintain supplier data and information across any spreadsheet, app or system.

HICX is changing the way B2B companies are identified and creating a low code software platform empowering an impressive array of enterprise customers across all industries to transform, automate and modernize, procurement and supplier operations.

Our Mission is to enable businesses to find, maintain and re-use trusted Supplier Data and Information across their Enterprise, across any spreadsheet, app or system.

## Key Responsibilities include:

*   Deliver sales performance against your own and company expectations.
*   Prospect opportunities to build and maintain a robust sales pipeline.
*   Develop sales and go to market strategy for assigned territory.
*   Partner with marketing and business development teams in order to maximize your market opportunity.
*   Work in collaboration with pre-sales support and implementation teams.
*   Develop expert knowledge of the HICX platform and the tangible benefits we bring to clients.
*   Maintain detailed records in CRM of sales pipeline to develop a forecasting capability as well as visibility of sales activities and ongoing account conversations.
*   Utilize appropriate sales methodologies to understand the objectives and needs of customers and develop solutions through the offerings of HICX.
*   Diligently and timely coordinate internal resources in customer bid processes.
*   Research and understand the market including competitor analysis and general market trends and provide input to commercial, product and company strategy.

## About you:

*   A thriving Enterprise Software Sales Hunter with 5-15 years’ experience as an individual contributor used to selling across functional stakeholders in the $5bn+ Fortune 2000 segments.
*   You have experience in enterprise data management, analytics or API related technologies and are knowledgeable of the challenges faced by several vertical markets including FMCG, Industrial manufacturing and Healthcare.
*   You have proven ability to engage both high and wide in accounts and can engage effectively with C-level and functional executives, preferably Procurement, Supply Chain, Finance or Shared Services.
*   You have the ability to conceptually understand our solution and explain it on a high level
*   You have a hunter’s mentality with a history of over achievement
*   You are exceptional at building a solid pipeline through own initiative
*   You have a forecasting skills
