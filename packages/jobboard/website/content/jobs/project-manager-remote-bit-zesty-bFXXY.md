## Description

We have a vacancy for a Project Manager to join us on a full time, permanent basis. This role will work remotely and can be based from anywhere in the UK. In this post, you’ll be responsible for the project management of bespoke software applications and website development. You’ll work closely with a technical team of developers, UX designers and fellow PMs to deliver an exceptional experience to our clients. It’s a varied, hands-on role in which you’ll also be performing business analysis, quality assurance and managing ongoing support requests.

On a day to day basis, you’ll be involved with the following:

*   Reporting to the Managing Director and working closely with Developers, UX Designers and Project Managers
*   Leading the delivery of multiple digital product projects for our clients
*   Writing user stories and acceptance criteria
*   Conducting client meetings to communicate progress, run demos, review priorities and provide budget updates
*   Creating and maintaining project documentation and project boards
*   Leading daily team stand-ups, identifying impediments and helping to eliminate them
*   Testing our work to ensure it meets the quality standards and acceptance criteria
*   Triaging and resolving support requests from the clients you look after
*   Overseeing the estimation process and actively tracking budget versus actuals

## Requirements

Ideally, we’re looking for someone with the following:

*   Strong project management experience
*   A technical background or passion for tech
*   Experience writing user stories and acceptance criteria
*   Experience in hands-on testing
*   Comfortable working on multiple projects and a varied workload
*   Strong communication across different teams and cultures
