_In January 2021, Hopin announced its acquisition of StreamYard, the leading live streaming studio which allows content creators to create professional-quality video streaming experiences. In addition to eventually integrating its features into the Hopin platform, StreamYard will continue to also offer its live streaming studio tools to independent content creators._ 

## The role:

StreamYard is looking for a creative content creator to produce unique, fun and engaging content for our social media channels. If you are a natural storyteller who wants the freedom to create and has a mastery of graphic and video-editing tools to create the best Social Media content, we want to hear from you.This is an exciting opportunity to work with a strong, fast moving marketing team in a dynamic and hot industry.

You are a creative content creator with strong creative, graphic and video editing skills, and a deep understanding of what works on social.

## Responsibilities

*   Create visual content for all our social media channels
*   Create video snippets from our live shows
*   Create stories that reflect our company culture and our community's activities
*   Create fun video snippets, overlays and images to spice up our live shows
*   Collaborate with our designers, freelancers and partners to scale content creation

## Skills and experience

*   A Video editing ninja
*   Experience with graphic design tools like Figma and adobe creative suite
*   A healthy sense of humour
*   A keen eye to identifying meaningful bits of micro-content within longform content
*   A talent for turning regular every-day stuff into great social content
