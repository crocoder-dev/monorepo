## Description

The Job:

Do you love helping? Do you want to be a part of the distributed work movement? Hubstaff is enabling other location-independent teams' success, and we are hiring and looking for 2 part-time employees to help manage our Hubstaff Talent platform. Across that, we look for someone with outstanding remote software support skills. The hours can be flexible. However, we are looking for coverage across a diversity of time zones and the ability to commit to 1-2 hours a day of work during a specified range of time (UTC + 3 to UTC +7).

Hubstaff is a time tracking, project management, and proof of work SaaS solution. We are on a mission to accelerate the digital transition of work and make it possible for even more work to be efficiently performed in a remote environment. Interested? Read below to see if you might be a fit.

What Will, Your day to day, look like?

*   To manage and review profiles and jobs along with other assets that are uploaded each day.
*   Ensure there are not long wait times for uploaded items to be approved (Please note some days we experience high volumes, and it will be your responsibility to identify when you need additional help from other team members)
*   Proofread profiles, resumes, and job descriptions for the correct use of grammar and spelling.
*   This role is repetitive in nature however plays a vital role in Hubstaff. You’ll be enabling remote work for individuals that often are new to remote work!
*   You must have a keen eye for detail and maintain a sense of urgency to handle peak volumes.

We look forward to hearing from you and are excited to have you join the Hubstaff team.

Data Protection:_Hubstaff treats users’ personal data with special care. The selected candidates will be contacted by email (HR department)._ _Notice that _Hubstaff does not contact candidates through Facebook or other social media platforms. If you receive a job offer from someone via these channels, please report and disregard it._ The data collected will be only used for recruitment purposes._

## Requirements

*   2+ years experience in fields related to Human Resources, recruitment, or customer service.
*   Live in the UTC+3 to UTC+7 and be able to adapt 1-2hours/day to work synchrony within the rest of the team.
*   Available to work Part-Time/ 20 Hours/week (notice: This is not a full-time position, please apply if you are available only for a part-time).
*   Stable internet connection with speeds high enough for videoconferencing and screen sharing. Having a backup (e.g., high-speed cellular internet) is a plus.
*   You have an organized workplace with a modern enough computer, web-cam, preferably a good microphone/headset, and an environment that allows you to conduct professional presentations with minimal interruption.
*   We will pay hourly a pay-rate between USD 3 to USD 6 (based on the experience and candidate's skills).
