## Job description

Luckbox is an esports betting platform built around the ethos of ‘Esports Betting Done Right’. As we continue to build our award winning platform, we immediately require a qualified candidate to fill the role of User Interface / Interaction Designer.

Overview

Working closely with the Director of Product, the successful candidate will be a key player in visualising the next generation of product designs and crafting world-class gaming features that will cement Luckbox’s reputation as the most distinct and innovative brand in the esports betting space. Your job is to help us create the richest, most immersive and most interactive esports betting experience for our global community of players.

The Product Designer will be responsible for creating the Luckbox user interface across desktop and mobile devices, including complex interactive controls, animations and transitions.

Working from product briefs and low fidelity wireframes, you will work iteratively to produce mock ups, prototypes and pixel perfect User Interface assets and key animations which will be used across the Luckbox platform.

During presentations and reviews, you will be expected to explain your designs with reference to user insights, competitor research and best practice in design.

On approval of your designs, you'll work closely with product managers and development teams to support the successful integration and deployment of your designs, and you will support post-launch evaluation and work with the design team on performance analysis and reports on your designs, with a goal of identifying potential improvements for the future.

## Job requirements

Requirements

The ideal candidate for this position will have experience developing interactions for gaming solutions (e.g. mobile gaming) or for interactive gambling products (poker, blackjack, casino games). A knowledge and understanding of betting and real money transactions and micro-transactions is a distinct advantage.

The candidate should have a demonstrable understanding of gamification principles and have appreciation of the latest trends in game design.

While knowledge of esports and competitive gaming is advantageous, it is not a prerequisite for the role.

This position is a communications role. All applicants should have strong English language skills and confidence discussing & presenting their work in English.

All candidates must provide a portfolio of design work with their application.

*   A minimum of four years of UX or interaction design experience in a gaming or igaming role
*   Strong work portfolio that demonstrates creative solutions to interactive UI problems, as well as a deep understanding of UI/UX best practices
*   Strong ability to recognise and predict use cases and design user interaction that anticipates user needs
*   Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English
*   Deep understanding of information design / architecture and user psychology
*   Ability to manage time across multiple projects and be accountable for work
*   Proficiency in graphic design and digital art, and an understanding of typography, colour theory and layout for responsive design
*   Experience collaborating within a cross-functional team comprising designers, product managers and development teams Proven experience working with in-house UI guidelines

Remote work option considered for suitable candidates. Occasional travel within the EU may be required, once COVID health and safety measures allow.
