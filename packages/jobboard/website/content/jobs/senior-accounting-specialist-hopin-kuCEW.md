## Responsibilities:

*   Own the month-end close including journal entry, accruals, BS account reconciliations and P&L fluctuation analysis for US based legal entities
*   Responsible for AR cash application, payment reconciliation between legacy and new ERP system during the transition period
*   Work with internal and external partners to manage, validate and automate FA accounts (additions, depreciation and disposals)
*   Evaluate and support the automation of Prepayment and Fixed Assets accounts
*   Responsible for monthly payroll entries and reconciliations
*   Work with external service providers to reconcile tax accounts (VAT, sales tax, CIT, etc) and
*   ensure taxes are filed and settled per statutory deadlines
*   Involve in Phase II ERP system testing & implementation, and investigate historical balance
*   after Phase I data migration
*   Document and update procedures for on-going accounting activities
*   Assist annual statutory audit for the Holding company and its subsidiaries
*   Support accounting team with various analysis and ad hoc projects
*   Guide and support the team with accounting entries per new COA and org structure

## Qualifications:

*   Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Finance, Accounting or related field
*   Strong knowledge in IFRS and US GAAP
*   Minimum 5 years working experiences in Finance & Accounting position, accounting
*   qualification (CPA, ACCA, CIMA) is considered a plus
*   Excellent computer skills, especially Excel and GSuite
*   Expertise in ERP system Netsuite is a plus
*   Strong analytical skills, ability to research and resolve issues without guidance
*   Curious, proactive, accurate, solutions-orientated, continuous improvement attitude
*   Strong interpersonal and English communication skills – verbal, written and facilitation
*   Flexibility to work across multiple time zones
