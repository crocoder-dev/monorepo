Auth0 is a unicorn that just closed a $120M Series F round of funding, with total capital raised to date of $330M and valuation of nearly $2B. We are growing rapidly and looking for exceptional new team members to add to our exceptional talent pool - and who will help take us to the next level of success. One team, one score. 

Our vision is to provide people with secure access to any application in one click or less. And our promise is to make identity work for everyone—whether you’re a developer looking to innovate, or a security professional looking to mitigate. We are looking for curious, excited, boundary-pushing team members. So, if you’re a big thinker who is nimble and adaptable, Auth0 may be an ideal place for you to shine.

We are a security company and Auth0's Security Engineering team is in the privileged position of supporting a security-first culture for a company that wants to make the internet safer. 

Auth0 is looking for a **Senior Platform Security Engineer** to join the Platform Security team to work directly with our engineering partners to build Auth0’s future data platform. Successful candidates will have a passion for building reliable and scalable distributed systems, a technical background, hands-on experience in developing data platforms and infrastructure, a solid understanding of data security issues, strong threat modeling experience, and the ability to collaborate with others to drive initiatives forward. In this role, you will have the opportunity to significantly contribute to making Auth0's future data platform secure by design.

This role can be based from a remote office anywhere in the US or CAN.

## What Will You Do:

*   Collaborate with Security, Engineering and Data teams to incorporate strong security controls, apply security best practices in our development life cycle, and mitigate risks and security vulnerabilities.
*   Promote and drive the implementation of a data security architecture that supports Engineering’s goals and deliverables, through strategy, design, requirements, and code.
*   Contribute to defining the n+1 cloud infrastructure maturity level and help to achieve goals that get us there.
*   Implement technical prototypes to understand new technologies as well as identify and manage risks for projects in active development.
*   Contribute to improving Auth0's data security patterns, security controls and best practices.
*   Mentor team members and engineers on security best practices, and develop the next generation of technical security leaders.

## What You Bring:

*   5+ years security engineer experience.
*   Strong experience with AWS data analytics services and security controls, and preferably hands-on experience with Azure.
*   Experience with MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Kafka.
*   Passion for building secure, reliable and scalable distributed systems in collaboration with multiple teams.
*   Excellence at creating clarity and alignment for technical initiatives. 
*   Ability to lead and resolve company-wide technical discussions.
*   Experience with data warehouses, star and snowflake schemas, ETL automation, BI Visualization, and cloud-based data management tools.
*   Proficiency in Golang, Python, Java.
*   Familiarity with Hadoop, Hive, Spark, Airflow, Snowflake, Druid and related technologies.
*   Experience with implementing security controls for data governance laws such as HIPAA, SOC2, PCI, and GDPR.
*   Knowledge of containerization, CI/CD pipeline, and orchestration with Kubernetes.
