## Description

At Nixa.io, we are on a global mission to change both _how_ and _where_ companies recruit technical talent. In fact, we want to flip the traditional model of recruitment by introducing an automated and self-service way of hiring pre-vetted talent in just 2 weeks.

We believe that by equipping people with the best tools to solve their own problems, we can tackle the whole world's problems better, together. We’re extremely proud of the work we do and the way we are changing the world, but it all boils down to the talented and dedicated people who make up the Nixa.io team. Do you want to be part of it?
