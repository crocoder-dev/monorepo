## Job description

**Location**: Remote for now (Amsterdam)

**Duration**: 6 months

**Start Date**: February 2021

Our client is a large global health and beauty retail group, headquartered in Asia.As part of the client's journey in leveraging data-driven decision making, we are looking for someone with hands onexperience with managing, analyzing and visualizing large data sets.This individual will haveexcellent analytical and communication skills. If you have experience as a Data Analyst using SQL and Tableau,and are up for a challenging and interesting role, this is for you!

Within this role you will be responsible for:

*   Identifying, analyzing, and interpreting data trends or patterns.
*   Analysisof results through statistical techniques to provide business insights
*   Build dashboards, reports and visualizations to allow others at the company to be able to ask the right data questions
*   Designing data models to provide fast and accurate answers to stakeholder questions and hypotheses
*   Preparation of continuous reports and dashboards
*   Keep a close eye on the changing data challenges at the client and collaboratewith the team to solve for them
*   Drive adata driven approach and philosophy at the client

## Job requirements

## Essential qualifications/skills/competencies:

*   3+ years of experience in a Data Analyst role
*   You have a bachelor's or master’s degree in computer science, economics, econometrics, and/or business administration.
*   Have a strong working knowledge of SQL
*   Excellent knowledge and experience using Tableau
*   Willingness to be hands-on
*   Excellent data analysis and problem-solving skills (incl. data crunching/process analysis)
*   Fluent in Dutch (preferred)
