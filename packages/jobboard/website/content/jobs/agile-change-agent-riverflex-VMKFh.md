## Job description

**Location:** Amsterdam Surrounds (Remote during COVID)

**Start Date:** January 2021

Our client is a global retail organisation headquartered in the Netherlands. We are looking for a strong Agile Change Agent that can help develop an agile delivery framework for the clients global IAM (Identity & Access Management) programme. We are looking for someone with excellent stakeholder management and communication skills, and the ability to drive strategic planning in a complex & dynamic environment. If you are up for a challenging and interesting role, this is for you!

We need a seasoned Agile Change Agent to work alongside the IAM leadership team to:

*   Stream strategy, roadmap, and accountability to be clearly defined
*   Support prioritizing and aligning day-to-day work to business objectives
*   Clear translation of the IAM strategy and roadmap into IAM streams
*   Co-develop and promote appropriate values, principles, and practices to enable greater value delivery
*   Create a self-sustaining capability for continuous improvement
*   Provide Program Management and Agile Delivery Oversight
*   Support the Product Owners to define the Product Backlogs and integrate User Experience Feedbacks
*   Create an environment in which to allow for improved User Experience
*   Collaborate with Operations to enable incremental delivery and de-risk projects
*   Standardize reporting which is currently fragmented, no single version of truth

## Job requirements

*   Highly experienced with agile delivery methods
*   10+ years track record in delivering IT successfully with agile methods
*   Ability to adopt a coaching stance to support others to articulate and address problems and opportunities
*   Experience working with Azure cloud migrations and Identity Access Management solutions is preferred
