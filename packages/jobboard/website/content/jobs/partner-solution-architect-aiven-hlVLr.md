## Description

Aiven is a cloud technology startup launched in 2016 and headquartered in Helsinki, Finland.

Our vision is to empower everyone with the best data technology. Not only do we aim to make life better for developers - we want to be good for business. Whenever a company uses their best talent for maintenance work, they lose an opportunity to focus on creating something new. No one expects you to cut down your own logs to build your furniture - why would it be any different with building applications? We provide an effortless way to turn great ideas into awesome applications.

Our mission is to make developers’ lives better. With our help, developers can do what they do best: create awesome applications. Meanwhile, we do what we do best: manage cloud data infrastructure. We want to free developers from operational burdens - and let them build what matters. We democratise access to open source databases and data technologies such as Apache Kafka, PostgreSQL, MySQL, M3, and Apache Cassandra through managed cloud services.

Aiven’s Partner team is looking for **Partner Solution Architect** to join the global team.

Our team’s mission is to create an exponential distribution of Aiven products. Partner Solution Architect works with the Partner sales team on pre-sales and post-sales motion. Aiven’s partnerships include strategic OEM partners who integrate Aiven’s DBaaS services as part of their overall solutions and Hyperscalers whose Marketplaces will be essential for Enterprises to acquire Aiven’s services on their selected cloud ecosystems.

Partner Solution Architect will work closely with Aiven’s product teams to align OEMs needs maximizing the capitalization of our partnership. Your daily responsibilities will include working with new partner opportunities as well as developing our existing partnerships further from a solution perspective.

You will preferably be located in a European time zone ideally close to one of our offices (Helsinki / Berlin).

## Requirements

*   Be an active contributor during the sales process
*   Helping partners succeed, building successful relationships from pre-sales stage to roll out and beyond
*   Conduct successful proof of concepts and solve the Partners problems in getting up and running
*   Understand partner’s’ technical requirements on an architectural level and propose solutions to address them using Aiven’s services
*   Ability to communicate complex technical concepts in an approachable and correct technical level for the audience
*   Ability to implement proof of concept software solutions as needed in either pre-sales and/or post-sales capacity to interface with Aiven services in order to address business requirements
*   Ability to form deep technical relationships with customers technical staff as well as Aiven’s product teams
*   Proven success working in a fast-paced, agile development environment.
*   Experience working in technology/software sales, pre-sales or consulting
*   Excellent communication skills to translate business needs into technical terms.

Technical skills

*   Fluency in one or more programming languages such as Java, Python, Go or Ruby
*   Knowledgeable with public clouds (AWS, Google or Azure) and in the areas of compute, storage - block and object, networking, and security groups
*   Technical knowledge on two or more of the Open Source data technologies used in our services
*   Linux operating system, demonstrable experience using various Linux utilities and tools (installation, securing system, storage, etc.) and has a good grasping of networking basics like DNS
*   Technical experience and conceptual understanding of Authentication and Authorisation methods (SSO, SAML)
*   Technical experience with Infrastructure as Code tooling
*   Act as a subject matter expert on Aiven service offerings and link between Aiven product organization and the Partner
