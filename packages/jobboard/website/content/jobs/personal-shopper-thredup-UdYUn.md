**About thredUP**

In 2009, thredUP set out to make used clothes the new normal and create a more sustainable future for fashion. The company designed a modern resale experience that took the work and risk out of thrift, and converted a generation of secondhand skeptics to fans. Sellers send clothes directly to thredUP by the bagful, and buyers shop quality-checked items from over 35,000 brands at steep discounts. To power this marketplace, thredUP built an infrastructure that has recirculated 100 million unique garments via proprietary technology, data and logistics. thredUP most recently expanded its platform with Resale-as-a-Service (RaaS) to power resale for the world’s leading fashion brands. As a circular fashion pioneer, thredUP is reducing fashion’s impact on the planet and unlocking economic value for consumers and brands alike.

**How You Will Make An Impact:**

We are seeking fashionable, independent, remote personal shoppers who want to bring the joy of thrifting to customers through a personalized shopping service. First and foremost, you will use your fashion expertise and attention to detail to help customers look and feel amazing by delivering a box styled just for them. You will use your insights to provide feedback that will shape the future direction of styling tools and team processes, in order to benefit the styling team and thredUP customers alike. You will be a coach, team player and problem solver, sharing your knowledge with your peers to help us create a world-class styling team that brings customers back again and again.

## In This Role You'll Get To:

*   Delight customers by providing a personalized box filled with the best thrifted items from thredUP’s unique inventory to elevate and refresh their wardrobes
*   Learn how to blend the art of styling with the science of data inventory to help a wide and diverse range of customers 
*   Develop a strong understanding of current fashion trends, styles, brands, body types, fit and silhouettes 
*   Learn about performance and productivity metrics and become an organized personal shopper who excels at prioritization and efficiency
*   Read customer feedback to improve personal performance and translate it to a better next box 
*   Communicate and partner with peers and leaders to increase styling adeptness, build relationships and make product improvements
*   Establish a client base by providing a great Goody Box experience that brings them back for more

## What We’re Looking For:

*   Ability to work a **minimum** of **30-40** hours per week 
*   Passion for inspiring customers to find their personal style and providing an excellent customer experience
*   Ability to style a wide-range of clients (including but not limited to petite and plus)
*   A well-versed knowledge of current trends, styles and a wide range of brands (including brand aesthetic and price structure)
*   Comfortable analyzing simple data to make quick decisions
*   Tech-savvy, able to navigate a new web platform for styling clients and use G-Suite applications (Google Docs, Gmail, etc)
*   Natural problem solvers and quick learners who enjoy taking on challenges and thrive in a fast-paced, goal-driven environment
*   Detailed oriented individuals who approach their work with care
*   Collaborative team players who are receptive to feedback and like to learn new things
*   Self-motivated with an ability to work independently, manage time efficiently with minimal supervision and comfortable styling customers all day on a virtual dashboard
