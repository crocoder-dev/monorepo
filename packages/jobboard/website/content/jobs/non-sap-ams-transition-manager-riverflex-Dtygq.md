## Job description

**Location**: Belgium - Brussels

**Duration**: 5 months

**Start Date**: September 2021 - Jan 2022

Our client is based in Belgium and ispart ofa global food retail and distributionorganisation. We are looking for a strong Non-SAP AMS Transition Manager that can take end-to-end ownership of the Non-SAP AMS project. They will lead the transition & coordinate both internal and external project team members to help deliver the Non-SAP AMS project on time and on budget. The Non-SAP AMS Transition Manager will manage all elements of the Project and be linked closely to the PMO, including but not limited to; Budget Management, Senior Management Reporting, Change Management and Management of Change and posses the ability to Communicate and Influence stakeholders at all levels of the organization.If you are up for a challenging and interesting role, this is for you!

## What you will be responsible for:

*   Deliver the Non-SAP AMS project on time and on budget
*   Establish and maintain strong project Governance
*   Chair relevant meetings, including the Steering Committee for the transition project
*   Challenge and further develop the detailed transition plan, liaising with different Managers / Directors leads and Partners
*   Develop relationships and liaise with vendor transition coordinators
*   Organize weekly meetings with team/stakeholders and create and review status updates
*   Ensure everyone has the tools they require to do their jobs and are directed appropriately for support
*   Create and deliver Non-SAP AMS project newsletter to be transmitted to all stakeholders on a regular basis
*   Keep team and stakeholders formally up to date on all project statuses and updates
*   Be the single point of contact for the IT Directors regarding the project

## Job requirements

**Essential qualifications/skills/competencies**:

*   Bachelors or Master’s Degree in Business, IT or management
*   8+ years relevant experience in Project / Programme Management
*   In addition to excellent English,French and/or Dutch language skills are necessary for this role.
*   Ability to quickly develop in-depth understanding of project scope and particulars
*   Excellent communication skills
*   Time management skills with proven track record of meeting hard deadlines
*   Ability to work with a mixed team of internal and external resources in different multicultural environments
*   Strong inter-personal skills, including teamwork, facilitation, communication, negotiation and diplomacy, as well as relationship management capabilities
*   Excellent organizational skills and ability to work under pressure
*   Results orientated with high standards of performance
