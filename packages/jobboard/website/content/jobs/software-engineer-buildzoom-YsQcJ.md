BuildZoom is a managed marketplace for construction services. A data platform that ingests and analyzes a vast amount of construction data enables us to cull through hundreds of millions of records to identify and match the most qualified contractors with our clients. On the application layer, project owners can manage their communications and workflow through planning, bidding, and hiring (pre-construction); construction businesses can manage their client pipeline, and drill down to work through the bidding process with an individual client. 

We’re a lean but growing product & engineering team that is having an outsized impact on the company and industry as a whole. As a software engineer on the team, you’ll tackle a range of projects that align with your strengths and interests. Opportunities exist across full-stack web development, native mobile, data engineering, and infrastructure. 

The ideal candidate is energized by tackling a broad range of challenges across the stack, and is excited about playing a key role in building a platform that is positively impacting an enormous industry. 

**This position can be located in Phoenix, AZ or San Francisco, CA.**

## Responsibilities

*   Responsibility for the full product lifecycle, from planning, through design, implementation, deployment, and maintenance. 
*   Craft elegant and extensible solutions, at scale, using modern frameworks and technologies to deliver features that people interact with, and rely on, every day.
*   Collaborate closely with software engineers and key stakeholders from other teams (e.g. operations, growth, sales) during the planning and design stages, synthesize feedback to iterate and improve the customer experience.   

## Requirements

*   Exceptional verbal and written communication abilities. 
*   2+ years of hands-on software engineering experience in a professional team-based environment.
*   Must have professional experience: Ruby on Rails, Javascript
*   Nice to have experience: ReactJS, React Native, Typescript, GraphQL, Elasticsearch, Redis, SQL
