## Job description

**Location**: Remote (ideally based inNetherlands, Sweden or Germany)

**Start Date**: 1 February2021 (for 3-6 months)

Our client is a mid-size European outdoor consumer products group that have multiple brands through acquisition. We have been engaged to support the Technology team setup their Dev(Sec)Ops organisation and processes. You will work directly with the CIO of the group and other IT leaders to develop a Dev(Sec)Ops organisation blueprint practices across a range of different technologies. Our client is presently undertaking a digital transformation where a number of products are being developed by offshore suppliers utilising a Jamstack architecture.

## Key Responsibilities

*   Undertaking an initial discovery of Development and Operational practices
*   Developing a recommendation for the future Dev(Sec)Ops organisation blueprint (enabling a single managed pipeline of demand across design, develop, secure, test and run activities)
*   Drive the Dev(Sec)Ops implementation in the company
*   Assemble, guide and coach the different teams involved
*   Works across entire IT organization, including Infrastructure & Operations to solve challenging problems, improving the working processes and scaling it to support our continuous growth in a cost-effective way

## Job requirements

**Essential qualifications/skills/competencies:**

*   Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science or related field, or equivalent IT experience
*   Over 7 years of previous experience in technology delivery roles and in Dev(sec)Ops environments
*   Previous experience setting up DevOps teams.
*   Has managed or lead a team of DevOps engineers, ideally delivering large scale systems in production
*   Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
*   Deep understanding and experience of agile methodologies
*   Understanding of cultural aspect of DevOps and how to build those in teams and organizations
