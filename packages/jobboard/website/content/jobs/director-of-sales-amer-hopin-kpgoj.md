## 

At Hopin, we’re reimagining virtual events.

As the first all-in-one virtual venue for live online events, Hopin brings people together in a highly interactive and engaging online experience that feels just like an in-person event, only without the barriers. Whether it’s a 50-person meetup, or a 50,000-person conference—any type of event organizer can host a Hopin event for their attendees, speakers, sponsors, and vendors to convene, learn, and interact face-to-face online. We’re spearheading the innovative technology that is enabling people to gather online like never before.

Our mission is simple: **better connections for a better planet**. 

We believe in events without limits, but events that are sustainable and accessible to everyone—and fun, too! To do this, we need passionate builders and storytellers to join us. Do you love people and technology and the magic that happens when they converge at scale? Do you have the skills and experience we’re looking for to better serve our clients? If so, we’d like to meet.

We’ve been recently funded by a16z, General Catalyst, IVP, Accel, DFJ Growth, Northzone, Coatue, Tiger Global, Salesforce Ventures and many other high profile investors.

## The Role

As a senior leader of the sales organisation this person will help build and scale the midmarket and enterprise sales teams in AMER. They will develop, train, and hire highly driven and enthusiastic team members. You will take overall responsibility for inspiring, mentoring and building within the Sales organisation. We are looking for a self-starter that can excel in a high pace environment, and be successful in dealing with ambiguity. 

Ideal candidates will have second line sales management experience at a Saas business where they had to grow a region and own a P&L. Having prior startup experience preferred.

## Responsibilities

*   Lead, coach and mentor a team of Mid-market and Enterprise AE's
*   Hire and train new Account Executive to ramp quickly
*   Develop the strategy to scale the business and hit business goals
*   Work cross-functionally with Marketing, Account Management and other Sales team
*   Inspire and motivate team members to develop and grow, and do the best work of their career

## About You

*   15+ years of sales or account management experience
*   5+ years of second line people management experience
*   Enterprise experience in software/SaaS, experience selling into fortune 500 companies.
*   Experience selling into C-Suite, Marketing Departments, and LOB are a plus
*   Ability to hire, train and coach high-performing Account Executives
*   Ability to support Managers in dealing with complex deals and customers
*   Exceptional communication skills to help your team succeed in the market, and effectively communicate with other functions at Hopin
*   Executive level presence and relationships
*   You are passionate about Hopin and our mission
