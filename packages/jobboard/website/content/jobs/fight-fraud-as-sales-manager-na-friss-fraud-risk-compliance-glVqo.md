## Job description

We need you to help us reach our Big Hairy Audacious Goal By building really smart software we enable insurers worldwide to automatically detect insurance fraud and identify high risks. Our ultimate dream is that in 2025 the FRISS Score is the global trusted standard for honest insurance. Are you ready for an adventure? We are expanding our USA team,are you going to beour new Sales Manager?

## Your mission as Sales Manager in the US

The need for fraud detection is increasingly important as insurance carriers accelerate their digital initiatives. Our company has a long reputation for being the best at what we do in several European markets and has established that same reputation in the US and Canada. Now it’s time to accelerate that growth, and you’re the one to help take us to the next level. In this role you will position FRISS products within insurance carriers from Directors to C-Suite. You’ll generate new opportunities, collaborate with industry partners, qualify prospects, craft solutions to meet their business needs, demonstrate our products in a compelling way and manage contract negotiations to effectively bring deals to a successful close.

## What will be your key activities?

*   Clearly understand the key challenges our customers (P&C insurers in the US and Canada) face and how our fraud detection solutions solve them.
*   Actively develop new business in the US and Canada, including an actionable territory growth plan
*   Lead sales activities from initial contact through deal close
*   Build and maintain relationships with key decisionmakers (technical and business) at current and prospective clients
*   Deliver compelling product demonstrations and presentations
*   Develop meaningful relationships with industry partners to further mutual growth objectives
*   Keep meticulous records in our CRM
*   Create and present accurate, realistic forecasts and unapologetically work through challenges with your team
*   Deeply understand our company’s offerings and present as a knowledgeable expert to our prospects
*   Prepare detailed quotations, proposals, RFP responses, executive summaries, etc. that exceed client expectations
*   Represent the company at trade shows, conferences, industry meetings and virtual events to initiate and progress sales
*   Research and develop a thorough understanding of the market, including prospects, partners and competitors, and work collaboratively with marketing, product development and other groups to maximize our offerings
*   Anything and everything else you and we can think of to drive success
