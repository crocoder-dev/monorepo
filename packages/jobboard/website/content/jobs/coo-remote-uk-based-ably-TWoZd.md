## Job description

**Location:** Remote, mainly UK based person, as travel to our London office will be required once a month.

Working at Ably means helping to build the infrastructure and technology that will power and shape the future of the internet: we handle the complex, behind-the-scenes realtime communication required to power digital experiences like chat, live updates in maps or apps, IoT device control, and remote collaboration.

Developers at companies like HubSpot, Toyota Racing Development, OfferUp, and Hopin choose Ably because, when it comes down to it, we’re the only realtime communication platform that dependably delivers at global internet scale.

The opportunity in front of us is immense. Our mission is to become the definitive realtime infrastructure of the internet while powering and accelerating adoption of realtime user experiences. We’re on track. Everyday we deliver billions of realtime messages to more than 100 million people in 80 different countries across web, mobile, and IoT platforms. But we can’t rest yet. In five years we want to reach one billion people everyday.

To see what life’s like at Ably, check out our careers page at[ably.com/careers](http://ably.com/careers).

#### **Role context**

Following their Series A fundraise, the two founders and the broader Ably team have reached the stage where they need to invest in bringing on board a proven operations leader, with experience managing the Finance function, to support the organisation through the next phase of Ably’s growth journey. The COO will have broad autonomy to refine the company’s operating model, financial strategy and drive budgets, KPIs and other growth objectives as well as collaborating extensively with other functional leaders to help manage sustainable growth.

## Requirements

#### **In this role**

Based in London, the COO will be responsible for the company’s strategic and financial vision, direction and operations. They will play a key part in formulating, developing and delivering on overall business strategy, with an emphasis on scalability, operational efficiency, and building long-term shareholder value. They will oversee the company’s growing operations, finance, and legal teams and will have an exceptionally broad remit, playing a key role on the executive team - reporting to Matt, the Founder/CEO.

First and foremost, the COO will formulate, develop and deliver Ably’s overall growth strategy and

target operating model. This will include:

*   Partnering closely with Ably’s executive team to formulate and prioritise business objectives and drive performance/delivery.
*   Acting as an interface between Ably’s current (and future) investors and the executive team, where appropriate.
*   Driving Ably’s next fundraising process (Series B, likely in H2 2021) and helping to ensure that its capital structure is optimal and scalable; supporting Matt, they will assist the funding process to a successful conclusion.
*   Preparation of budgets, board materials and business plans on a continual basis.
*   Challenging current ways of working, driving efficiencies, revenue/margin generation opportunities, and cost-saving initiatives wherever possible.
*   Leading (and building) the analytics and business intelligence capabilities for the business, ensuring the teams can make informed decisions quickly and optimise frequently.
*   Developing sound operating and commercial models to support strategic initiatives - as an example, they will collaborate directly with the Sales and Marketing teams to design budgets, KPIs, forecasting plans, delivery schedules, and contingency plans.
*   Participate in operations reviews to ensure both short-term and long-term plans are on track and help manage priorities and track metrics across the organisation.
*   Providing proactive input into contract negotiations and contract decision making; help ensure compliance and business processes are followed; maintaining strong HR, legal and operational processes.
*   Overseeing cash flow, spend, budgets and monitoring financial operations and decision making.

Building on the above, the COO will own the operational plan and revenue model to drive revenue maximisation. They will develop and lead the annual forecast and budgeting process and assess how best to deploy capital. They will define high-value and revenue-critical activities and strike a balance, ensuring the business is focusing on the right activities to maximise growth, but in an efficient and prudent way. They will also monitor external business, economic and any relevant regulatory developments that could impact Ably.

The COO will operate as a conduit between external investors and Ably’s leadership. In doing so, they will provide guidance, data and analysis of exceptional quality, relevance and insight, enabling all parties to make bold, business-critical decisions with complete confidence in the integrity of the underlying information.

Team development will be a key component of this role over time. The COO will inherit a small team on day one, but will be expected to expand this as they see fit to meet evolving business demands. Irrespective of team size, they will be an outstanding leader who will set the highest standards in terms of hiring, training and development, retention and employee satisfaction across the business as a whole. A strong and inspiring communicator, they will act as an advisor and mentor to their direct reports, and drive a culture of excellence, accountability and operational rigour across the company.

They will become a true partner, ally and trusted advisor to all members of the executive team tasked with driving the growth of Ably into a much larger business with a significant valuation. They will share the pace, entrepreneurialism, ambition and resilience, and passion that Matt and Paddy (the founders)have based the Ably team culture upon.

#### **To be successful in this role**

The successful candidate will be an exceptional COO, with a stellar track record of operationalleadership within high-growth businesses. They will ideally have experience in a B2B / SaaS businessenvironment. With outstanding leadership and communication skills, they will have built and ledhigh-performance teams, but at the same time will be prepared to roll up their sleeves and get stuckinto the detail (for example, building their own financial/commercial models, writing investor decks,etc.). They will have established productive, successful relationships with Founders/CEOs and willhave a track record of partnering with high-calibre demanding boards and Venture Capital investors.

*   **Operational leadership.**Proven operations leader with experience interfacing with other functional leaders and C-level execs. Will have a commercial and growth mindset. Demonstrated track record of impact on growth initiatives and a clear bias towards data-driven decision making. If not directly owned, will have comfort overseeing the Finance function as well as Operations, HR and Legal and will be able to lend credibility to the fundraising process. Will ideally have experience with international expansion of companies similar to Ably’s current stage/profile.
*   **Growth/scale-up experience.**Understands the complexity and challenges of navigating a business through an earlier phase of lifecycle with limited resources (and subsequently comfortable to wearing different hats, as priorities dictate). *   **Commercial Acumen, data/KPI driven, strong business partnering.**Able to evidence having deployed highly data-driven, ROI-based commercial frameworks in collaboration with Marketing and Sales teams. Will serve as a sounding board to Ably’s other leaders, helping them to develop and operationalise near- and long-term plans. They will possess strong commercial instincts and acumen, continually striving to understand Ably’s growth drivers, new market opportunities, risks, etc.
*   **Business and people leadership.**Strong communicator who can set a clear vision and strategy, and can mobilise teams. Proven track record of building and managing high performing teams and leading them through periods of change and growth. Experience operating at Executive levels of an organisation and collaborating with founders / reporting into CEOs is a plus. Challenges constructively in order to help teams reach the best decisions and outcomes.
*   **Culture alignment.**Will be passionate, tenacious and ambitious, as well as high-integrity, high energy, rigorous, low-ego, collaborative and personable. Will possess as much EQ as IQ. Will be excited by – and passionate about – Ably’s mission, values and journey.

#### **What’s it like to work at Ably?**

Founded by two engineers who spotted a hard and largely unsolved problem, Ably is, at its heart, an engineering company. To provide the infrastructure required for a high-bandwidth, remote-first world we hire engineers with a broad set of technical skills who are ready and capable of taking on challenging realtime engineering problems.

As a company and engineering team we’re committed to a future internet that’s open, with all engineers contributing to [existing open source projects](https://go.ably.io/open).

We want to work in a company of inclusive people who treat each other well. As an Ablyan, you’ll enjoy taking ownership and making an impact, with work that is both varied and challenging. To thrive at Ably, you’ll need to believe in and be prepared to do your work in line with our values, which are:

*   **Open for all.** Open is a mindset. When open, we’re better collaborators, communicators and creators. Whether open source, standards, or protocols, open hearts, minds, and eyes. It’s better when it’s open.
*   **Bias for action.** We avoid analysis paralysis at all costs. We lean into doing in order to move us forward. When we have enough of what we need to act, we act.
*   **Even the best do better.** We want to be the best - but that doesn’t mean we rest when we are. Improvement and growth - true betterment - is an ongoing journey. One fuelled by curiosity and taking risks.
*   **Technology needs humanity.** Our technology means little without people - whether that’s how we treat our colleagues, or how we design developer experiences. Being human matters.
*   **Dependable people make dependable things.** Dependability is integral to our mission and technology, but being dependable starts with our people. When we care enough to take real ownership of our products and problems - all else will follow.
*   **Bold creativity leads to the greatest rewards.** When viewed through a different lense, what stands in the way can become the way. The bigger the challenge the easier the choice: bold creativity will always move us forward, create better outcomes, and set us apart.
