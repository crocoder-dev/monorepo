## Job description

## WHO WE ARE

[DockYard](https://dockyard.com/) is a 100% remote software consultancy that specializes in web applications. We have in-house designers, developers, and client partners, and we are looking for an Employee Experience Manager to join our growing team. You’ll work alongside talented individuals on groundbreaking projects for the world’s largest companies.

## WHAT WE DO

We help ambitious companies transform new ideas into fully deployed web applications. Our clients range from seed funded and early stage startups, who depend on us to define and deliver business-critical workflows, to established enterprise companies seeking fresh perspective and deep expertise on new projects.

The ideal candidate is a champion for diversity and inclusion, is self-driven, team-oriented, passionate about web technologies and industry best practices, and has experience managing engagement and people operations with exceptional results. This role will work closely with the Chief People Officer and be involved in every facet of the Employee Experience!

## WHAT IT’S LIKE TO WORK HERE

We offer a comprehensive benefit plan with a focus on providing an exceptional working environment. Some of these benefits include:

*   100% Remote workforce
*   100% DockYard-paid dental, vision, life & AD&D, STD/LTD starting Day 1
*   100% DockYard-paid medical option available Day 1 (buy-up option also offered)
*   401(k) offered through Vanguard with company match
*   Substantial paid time off

## RESPONSIBILITIES

This full-time, fully remoteposition reports directly to the Chief People Officer.

Recruitment & Onboarding

*   Oversee and facilitate entire recruitment process from sourcing to hire
*   Build out recruitment to maximize efficiencies while ensuring quality and consistency
*   Coordinate with hiring managers and IT to create an exceptional onboarding experience
*   Meet standard measurements of success for staffing, including but not limited to: annual hiring plan, diversity goals, and cost/time to hire standards
*   Provide a world-class experience for every potential candidate
*   Partner closely with hiring managers to craft and deeply understand the organization's needs and priorities

Employee Engagement

*   Support current and future business needs through the development, engagement, motivation and retention of the DockYard team
*   Lead the planning and execution of employee events including retreats
*   Coordinate employee survey feedback, reporting to leadership
*   Partner with DockYard’s Culture Team on projects and provide guidance as needed
*   Oversee the Employee Rewards Program and Company store
*   Work with the Chief People Officer to develop and lead training programs
*   Sponsor the Diversity, Equity & Inclusion group
*   Coordinate with managers and leadership on merit increases, performance management guidance, and providing input on employee relations issues

Compensation & Benefits

*   Own the payroll process including report outs to C-suite and others as needed
*   Partner with the Chief People Officer in benefit plan design and open enrollment
*   Work with the Chief People Officer to evaluate compensation bands and establish merit pools annually

Strategy

*   Works closely with the Chief People Officer to define strategy and departmental goals
*   Formulates and recommends policies, procedures, and programs to enhance the Employee Experience while maintaining regulatory and legal compliance
*   Manage roles and competencies for career development within DockYard
*   Suggest organizational design changes to support the vision and mission of DockYard

Administration

*   Maintain and update employee handbook
*   Complete at least 2 blog posts per year in coordination with Marketing
*   Responsible for regulatory and compliance filings
*   Other duties as assigned

## Job requirements

## MUST HAVES

*   3-5 years proven work experience as an HR Generalist or equivalent
*   2-4 years recruiting experience within the tech or software industry
*   Passionate champion of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion
*   Competence to build and maintain relationships with colleagues at all levels of the organization
*   People oriented and results driven
*   Able to work well independently
*   Demonstrated knowledge of labor laws and regulations
*   Some travel may be required post-COVID

## NICE TO HAVES

*   Kronos payroll experience
*   Prior experience with remote employees and/or within a fully remote company
