## Job Description

As a Senior Software Engineer in Developer Relations at Magic Leap, you will be responsible for maintaining learning paths for a wide range of Magic Leap’s developers and partners creating experiences for Magic Leap. In this role, you will be the primary owner of our developer-facing website portal, which hosts advanced knowledge of the company's current and future technology to enable content developers on the Magic Leap platform. You will ensure continuity of a high quality of up-to-date content.

As a hybrid role, you will also be responsible for supporting our developer community including, but not limited to, participation in online forums and internal slack channels with our Magic Leap ambassadors. This involves back and forth communication to identify issues in platform development, and working to improve our resources to proactively address gaps in documentation & samples.

The ideal candidate has 5-7+ years of experience in interactive real-time immersive (AR / VR / MR)  development and/or the games industry.  In addition, the ideal candidate has experience in web technologies, including but not limited to client-side facing HTML, JavaScript, & CSS. Experience with website management tools is a plus

## Responsibilities

*   Provide documentation for developers building experiences for Magic Leap
*   Maintain an external knowledge base of Magic Leap's current and future technology to understand how they enable content developers.
*   Document discovered solutions into the company knowledge base to foster a culture of leveling-up the developer community
*   Relentlessly advocate for developers’ needs internally to improve our overall platform technology, acting as the voice of the external developer.
*   Develop and recommend improvements to our learning paths.
*   Work hand-in-hand with key stakeholders and Unity team building out SDK, Unity 3D and associated tools documentation
*   Build a deep knowledge of the company's technology/products/services so as to articulate how they enable developers.
*   Collaborate with internal subject matter experts in technology and product design to guide partners’ development efforts.
*   Identify gaps and opportunities for improving our developer resources
*   Understand the Magic Leap software development platform, including being able to reproduce developer issues and help solve them together with internal support teams.
*   Participate in and help to facilitate Magic Leap’s engagement in developer industry events, meetups, hackathons.

## Qualifications

*   5-7+ years of Immersive (AR / VR / MR) development experience using real-time 3D game engines (ideally Unity and/or UE4). Website development experience is a plus.
*   Strong interpersonal skills working with a range of developer experience levels; the ability to develop strong rapport and credibility both externally with developer partners and internally across the organization, resolve conflicts and promote ideas persuasively
*   Excellent problem solving/debugging skills to track down the cause of problems and quickly find solutions
*   Excellent proactive communication skills both written and verbal
*   Able to communicate complex technical issues via email and in-person to small groups of engineers, technical artists/designers, and stakeholders
*   Highly collaborative and able to influence. Should embrace, support and practice a collaborative style which goes beyond team and relationship building. Will achieve results with and through others.
*   Flexibility to work outside your job scope, work as a team and support each other.

## Education

*   *   Bachelor's degree is preferred, or comparable work experience in a related field (web development, technology, interactive entertainment, VR/AR, enterprise application development)
