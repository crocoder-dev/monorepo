**About us**

TeamSnap is a sports and communication platform dedicated to empowering play in youth sports. We empower our people to bring big ideas and tiny egos which lands us on Outside Magazine’s list of **“Best Places to Work”** on the regular.

TeamSnap is looking for a seasoned Director of Talent Acquisition with a history of leading rapidly growing teams to quickly scale out and drive our Talent Acquisition (TA) function. This role will involve building and leading an exceptional team of cross-functional talent acquisition partners, sourcers, coordinators, and specialists. You will own the entire recruiting cycle from employer branding and sourcing to systems and headcount planning. This role will report directly to [Sheila Repeta, Head of People](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sheilarepeta/).

## What you’ll be doing:

*   Create a strategic plan and vision for the TA team to operate at scale, identify exceptional talent and create a remarkable candidate experience.
*   Be responsible for scaling and developing the TA team, culture, and operations through a period of high growth.
*   Collaborate and partner with DE&I teams and Employee Resource groups to build and improve diversity in our hiring practices.
*   Regularly report on TA progress and leverage systems and tools to help facilitate better business decisions and meet our hiring plans.
*   Create a data-driven talent acquisition function that can quantifiably and qualitatively demonstrate our processes are best in class, as well as make informed decisions.
*   Build, manage and oversee our full recruiting cycle that builds a strong pipeline of talent and ensure a fabulous candidate experience.
*   Prepare, review and monitor the talent acquisition budget.
*   Manage agencies, vendors, tools, and technologies that we need for our team.

## Some things that are important:

*   3-5+ years owning and growing talent acquisition functions in high-growth, technology-focused companies.
*   10-12+ years in high growth recruiting environments
*   A history of building diverse teams and organizations
*   Experience running executive-level searches, as well as demonstrated experience with technical and engineering recruiting
*   Exceptional interpersonal skills
*   An entrepreneurial and open mindset
*   A sense of humor. We work hard so we make sure we have fun doing it
*   Comfort providing coaching and feedback to all levels - we mean it when we say trust is a core value

## Other things that would be nice to have:

*   Experience working for a fully remote or distributed organization
*   2nd place llama riding medals, 1st and 3rd will do in a pinch
*   Be a TeamSnap user (sign up for a free trial to see what we’re about)

**Location**

We are headquartered in Boulder, Colorado, but this job is remote (unless you happen to live near Boulder, in which case you’re welcome to come to the office). TeamSnap is a mostly-distributed company, so you must be very comfortable working with people who aren’t in the same physical location as you or each other. While we love all parts of the world, we can only hire permanent US residents at this time.

**Compensation**

We’re committed to equitable compensation for all TeamSnappers. The annual compensation range for this role is $140,000 - $180,000, inclusive of base and bonuses or commissions. This range applies to Colorado-based employees only and may be adjusted based on work experience, skills, certifications, and geographic location.
