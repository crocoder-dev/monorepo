Quidco is a forever changing company whose mission is to empower its users to get the most out of every pound they spend.  Using Quidco, over 10 million members have earned over £800 million worth of cashback on their everyday purchases.  Quidco is committed to providing the best technology to our end users to help them save on any device they are using, and any purchase they make.  We are looking for passionate and dedicated colleagues to join our team to help shoppers save.

## ROLE OVERVIEW:

Work proactively with internal and external parties to generate outstanding offers for Quidco members, and to fully service assigned online and card-linked clients so as to retain relationships and develop their performance.

## PRIMARY DUTIES:

*   Negotiate outstanding exclusive offers from clients for Quidco members, liaising with Marketing and other departments to provide the necessary tools to achieve these.
*   Providing excellent, pro-active account management to our partners.
*   Develop clients and their presence on Quidco to boost their performance.
*   Deepen partner relationships and generate opportunities for co-marketing and coalitions.
*   Build effective relationships with clients, networks and agencies. This will involve attending network events and affiliate days as well as dealing appropriately and promptly with queries received on a daily basis.
*   Generate advertising revenue for the business using on-site email placements and opportunities.
*   Deliver reports and analysis to partners to encourage increasingly better exclusive offers and higher advertising spend.
*   Develop a strategic plan for your accounts portfolio based on data and analysis. Present plans to the wider team and deliver results in an effective manner.
*   Working as part of the Commercial team to help each other achieve shared and individual goals.
*   Take responsibility for helping to prioritise offers throughout the site and mobile app.
*   Taking responsibility for own personal development, in line with agreed annual performance objectives
*   Involvement in project related work and any other additional duties as and when required

## KEY ACCOUNTABILITIES:

*   Provide best in class account management
*   Improve the performance of sector and managed clients
*   Contribute towards team revenue targets
*   Work with the wider Commercial team to complete initiative

*   Employee Recognition Award
