## Description

System Cloud Security Administrator in a SaaS environment for eVisit, a unique Telehealth company and model – patients keep their doctors and doctors keep their patients. Are you an experienced, passionate System Engineer, a roll-up-your-sleeves technologist who wants a daily collaborative environment, think-tank feel and willing to share new ideas with your colleagues – with only limited travel? With 2020 personal distancing constraints eVisit’s volume has grown many fold and is revolutionizing the healthcare industry by simplifying the patient-provider medical visit and connective consultation. We are looking for an engineer who brings basic certifications, creativity and expertise from many areas, including streaming video and real-time communications, information retrieval, security products, Linux administration PC and mobile application deployment and asset management.

eVisit, headquartered in Mesa, AZ, is a telemedicine software company revolutionizing healthcare delivery by creating physician-first tools that fundamentally simplify healthcare. Our HIPAA compliant telemedicine software was built for providers, by medical physicians, to optimize patient flow and boost practice revenue; while providing added convenience and high-quality care to patients, whether at work, home, or on the road

eVisit is among the fastest growing telemedicine software platforms. The company is comprised of a unique team of talented individuals whose experiences are driven from backgrounds in healthcare and software development fields.

As a member of the eVisit Technical Security Operations Team overseeing enterprise security your primary responsibilities will include:

*   Overseeing the configuration management and HIPAA compliance of employee computing devices with employees distributed and working in the Americas
*   Installing, testing and deploying network hardware and software on employee computers
*   Serving as Level 3 workstation and device support for Mac, Windows, Linux and mobile devices
*   Making needed upgrades and repairs to headquarter’s networks and ensuring that systems are operating correctly. eVisit, although, Cloud-based, has a critical, limited headquarter’s infrastructure
*   Identifying, scheduling and implementing patches and upgrades to all staff systems on Mac, Windows and Linux laptops
*   Maintaining network and computer system security
    *   Installing and maintaining the eVisit Identity Management System with agents installed on all PCs and non-cloud systems
    *   Ensuring no unauthorized software is installed
    *   Pushing patches, updates and required software to PCs
    *   Erasing terminated or resigned employee's systems
    *   Retrieving terminated employee systems for scanning, erasing and refreshing to be redistributed
*   Evaluating and optimizing network and system performance
*   Adding users to the appropriate network and cloud assets
*   Assigning and updating security permissions on the network and cloud assets
*   Assigning and tracking licenses for authorized software
*   Training users in the proper use of hardware and software
*   Interpreting and solving problems when a user or an automated monitoring system produces an alert
*   Deploying, supporting, and monitoring the following tools:
    *   Sophos End Point Protection
    *   Sophos Email Security
    *   ESET End Point Protection
    *   1Password
    *   Tenable.io
    *   Google Workspace (GSuite) and Gmail
    *   Office 365
    *   VPN
    *   Network
    *   Palo Alto Prisma
