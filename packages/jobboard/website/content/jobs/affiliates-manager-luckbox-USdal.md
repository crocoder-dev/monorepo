## Job description

Are you looking for an affiliates role in a business that’s a bit different? Do you want to do something you love every day? Looking for a role where you have the autonomy to make a difference and get the credit? Well, why don’t you apply for this role then!?

Joining a lean team of marketing experts, you will have the support you need to develop but also the space required to challenge yourself and grow.

What else is in it for you? You will be working within a globally dynamic and fast-paced environment where esports betting is done right. We are united by the mission to improve the gaming and esports scene by introducing a fully regulated and safe betting product. The group has recently closed CAD $6 million in financing and is expected to grow revenue by 2500% in the next 24 months.

What makes us different from other esports betting start-ups is our approach - we are building a product specifically for the competitive gaming audience in a fully regulated manner. Luckbox are breaking new grounds; with the future of gambling being very much in our capable hands and the world is starting to take note; we were very proud to be the recipient of the EGR 2020 Rising Star award.

This role is based in Bulgaria.

## Role & responsibilities:

*   Define and execute Luckbox affiliates strategy
*   Develop and drive relationships with key affiliate networks and partners
*   Use analytical models to review and optimize affiliate strategy and relationships with partners
*   Own our communication and promotion strategies across all affiliates
*   Work with the leadership to define and exceed ambitious KPIs
*   Develop reward programs to cultivate relationships with the best performing partners
*   Collaborate internally to contribute towards culture of continuous improvement
*   Own and deliver the growing budget for affiliates team
*   Monitor competitors and industry trends with the aim of staying two steps ahead of the rest
*   Determine the ROI spend
*   Previous experience as an affiliate manager
*   Knowledge of the sportsbook industry

## Job requirements

****Essential:****

*   Previous experience as an affiliate manager
*   Knowledge of the sportsbook industry
*   Self-motivated to thrive in a target driven environment
*   Fantastic people skills, able to build strong and commercially sound relationships
*   Enjoy working in a challenging and fun start up environment (there is not masses of structure, but it’s also low on bureaucracy
*   Fluent in English (additional languages would be an advantage)

## Bonus skills:

*   Passion for e-gaming
*   Basic knowledge of SEO and HTML
*   Solid affiliate connections
*   Willingness to travel and attend fairs locally and abroad

Inclusion comes naturally to us, we are obsessive about finding the best talent; regardless of race, gender, age, religion, identity, and experience. We are committed to creating a working environment where our people can be themselves and flourish.
