## Requirements

#### **In this role**

Ably is an engineering company at its heart. Our audience ranges from software engineers to CIOs and it’s our job to make sure the world knows about Ably.

You will take complex information and communicate it clearly, concisely and accurately without relying on technical jargon to explain how developers can quickly use andbenefit from our platform. You will maintain, develop, and improve sites such as [https://github.com/ably/docs](https://github.com/ably/docs) (which produces[https://www.ably.io/documentation/](https://www.ably.io/documentation/)). You will be the key link between our engineers, marketing team, developer advocates and our developer customers.

As a Technical Writer, you will be juggling many documentation tasks, from writing how to guides, toreading and writing example code, to updating FAQs, UX interfaces, API documentation and tutorials, to supporting new featurelaunches.

#### **You will be successful if you:**

*   Like and have a special interest in web technologies, RESTful APIs, coding -- and teaching others about these topics.
*   Enjoy best practice and creating documentation that helps customers to make optimal decision to achieve their requirements.
*   Can analyse documentation usage, support data, and user feedback to continuously improve documentation and inform documentation roadmap.
*   Enjoy writing code, working with APIs, and building prototypes as a polyglot developer.
*   Provide thought leadership to the wider company in regards to how we manage, structure and deliverour documentation to developers.
*   Care about the experience developers have and find it rewarding to positively influence how quickly they can learn concepts and features and ultimately build apps and services more quickly.

#### **The skills needed:**

*   Can maintain, develop, and improve sites such as [https://github.com/ably/docs](https://github.com/ably/docs) (which creates [https://www.ably.io/documentation/](https://www.ably.io/documentation/)). You should be comfortable enough in code to be able to create, edit, and test documentation-related websites.
*   Ability to create prototype implementations (including lightweight API integrations) to test documentation accuracy and effectiveness.
*   Aptitude for organising information and clearly describing complex business workflow, data management, and Internet networking concepts. Strong written skills are necessary.
*   3+ years of experience as a technical writer, preferably documenting cloud-based software products and/or APIs.
*   Proven track record in understanding the challenges of keeping documentation up to date with improvements in fast-changing, in-development code, and how to write enough documentation to be helpful without overloading the reader.
*   Ability to build strong working relationships with the Product, Engineering, and Customer Success teams, who willcontribute to documentation, led by you.
*   Experience producing training and learning materials that help developers understand how to use an API product.
*   You are proficient in Javascript, and ideally one other widely used general-purpose programming language (Javascript, Python, Java, Ruby, etc.).
*   Comfortable with HTML, CSS and Markdown.

#### **Nice to have skills:**

*   Experience managing your own documentation projects.
*   Working knowledge of Swagger, Markdown, Textile and Github or other code repository.
*   Familiarity with web standards, APIs, and other internet technologies.
*   A good understanding of realtime technologies, pub/sub, event-driven architectures or streaming services.
*   Know the SaaS and API business model and have experience documenting solutions for developers.
*   Have a technical background and/or demonstrated fluency in distributed computing systems or other technical concepts relevant to our work

#### **What’s it like to work at Ably?**

Founded by two engineers who spotted a hard and largely unsolved problem, Ably is, at its heart, an engineering company. To provide the infrastructure required for a high-bandwidth, always-connected world, we hire engineers with a broad set of technical skills who are ready and capable of taking on challenging realtime engineering problems.

To provide the infrastructure required for the next generation of the internet, to tell the world about it, and to support everyone inside Ably to do so, we hire people who are ready and capable of taking on challenging problems in a high-growth environment.

We want to work in a company of inclusive people who treat each other well. To really thrive at Ably, you’ll need to believe in and be prepared to do your work in line with what we value, which is:

*   **Even the best do better**. We want to be the best - but that doesn’t mean we rest when we are. Improvement and growth - true betterment - is an ongoing journey. One fuelled by curiosity and taking risks.

*   **Dependable people make dependable things**. Dependability is integral to our mission and technology, but being dependable starts with our people. When we care enough to take real ownership of our products and problems - all else will follow.
