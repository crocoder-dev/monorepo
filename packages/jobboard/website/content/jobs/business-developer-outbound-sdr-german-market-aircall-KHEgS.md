Aircall is on a mission to revolutionize the business phone industry!

We exist to empower every professional to have richer conversations. We provide an entirely cloud-based voice solution, that integrates seamlessly with popular productivity and helpdesk tools that workplaces are already using. We have raised more than $100 Million since 2015, and we care about our growing base of 6000+ customers.

Behind our product are the amazing teams driving it, split between Paris, New York, Sydney, Madrid, and remote locations.

**About the role:**

The ideal SDR is curious and rigorous, looking to rocket-start a sales career.

**This position is based in Berlin.**

The Sales Developer works within the Northern Europe sales team, hand in hand with Account Executives to create business opportunities. This role is crucial in developping the German and spreading the Aircall product vision across new regions.

## Your mission @Aircall:

*   Understand Aircall’s business value and product vision. Understand our customer profiles and their specific challenges and needs
*   Identify relevant sales targets and gather key lead information across the market: verticals, customer segments, current solutions, etc.
*   Run outbound prospecting campaigns (emails, calls) to raise curiosity and interest, and hunt business opportunities
*   Represent Aircall towards the Northern market with integrity and professionalism at all times, and escalate your inputs to the Head of Business Development
*   Travel with the team to all events

## A little more about you:

*   Native in German : you will be emailing, calling, meeting native prospects from the German market
*   Bilingual in English is a big plus
*   A good knowledge of the market is mandatory (experience in the country is truly a plus).
*   Strong communication skills: active listening, empathy, problem-solving attitude
*   Rigor and organization: ability to manage tightly, over time, a large quantity of information
*   Willingness to learn at all time and entrepreneurial spirit to open the Northern market
*   Enthusiastic, positive and a team player. This might be the single most important point, even if it’s the last
