## Job description

We need you to help us reach our Big Hairy Audacious Goal! By building really smart software we enable insurers worldwide to automatically detect insurance fraud and identify high risks. Our ultimate dream is that in 2025 the FRISS Score is the global trusted standard for honest insurance. The need for fraud detection is increasingly important as insurance carriers accelerate their digital initiatives. FRISS is looking for a proactive Tele Salesto accelerate our growth in Colombia and Mexico. Are you based in either of these countries, and do you have the mindset we need to grow our business? Just contact us!

You’ll be setting up qualified leads for our sales team.  With your strong network (preferably within the insurance industry), you are able to discover new opportunities for FRISS. You can show the insurance world that FRISS is THE partner they need to have on board!

Open doors! That’s what you will do. You do this as a trusted advisor, you like to connect the dots to be able to spot new opportunities. How are you going to do this? By:

*   Cold calling to discover warm leads
*   Identifying the rightstakeholders and getting the first meeting for your FRISS sales colleagues
*   Increasing qualified sales pipeline
*   Collaborating and strategizing with the Sales Manager to create a healthy pipeline
*   Doing what's needed to succeed – whether it is gathering market intel, identifying stakeholders, or finding key messages that fit the Colombian and Mexicanmarkets
*   Achieving your targets
*   Don't worry, there's no travelling involved!
