## About the role

Netlify is looking to hire a Staff or Senior Accountant to help build a world-class finance & accounting team! This will be a foundational role that will help the company scale efficiently and set the company's accounting department for success in the future.

Responsibilities include but are not limited to:

*   Ownership of the accounting function (including month-end close, financial statement preparation, AP/AR, tax filings and other activities).
*   Review payroll reconciliations, expense accruals, deferred rent, and other accounts as part of month-end close
*   Work closely with the Accounting Manager and Head of Finance in managing month-end, quarter-end and annual close processes
*   Partner cross-functionally in order to make improvements across all accounting process (automating reconciliations, report preparation, and reviewing accounting transactions)
*   Assist in the administration of financial operations software (Airbase, TripActions, Expensify, QuickBooks, Braintree, etc.)
*   Draft and propose accounting policies and procedures to help scale company

This is a great opportunity for someone who is looking for a role that is both operational, but also strategic as we are constantly thinking about how to shape and set goals for the accounting / finance functions. 

Our Accounting and Finance team is based in San Francisco, for this position we are open to remote candidates within the pacific and mountain time zone for scheduled working hours. 

## What are we looking for

*   B.A. or B.S. in Accounting or in Finance
*   CPA certification preferred
*   Minimum 3+ years of experience, preferably some experience within a Big 4 accounting firm background
*   Mix of both operational and technical accounting knowledge with emphasis on operational day to day accounting.
*   Ability to understand systems and be able to streamline processes
*   Proficiency with systems such as QBO, Expensify, [Bill.com](http://Bill.com), and Excel (intermediate to advanced). Zuora system experience is strongly preferred.
*   Positive attitude and ability to work in a fast-paced, collaborative startup environment
*   Strong attention to detail and ability to research discrepancies and solve problems.
*   Would like this person to be familiar with some of the systems we use (strong preference for candidates who are familiar with Zuora).
*   Thrives in a less structured environment and is able to work well with multiple departments. Startup experience is preferred.
