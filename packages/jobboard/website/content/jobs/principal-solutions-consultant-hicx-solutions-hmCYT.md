As part of our exciting growth plans for 2021 and beyond, HICX is looking to hire an experienced, ambitious, and energetic Principal Solutions Consultant to support our activity in the US market. Last year we trebled revenues and with your help will be looking to do the same again in the coming 12 months.

## Our Company

HICX is the world’s leading solution provider for supplier data and information management software. We enable some of the world's most complex, and innovative, organizations to find, re-use, and maintain supplier data and information across any spreadsheet, app, or system.

HICX is changing the way B2B companies are identified and creating a low code software platform empowering an impressive array of enterprise customers across all industries to transform, automate and modernize, procurement and supplier operations.

Our Mission is to enable businesses to find, maintain, and re-use trusted Supplier Data and Information across their Enterprise, across any spreadsheet, app, or system.

## Responsibilities

\- Assisting prospects in developing the business case for acquiring HICX's products and

also to identify and understand the business issues that our solution can affect.

\- Explaining the benefit and value of adopting HICX's products including, but not limited to, related to architecture, features, technical and business benefits, to prospects through presentations, detailed discussions, value engineering/ROI workshops, demos, and other proof points in order to reinforce the value proposition.

\- Manage all activities as part of the technical sales cycle.

\- Prepare, configure and deliver compelling demonstrations and proof of concepts with the HICX software platform, highlighting areas of greatest value and ROI based on specific customer requirements and needs, to convert prospects to customers. Moreover, you'll draw on your domain expertise and practical experience to become a trusted advisor to the prospect's technical and business teams.

\- Create and maintain a library of best-practice demonstration content that highlights how HICX solves specific challenges by industry.

\- Proactively craft and propose solutions that clarify how the business brings value to address critical business challenges faced by prospects.

\- Understand the competitive landscape and how to best position our solution against the competition.

\- Understand and communicate how HICX has delivered value to existing customers and its applicability to prospects.

\- Prepare proposals and manage and respond to RFx's

\- Prepare, manage and run SoW Workshops

\- Prepare and manage SOWs

\- Prepare and manage Pre-Sales collateral templates such as resource plans and solution proposals

\- Support marketing on trade-shows, webinars, and blog postings to bring better awareness to HICX and the value we bring to clients

\- Travel as needed to support sales cycles.

About you

*   At least five years Pre Sales experience in delivering complex enterprise solutions to Fortune 1000 clients and supporting clients through their buying journey.
*   Proven experience within Supplier Master Data or ProcureTech solutions is preferred but expertise in other Enterprise SaaS solutions aimed at improving Enterprise governance, control, and efficiency across finance, shared services, supply chain or procurement will be considered.
*   Solution-oriented – the ability to leverage HICX products to address the business challenge and provide a complete business case solution.
*   Hands-on experience with solution configuration and the ability to set up and manage your own demo environment is essential. Coding skills (such as Java, .net) and query language (such as SQL) desirable.
*   Experience with, or a knowledge of, integration-specific technologies.
*   Experience working for both large and small SaaS vendors.
*   Excellent discovery, communication, and presentation skills.
*   High levels of intellectual curiosity, conscientiousness, and a growth mindset.
*   Willingness to travel to clients as needed.
*   Native-level English language skills
