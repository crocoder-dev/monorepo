**About us**

TeamSnap is a sports and communication platform dedicated to empowering play in youth sports. We value trust, communication, and fun more than big company policies. We empower our people to bring big ideas and tiny egos which lands us on Outside Magazine’s list of **“Best Places to Work”** on the regular.

TeamSnap is seeking a highly motivated, analytically minded, and creative **Senior Manager of Growth Marketing** to efficiently grow our consumer business. This role will directly report to the Head of Members with cross-functional stakeholders including design, product, finance, and analytics.

**What you’ll be doing**

As a versatile and analytical **Senior Manager of Growth Marketing**, you will help drive the efficient growth of TeamSnap’s independent teams and members. You will be responsible for identifying growth opportunities, deploying campaigns, and optimizing performance across all digital marketing channels including search, social, and SEO. You will consistently measure, analyze and report on performance to ensure we are tracking toward a positive ROI, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve channel performance and increase conversion. In addition, you will explore new channels and test creative opportunities for growth. You will also collaborate on projects with cross-functional teammates including B2B marketers to ensure strategies and campaigns are aligned.

## Some things that are important:

*   Experience building digital marketing excellence in at least two consumer businesses 
*   Experience creating acquisition strategies, executing campaigns and measuring performance for display advertising, referral marketing, remarketing, paid social, and more
*   Deep understanding of all the TLA performance marketing metrics including LTV / CAC
*   Experience launching and optimizing SEO and content marketing campaigns
*   Deep analytical skill set to distill insights, optimize and execute campaigns, and analyze A/B tests and incrementality studies
*   Ability to think strategically and develop comprehensive, data-driven marketing plans In-depth experience with at least two paid channels
*   Obsessed with growth, building, and optimizing things
*   Highly curious and technically minded
*   Flexibility, teamwork, sense of humor, and the willingness to do what's necessary to help a relatively small company grow quickly

## Other things that would be nice to have:

*   Experience with SQL or using BI tools like Looker, Tableau, or Mode Analytics as well as with Google Analytics
*   Deep understanding of virality in consumer software
*   Exposure to full lifecycle and retention marketing
*   Be a parent, coach or participant in a recreational sport
*   Be a TeamSnap user (sign up for a free trial to see what we’re about
*   Ability to talk to animals, Doctor Doolittle-style

**Location**

We are headquartered in Boulder, Colorado, but this job is remote (unless you happen to live near Boulder, in which case you’re welcome to come to the office). TeamSnap is a mostly-distributed company, so you must be very comfortable working with people who aren’t in the same physical location as you or each other. While we love all parts of the world, we can only hire permanent US residents.
