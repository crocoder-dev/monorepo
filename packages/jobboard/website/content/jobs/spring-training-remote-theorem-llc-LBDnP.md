Theorem is looking for a motivated craftsperson to join our global team of 200+ professionals to support our growing distributed services business. We are on a mission to dismantle the traditional consulting ecosystem and replace it with an effective framework for innovation that transforms the way businesses think about and solve problems from the inside out.

**THINK SPRING!! Spring Training with Theorem, that is!**

Theorem is an enterprise innovation consultancy that focuses on solving big problems. We are a fully distributed team of 200+ craftspeople and growing. 

Theorem is currently BETA testing a new training program called Spring Training; we are looking for participants to join **with a 100% scholarship.** In the program, you will focus on learning how to work on a high-performing distributed team effectively**. Spring Training is not a boot camp**; it is a way for skilled practitioners to focus on and improve their team communication and collaboration skills, learning how to work remote first. 

In Spring Training, you will work with a diverse team of skilled practitioners to solve a real-world problem. However, all situations in the program are fictitious; none of your contributions will be used by Theorem LLC for any purpose other than training.

**OVERVIEW**

A 4-week program of 4-6 participants requiring approximately 15 hours per week, mostly asynchronous.  We expect that participants will have full-time jobs and personal commitments.  Spring Training is designed to be fully flexible to meet your packed schedule.  It is expected that participants spend only 1 hour a day in synchronous communication with the team

We will be using C# .net Core and React as core technologies in the project. 

Participants will experience how high performing teams work, take on new technology opportunities to grow their skills, and test themselves by working alongside other high performing craftspeople in a fully remote environment. 

Participants in Spring training are assigned a coach for 1:1 sessions and asynchronous work reviews; their contributions are also watched by Theorem hiring managers.  Participants who excel in spring training may be asked to join Theorem as a full-time team member.

**WHO YOU ARE**

You are a passionate technologist with 3-5 years of professional experience, who has the discipline to create and finish projects. You have opinions about your favorite open source projects, or perhaps you have contributed to some or started one of your own. You are a communicator. Whether you are raising the flag within an implementation opinion or sharing your favorite playlist or puppy pictures in Slack, you will not shy away from letting your voice be heard.  

## QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS

*   Previous professional development experience, witnessing code in production in various environments, both large enterprise web and small.
*   **C# .net Core and React a Plus**
*   Previous experience working within a modern development environment using Docker containers.
*   Previous experience collaborating in a quality-focused environment where testing and reviewing is a part of the everyday process.
*   A passing score on the C# or React entry assessment.
*   Excellent communication skills
*   Experience working within an Agile/Scrum environment.
*   Possess a strong and reliable internet connection.
*   Align a portion of your day with the business hours of Eastern Time Zone (UTC -5)
