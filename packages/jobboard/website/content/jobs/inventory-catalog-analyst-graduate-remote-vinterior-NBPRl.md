## Description

**Who is Vinterior?**

We are a fast-growing UK- based marketplace who sells Vintage furniture and Antiques from dealers all around Europe.

We help professionals and consumers to find products that match the values that matter to us: character, a passion for craftsmanship and an obsession for delivering exceptional customer service.

We’re only four years old, but we’re already disrupting the furniture industry: since starting, our sales have increased a hundredfold. We are a passionate team who want to offer sustainable furniture and share the love of unique antiques and vintage pieces!

## The role

Our focus is to partner with the very best furniture dealers and retailers across the UK and Europe, to offer Vinterior customers an unrivalled selection of products and the most professional service possible.

As our inventory grows, we must ensure that the range of products available to our customers is carefully curated and that the most popular and exciting items are showcased across the platform.

To date, our sellers offer on Vinterior an inventory of 180,000 items. It is both our biggest strength, but also where lies the most significant room for improvement to make the items more searchable and discoverable.

You will be part of the Supply Team that is currently composed of one business developer, one business analyst, one account manager, and one catalog specialist.

You will be in charge of our inventory accuracy, that means you will have to carefully check and ensure that all product pages have all the relevant information. You will manage assortment, categorisation, and curation. As you will be working in Excel (or similar) most of the time, you need to be comfortable with large dataset and it will be essential that you know how to use formulas (v-lookups, pivot tables, index/match).

You will be responsible of:

Inventory management:

*   Ensuring that the categorisation of our inventory is perfect so our customers can find what they are looking for
*   Cleaning the attributes of our product pages like designers, brand, dimensions, etc. to improve the filtering experience of our users when they browse on our platform
*   Ensuring that the product pages created on our platform follow our guidelines and that all the information is at the right place in the right field
*   Tracking and reporting on the 'freshness' of our inventory and implementing actions to solve accordingly

Research and analysis:

*   Analysing search trends, our inventory and identifying opportunities to create new categories and fields
*   Running competitor analysis and internal research to improve our listings and fields

Cross-functional attributions:

*   Managing a team of freelancers
*   Be the point of contact to the Seller support team about all questions with regards to seller's listings and inventory
*   Working closely with the Merchandising team to ensure that the products we push in our top pages are accurate and available
