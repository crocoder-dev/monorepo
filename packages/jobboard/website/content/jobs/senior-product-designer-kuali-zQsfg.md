## Description

Kuali is looking for a Senior Product Designer with at least four years of experience working in software product development. We hire humble, optimistic design leaders who are naturally motivated to solve challenging customer problems. This position is on our Research, Conflict of Interest product team.

Who are we?

Kuali builds software solutions for higher education. We help our customers — colleges & universities — focus on providing a fantastic education to students by decreasing their administrative costs. We work in a competitive space, ripe for innovation, with users ready to be delighted.

Our Culture

As a company, we are guided by our cultural values:

*   Iterate to evolve
*   Cultivate openness
*   Act with accountability
*   Assume the best
*   Practice humility
*   Deliver amazing experiences

We work remotely, and have for years. Distributed work is in our bones, with a history of

institutions working across state lines for many years. Our employees each work in the

environment where theyʼre happiest, from Massachusetts to Hawaii. We work consciously to create a collaborative and healthy remote work culture, and we travel to meet in person a few times each year.

Everyone should love their work.

Kuali has been voted a [top place to work](https://topworkplaces.com/publication/saltlake/kuali/) for 4 years in a row by the Salt Lake Tribune. We also made [Forbes' list of America's Best Startup Employers](https://www.forbes.com/americas-best-startup-employers/#1100e1e65275) for 2020. Not too shabby.

## Requirements

WHAT YOU'LL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR

*   You enjoy building and delivering beautiful user experiences.
*   You can balance competing priorities. You know when to compromise on pixel perfection to deliver value faster.
*   You’re versatile. You know when to dream big and when to act small. You’re equally comfortable in both green field and refactoring projects.
*   You’re a collaborator. You build great relationships with customers and other disciplines within the company.
*   You’re inspired to achieve results, and your enthusiasm is contagious.
*   You’re passionate about your role in representing the voice of our customers and you advocate for them fiercely.
*   You’re curious and continuously seek knowledge and share your discoveries with others.
*   You know how to influence and serve.
*   You understand the complexity of large enterprise applications.
*   You’ve used agile methodologies.

*   Lead design for new features and maintenance of a foundational software product within our higher education product suite.
*   Build strong relationships with customers to develop a deep understanding of their needs, challenges, and frustrations in order to distill the key problems they are facing.
*   Advocate passionately for the prioritization of customer needs throughout the product development process.
*   Lead user research within the product team
*   Create wireframes, models, and prototypes to materialize and test concepts with stakeholders.
*   Support engineering development of design and bugs
*   Advocate for and implement accessible design practices
*   Create elegant and beautiful responsive web UIs
*   Design with the Kuali design system
*   Consider every touchpoint with the customer and discover solutions to make their end-to-end experience fluid and efficient.

Portfolio

Be prepared to provide examples of your work. While we’re not concerned with how fancy your website is, we will need to see 3-4 examples of professional design work with commentary on the process, insights and rationale that lead you to the chosen deliverable. Something as simple as an email or slide deck is sufficient.

Position Requirements

*   Expert skills with graphic design, illustration, and prototyping tools
*   Proven experience in successful UX and UI design for multiple web applications
*   Expert knowledge of user-centered design and accessibility principles
*   Excellent collaboration skills
*   Thorough communication skills
*   Experience in designing responsive web applications
*   Experience running usability tests and writing up and sharing results
*   You’ve shipped Software as a Service (SaaS) products
