Over a million designers use Sketch to transform their ideas into incredible products. Want to join us and help take this leading design product to the next level? We're looking to expand our team with a full-time **Design System Manager**.

**What you'll do**

You'll join our incredibly talented team of designers where you'll have the opportunity to directly impact on Sketch's design language foundations throughout its entire lifecycle — ideation, discovery, planning, execution, and delivery — while coordinating and collaborating with the design and product teammates.

It's expected you:

Take ownership of our Design System

Help to improve and maintain it

Develop concepts for new experiences and functionalities

Follow the internal procedures and keep accurate documentation of your interactions

Become a key point of contact between design and product teams

**What we're looking for**

We're looking for an experienced and talented Design System Manager who will display these core qualities:

You’re proactive and capable of strategically thinking about product design and development at a holistic level, considering how its constituent parts relate to and complement one another, and the role a Design System plays in the broader picture

You have a solid understanding of the principles, capabilities, and constraints of product development. You don’t _need_ to know how to code, but you must be familiar with web and macOS guidelines and standards

You champion the importance of a well-structured Design System and how it can help lead to a frictionless handoff between designers and developers

## Essentials

*   4 + years in product design or comparable role
*   We value every year of experience in your field, but the experience isn't the be-all and end-all. The ideal candidate has the enthusiasm and the knowledge to create high-quality content and be excellent at organizing. We'd love for you to learn from us, just like we'd love to be able to learn from you.
*   HTML/CSS/JS knowledge
*   You have a passion for details, user experience and consistency
*   You use Sketch and understand our product inside out
*   Solid knowledge of design fundamentals, methodologies and techniques
*   A strong design and digital asset portfolio showing recent examples of your work
*   Ability to pitch ideas and concepts to improve an ever-evolving Design System
*   You have excellent communication skills, are self-motivated, and comfortable working in a remote position
*   You’re able to work full-time and are based in European / African timezones.

## Bonus Points

*   You have a BA in design-related field
*   You’re familiar with the key tools we use — Slack, Notion and GitHub.
*   You're familiar with tools like Storybook and ZeroHeight.

**Even if you're not able to tick all of these boxes**, we would still love to hear from you.
