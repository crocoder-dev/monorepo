## Job description

**Who you’ll report to:** Operations Director (Content)

**Location:** U.K., Remote (MUST BE U.K. BASED)

**Experience:** Mid-Senior

BibliU is a digital learning platform that is changing the future of the multi-billion-dollar textbook industry. Our mission is to shake up higher education and make reading materials more accessible and learning more effective. We do this by making textbooks and monographs more affordable for students, so that in most cases, they are included in tuition costs. We have incorporated accessibility features to accommodate multiple learning styles and the different needs and preferences of each student.

BibliU has grown rapidly, and during this time we have created Europe's largest digital education content programme, in partnership with Coventry University. Upon entering the US market, we have secured the largest digital textbook programmes in the US with Phoenix and Grand Canyon Universities.

We are working with over 60% of universities in the United Kingdom, and are steadily growing in the US . We have recently raised $10 million in a Series A investment round (you can read more about this on [Sky News](https://news.sky.com/story/innovation-agency-nesta-backs-uk-edtech-start-up-bibliu-11974089) and [EdSurge](https://www.edsurge.com/news/2020-04-17-bibliu-raises-10-million-to-scale-online-textbooks)) and have ambitious plans for continued growth and expansion.

Our team of 50 +, of which half the senior management team are women, are seeking a pricing analyst to join our Content Operations team.

## Content Operations Team at BibliU

The Content Operations team works to deliver operational improvements to content workflows, from file delivery and metadata, through to delivering deal specifics to the finance team.

## Role Description:

*   Prepares pricing and file requests from other teams and handles publisher title requests
*   Takes a lead role in working with technical representatives at newly onboarded publishers
*   Works with institutions directly to provision content for key accounts
*   Queries and manipulates databases to provide pricing information for internal teams
*   Develops and improves existing processes involving multiple stakeholders across different teams

## Job requirements

## What we’re looking for

*   You enjoy looking at processes and finding procedural solutions
    
*   You are a whizz at Excel/Google Sheets
    
*   You are able to prioritise effectively
    
*   You can collaborate with multiple stakeholders and teams
    
*   You are a confident and professional communicator
    
*   You have experience querying SQL databases
    
*   You have experience integrating with third-party tooling and/or technical teams
    
*   You are able to work independently and take responsibility for project-based work
    

## Areas of responsibility

*   Manually preparing pricing requests and handling publisher title requests
*   Working with technical analysts within the Content Operations team to establish improvements
*   Responding to publishers and attending publisher calls along with our Publisher Partnerships representatives
*   Working with Publisher Partnerships representatives to handle onboarding of new publishers
*   Handling the in-flow of new titles and guiding publishers on better procedures
*   Taking responsibility for the content provision of key accounts
