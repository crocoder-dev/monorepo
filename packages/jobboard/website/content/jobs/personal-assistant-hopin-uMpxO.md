## The role

We are looking for an experienced Personal Assistant to support our CEO. You will provide a mix of personal and business support at a fast-paced technology scale-up. The ideal candidate will be a fluent multilinguist (English/Spanish and Catalan), tech savvy and will be a motivated self-starter who is results-driven, highly organized, detail focused, and has a high level of integrity.

You will manage an assortment of tasks such as getting documents notarized, mailing packages, assisting with translation (into English) and other organisational tasks. To do this role properly you should be very comfortable using Google suite, Slack, and other new technologies and tools. You should also be extremely fast at solving problems and have experience as an administrative assistant in a fast-paced startup.

## What will you do?

*   Carry out a variety of personal requests and errands
*   Schedule personal appointments and arrange related travel
*   Support search for short term apartment rentals, assist with apartment moves and arrange transport & cleaning of apartments as needed 
*   Occasionally walk, feed and care for a small dog – must love dogs
*   Manage international deliveries (this can include calls with customs to submit documents, manage delivery across multiple addresses and countries, etc)
*   Schedule deliveries and implement a consistent routine (e.g. delivery of select groceries three times a week, or to reorder supplements every month...etc.)
*   Schedule doctor appointments, follow up on reports, etc as needed
*   Can physically pick things up and drop things off (e.g. letter from the bank...etc.)
*   Perform general office duties including managing the CEO’s personal calendar 
*   Join calls or attend in-person appointments if translation (into English) is required
*   Other ad hoc duties

How will you get it done?

*   Must be able to meet deadlines in a fast-paced quickly changing environment.
*   A proactive approach to problem-solving with strong decision-making skills.
*   Professional level verbal and written communications skills.
*   Thrive in a fast-paced, high-performance culture
*   Anticipate needs and work proactively to create lift for the team
*   In-depth understanding of Google suite, Slack, Whatsapp, etc.
*   Exhibit strong communication, decision making, time management, organizational, and problem-solving skills
*   Have a strong professional presence and communicate successfully with vendors
*   Multitask and prioritize your daily workload
*   Maintain a high level of trust and confidence by protecting confidential information

## Are you a fit?

*   Must speak fluent Spanish and English
*   Have at least one year of experience as a personal assistant (highly preferred) or in an operations role
*   Have a valid Driver's License and car, and complete our background check requirements
*   Tech savvy and comfortable using new services like Glovo for fast delivery (as opposed to traditional couriers), use Whatsapp for communication, etc.
*   Able to meet in person when needed, not always
*   Super motivated, can-do attitude, problem solver.
*   Able to demonstrate all of the competencies listed above under How will you get it done?
