## Job description

**Location**: Amsterdam Surrounds (Remote)

**Duration**: 6+ months minimum

**Start Date**: January 4, 2021

Our client is a global retail organisation headquartered in the Netherlands. We are looking for a strong IGA Functional/Technical Consultant that can be the bridge between regional business and development teams. We are looking for someone with excellent stakeholder management and communication skills. For someone withthe ability to set up and drive good processes in dynamic environments. If you are up for a challenging and interesting role, this is for you!

As IGA functional/technical consultant you will assist with connecting the regional business and development teams for the IGA implementation based on the selectedIGA product (Saviynt or Sailpoint). You will be the champion on the regional requirements and product capabilities and align these with the global templates and processes as much as possible. You will work closely with development or delivery team to convert functional requirements to technical workflows and make sure requirements are delivered without compromising the set standards and process.

## Key Responsibilities

*   Conducting research, surveys and interviews to gain understanding of the business and processes
*   Gathering functional requirements for the business
*   Developing user stories
*   Conducting process mapping
*   Detecting issues and investigating ways to resolve them
*   Assessing the pros and cons of possible strategies
*   Making recommendations for improvement
*   Supporting the implementation ofagreed solutions
*   Developing and implementing new procedures or training
