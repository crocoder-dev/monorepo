**About Us**

TeamSnap is a sports and communication platform dedicated to empowering play in youth sports. We value trust, communication, and fun more than big company policies. We empower our people to bring big ideas and tiny egos which lands us on Outside Magazine’s list of **“Best Places to Work”** on the regular.

TeamSnap is seeking a Senior Back-end Software Engineer/Developer to join our fully distributed engineering team and help us continue our impressive growth from 23 million customers to beyond! Our back-end stack includes **Ruby/Rails and Elixir.**

Join a literally **award-winning team**. We released a first-in-industry health check screening for teams to help ensure members are healthy before competition of any kind. This led our team to win the [2021 Big Innovation Award](https://www.bintelligence.com/blog/2021/1/12/7-executives-52-companies-and-137-products-win-2021-big-innovation-award) presented by the Business Intelligence Group!

To deepen our connections with each other, we also love to travel to fun locations across the country for all-company gatherings, team meetings, and the like.

## What you’ll be doing:

*   Building new communication features for our users. We send 100 million+ messages a year and we’re architecting the future of communication for TeamSnap users.
*   Building features to foster engagement within teams - think photos, video streaming and ways to connect our users in more meaningful ways.
*   Defining, designing and implementing custom sponsored experiences that are a win-win for our customers and brands.
*   Optimizing activity workflows like rostering, scheduling, etc. to streamline our customers experiences.
*   Integrating our products and features into a unified product and system architecture.
*   Creating scalable and performant APIs and services to power our web and mobile apps.
*   Working collaboratively with the team to design services and systems to support product features and our growth.

## What’ll set you up for success:

*   Experience leading projects for web apps and services at scale.
*   Exposure and interest in backend development. You have deep experience building experiences with Ruby. You have experience or interest in building experiences with Elixir.
*   Ability to debug and fix complex problems that may span a number of systems.
*   Experience with direct usage of SQL and relational databases such as MySQL along with migrations, profiling, and optimization of such databases.
*   Excitement for building great solutions and user experience.
*   A sense of humor… or at least sympathy-laugh at our bad jokes.

## Bonus points:

*   Experience working with functional programming concepts.
*   Ad Tech experience delivering and creating great user experiences.
*   Experience with frontend technologies (we use React and Typescript heavily)Experience with automated testing, CI/CD, and release management.
*   Experience with Docker-based development environments and Kubernetes-based production environments.
*   Ability to talk to animals, Doctor Dolittle-style.

**Location**

We are headquartered in Boulder, Colorado, but this job is remote (unless you happen to live near Boulder, in which case you’re welcome to come to the office). TeamSnap is a mostly-distributed company, so you must be very comfortable working with people who aren’t in the same physical location as you or each other. While we love all parts of the world, we can only hire permanent US residents at this time.
