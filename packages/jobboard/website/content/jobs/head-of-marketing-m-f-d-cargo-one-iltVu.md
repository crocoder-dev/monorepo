**Join us in changing an entire industry**

We need you to help us shape the future of air freight! At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) we believe that we can only win as a team, and we want you to make an impact with us!

The opportunity

As the Head of Marketing you will help us building a powerful B2B brand in the air cargo space, that delights customers and partners on every step of the journey.  You will define and execute a data driven strategy in order to share our vision across the industry and beyond to fuel our global growth. We’re looking for a highly skilled marketeer, familiar with the specific challenges of two-sided marketplaces in the B2B space, who builds up and leads our marketing function and significantly contributes to our ambitious growth objectives. Roll up your sleeves and join the team on the ground floor, by setting strategic priorities, establishing best practices in positioning, creating content, driving sales enablement, and leveraging data to optimize performance. This is a leadership role that requires you to be hands-on and passionate about execution. 

**⚠️This position is remote.** The caveat is that you must be based within certain countries to be able to work with us. **F****ind out which countries qualify [here](https://www.cargo.one/remote-working).**

## What you’ll be doing:

*   Develop our overall marketing vision and execute on effective strategies to drive growth on the supply- (airlines) as well as demand-side (freight forwarders) 
*   Build, lead and mentor the growing marketing organization, set up processes, prioritize and balance requests from different internal and external parties according to our overall company objectives
*   Coordinate all marketing activities across different channels and be part of the execution 
*   Develop our tone of voice and ensure our brand message is strong and consistent across all channels (such as events, email campaigns, web pages or sales enablement collateral)
*   Build and leverage data to understand how customers are using [cargo.one](http://cargo.one) and to monitor and optimize the performance of our activities.
*   Prepare and manage monthly, quarterly and annual budgets for the Marketing team
*   Set, monitor and report on short-term and long-term team OKRs in alignment with our overall business strategy

## What you’ll need:

*   You have multiple years of experience in building and leading successful marketing functions in two-sided marketplace B2B companies and know how to scale fast 
*   You have a board range of marketing skills with a focus in brand-building and content creation.
*   Experience in the fields of Account-Based Marketing and Demand Gen is an extra bonus for this role. 
*   You have a track record of successful creative campaigns, backed up by measurable ROI
*   You possess strong leadership and project management skills to manage multiple team members and projects at once with ease
*   You are a strong communicator with the ability to build relationships and drive collaboration within and cross-functional teams
*   You are highly data-driven with strong numerical skills, the ability to analyse and optimise our marketing activities
*   You are a pro with project management software (ideally Jira), CRM, automation tools (ideally Hubspot) and data analysis tools to set up efficient processes and orchestrate our activities
*   You have a strong desire to serve the team and a willingness to get your hands dirty!

## What you can expect from cargo.one? We think you will enjoy working at cargo.one, here are just a few of the things that we think make it a great place to work:

*   Work within a virtual-first setup in a team that puts high emphasis on creating a great company culture
*   The opportunity to have a serious impact on a growing organization that’s changing an entire industry
*   An extremely steep learning curve, working alongside an ambitious and international team
*   MacBook or ThinkPad, the choice is yours – whatever helps you get your job done, we’ve got you covered.
*   If there’s something important to you that’s not on this list, let us know.
