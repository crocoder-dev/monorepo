## Keyrock is expanding!

Keyrock was founded in 2017 and has quickly grown from 3 to over 30 people today. As an industry recognized liquidity provider and market maker, Keyrock is a leading European creator of algorithmic trading technology in the digital asset space.

With VC backing and a constantly expanding client base, Keyrock is one of Belgium’s fastest-growing fintech companies, offering a service used around the world.We are looking for:

We’re now looking to add a Quantitative Trading Intern to our team to assist our traders in monitoring and supervising operations related to all trading activities, flows of assets, treasury and risk management. You will receive hands-on training from senior members of our trading team and learn how to manage, supervise and track all Keyrock’s trading algorithms. You will gain a thorough understanding of Keyrock’s activities and of the global digital asset market more broadly.

The internship is paid and the projected start date is April 2021.

## NB this position can be remote or based in our Brussels office, but candidates must be in a time zone within 2 hours of Brussels in either direction.

## What you will be doing

*   Supporting the deployment of new algorithms, clients and markets
    
*   Keeping a global updated view of all algorithmic trading operations
    
*   Financial monitoring of alerts
    
*   Assisting with active trading
    
*   Assisting with trade execution
    
*   Preventive actions by spread adaptations
    
*   Helping to keep track of the treasury
    
*   Helping to keep track of and report technical and financial problems and provide feedback to the dev team for improvement.
    

## Skills and experience

*   Able to act and to take decisions under pressure
    
*   Digital native
    
*   Understanding of/interest in digital assets and blockchain technologies
    
*   Understanding of/interest in financial market structure
    
*   Strong attention to detail
    
*   Good communicator
    
*   The ability to take ownership of a project while collaborating on it with colleagues
    
*   A passion for Blockchain/DLT and digital assets
    
*   A passion for financial markets and asset management in general
    
*   Proficient English speaking
    

Previous experience in trading (stocks, equities, cryptocurrencies etc.) is an asset (this doesn’t have to be professional experience - we’re also interested in talking to people who currently trade as a hobby).

That means that we expect to work with people who not only have the right skills, but who have also made the conscious decision to look for a company in our industry and with our characteristics.

Our offer

*   A 6 month Convention d’Immersion Professionnelle, with the possibility to transition to a permanent contract afterwards
    
*   Complete autonomy in your time management thanks to flexible working hours and the opportunity to work remotely one to two days per week
    
*   A ton of fun in a thriving and multicultural team that enjoys regular teambuilding activities
