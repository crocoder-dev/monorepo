*   Always interesting tasks and strong challenges.

## Job requirements

##### **Requirements:**

*   Live in Latin America (it works best with our timezone)
*   3+ years of work experience in companies/startups as developer (not small projects/school/bootcamp)
*   Understanding networking (http, tcp etc., as well as async io)
*   Experience with frontend frameworks/libs (React or Angular would be awesome, but if you have solid experiencein other libs/frameworks we are happy to help you learn)
*   Server Side Development experience.
*   Frontend and backend performance optimizations
*   Positive attitude :)
