## The Role

Hopin is looking for an experienced Digital Marketing Manager to join our fast-growing Marketing team, reporting to the VP Revenue Marketing. As Digital Marketing Manager, you will own Hopin’s organic and paid strategy and overall contribution to the sales pipeline. You will directly manage Hopin’s technical SEO and SEM vendor relationships and track, analyze, determine strategy, and execute Hopin’s monthly media spend to achieve top line metrics.

## Responsibilities

*   Own our SEO and SEM/PPC vendor strategies, relationships, implementation, and budgets
*   Execute tests, collect and analyze data,  identify trends and insights
*   Achieve maximum ROI in paid search campaigns using analysis of data
*   Track, report, and analyze website analytics
*   Track, report, and analyze PPC initiatives and campaigns
*   Optimize copy and landing pages for paid search engine marketing campaigns
*   Continually expand and optimize paid keyword discovery
*   Research and analyze competitor advertising links
*   Work with content and product teams to optimize Hopin’s website for search engine
*   Work cross-functionally to plan and execute on marketing campaigns to drive pipeline
*   Measure the performance of campaigns and events
*   Manage vendor relationships to ensure alignment with achieving topline funnel metrics
*   Execute tests, collect and analyze data and results, identify trends and insights in order to achieve maximum ROI in paid search campaigns
*   Track, report, and analyze website analytics and PPC initiatives and campaigns
*   Manage campaign expenses, staying on budget, estimating monthly costs and reconciling discrepancies.
*   Optimize copy and landing pages for search engine marketing
*   Perform ongoing keyword discovery, expansion and optimization
*   Research and implement search engine optimization recommendations
*   Research and analyze competitor advertising links
*   Develop and implement link building strategy
*   Work with the development team to ensure SEO best practices are properly implemented on newly developed code
*   Work with editorial and marketing teams to drive SEO in content creation and content programming
*   Recommend changes to website architecture, content, linking and other factors to improve SEO positions for target keywords.
