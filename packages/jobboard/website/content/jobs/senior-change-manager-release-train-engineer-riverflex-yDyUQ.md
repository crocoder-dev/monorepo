## Job description

**Location**: Remote (ideally based in the Netherlands)

**Duration**: March - November

**Location:**The Netherlands (remote during COVID)

Our client is a retail bank based in the Netherlandsand is looking for a senior change manager/release train engineer who is able to manage changes from a brand perspective. You will be involved in promoting the interests of marketing and brand organization and facilitate the implementation of a multi-brand policy. You will prioritize changes that make the greatest contribution to the company's strategic goals - this includes mapping dependencies and managing the backlog. Additionally, you will be involved in improving the Portfolio Management and the movement towards an Agile organization and an agile / scrum / SAFe way of working.

If you are a change manager with extensive knowledge of brand organizations, disruption channels, products and services, and cultural change this may be the role for you!

## Responsibilities:

*   Managing change initiatives, ensuring progress, creating cohesion, and achieving strategic objectives
*   Manage changes from a multi-brand perspective in a careful, efficient and result-oriented manner
*   Working in a multidisciplinary manner and will be liaising between brands, capabilities, IT and other stakeholders
*   Continuously informed and engaged on the latest methods and developments within your field
*   Support the Information Manager to improve Portfolio Management, in which the change of building a multi-brand and phasing out the brand-specific change will be important

## Job requirements

**Essential qualifications/skills/competencies:**

*   At least 5 years of relevant working experience as a product owner or project/change manager
*   Knowledge of and experience with agile project management, SAFe methodologies, and PMW-pro
*   Knowledge of and experience with change and portfolio management in complex multi-brand organizations
*   Experience in managing implementations of large and complex change processes, preferably in an Agile environment
*   Excellent expectation and stakeholder management skills
*   Experience with managing and developing self-steering multidisciplinary teams
*   Knowledge of distribution models
*   Experience in the financial industry (preferably banking)
*   Dutch Native speaker
*   A completed Bachelor's or Master's degree
