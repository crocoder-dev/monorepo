**Join us in changing an entire industry**

We need you to help us shape the future of air freight! At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) we believe that we can only win as a team, and we want you to make an impact with us!

_We are a remote-first company and can work with you if you are based in one of the following_ [_countries_](https://www.cargo.one/remote-working) _(more countries are becoming available soon!)_

**🏆** **What’s the opportunity?**

“Came for the product, stayed for the customer support” – that’s what our customers often tell us.

At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) our goal is to offer the best air cargo booking experience. We do that through a great product and great service to our customers and partners. Our customers, freight forwarders, are also B2B – they work with shippers such as Tesla or Apple. As they depend on us to provide a great experience to their own customers, it’s essential that we have their backs at all times. As a Customer Support agent at [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/), your role will be to deliver happiness to our customers.  

We believe customer support plays a key role in the success of [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/). By creating great experiences for our customers, you create [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) champions that will generate repeat business and new customers through word of mouth.

## 🕵🏼‍♀️ What will you be doing?

*   Support our customers through email, live chat (Intercom) and phone.
*   Build relationships with our customers and partners to be our best salesperson and marketeer.
*   Advocate for our customers and be their voice inside our organization.
*   Work closely with the Product and Airline Partnerships teams to improve the user experience.
*   Contribute to the continuous development of our customer support function.

## 🙋🏽‍♀️ What do you bring?

*   You’re just great with people.
*   You have a service-mindset, are empathetic and know how to listen.
*   You have a positive attitude and know how to navigate your way even in situations of uncertainty.
*   You’re curious and actively seek new ways  to develop yourself.
*   You have excellent written and verbal communication skills.
*   You’re fluent in English, plus either French or Dutch
*   Ideally you’ve had previous experience at a fast-paced startup or similar environment.

## What can you expect from cargo.one?

*   The chance to contribute from day 1 with plenty of room for career progression.
*   The opportunity to have a serious impact on a growing organization that’s changing an industry.
*   An extremely steep learning curve working alongside an ambitious and international team.
*   MacBook or ThinkPad, the choice is yours – whatever helps you get your job done, we’ve got you covered.
*   Work within a fully-remote setup.
*   We offer a budget that can be used towards setting up the home office.
*   Company get-togethers

If there’s something important to you that’s not on this list, let us know.
