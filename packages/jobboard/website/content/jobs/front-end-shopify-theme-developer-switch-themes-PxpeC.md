## Description

Switch Themes builds themes exclusively for Shopify’s theme store.

We’re looking for a Front-End Developer who can assist us with rebuilding one of our Shopify themes.

This is a casual / contract role with an estimated 20 hours a week but based on performance can extend to full-time hours.

This is a remote position - ideally the candidate will be in a timezone that is close to Melbourne, Australia

## Requirements

**Your Role:**

We're currently rebuilding one of our Shopify Themes from scratch. This is using our custom Webpack tool and we will be removing jQuery dependency and using Tailwind CSS. We need a Front-End Developer to come onboard and assist us with this rebuild.

**Your experience and skills:**

*   Expert experience with building Shopify Themes and Shopify's liquid templating language
*   Expert knowledge in the Shopify platform and Shopify Themes
*   Understand all requirements for Shopify Themes to be listed on the theme store:[https://shopify.dev/tutorials/review-theme-store-r...](https://shopify.dev/tutorials/review-theme-store-requirements)
*   Strong web development skills (HTML, CSS and Javascript)
*   Experience with Webpack and using build tools
*   Strong Javascript skills (Vanilla JS)
*   Be able to follow designs exactly
*   Be able to take designs and apply the same principles to other templates that don't have designs

## Benefits

*   Work with a leading Shopify theme development company
*   Work from anywhere
*   Small team of 3-4 people
*   Great exposure to Shopify theme development and the theme store
