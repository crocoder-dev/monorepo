## The role

Myagi is on the lookout for an experienced Product Manager to join us on our high-growth journey revolutionising the way global brands connect with retailers. This role will have responsibility for delivering the product vision related to our customers that consume the content created by our brand partners. You will be part of the product team but work extremely closely with the engineering department and key stakeholders of projects. With a team that spans Australia🦘🇦🇺, US🇺🇸, UK🇬🇧 and Europe🇪🇺, you'll need to be able to work well in a highly autonomous and diverse environment.

This is a REMOTE role, but only candidates in Australasia or the US will be considered.

## Responsibilities

*   Work with partners and internal stakeholders to develop innovative solutions for new features and product improvements, across a range of product verticals within the platform
*   Own products or features throughout the product lifecycle -- from discovery, production, through to product retirement
*   Manage stakeholder and customer escalations when needed.
*   Using product research input, create detailed business and technical requirements, working alongside engineering and design to identify, develop, and bring concepts to market
*   Manage project timelines and work with stakeholders and the Product Owner to deliver the product roadmap
*   Make use of product analytics and user feedback to analyse and optimise existing functionality, and to drive product decisions and roadmap prioritisation
*   Contribute to improving and developing product management processes and workflows to optimise the product development cycle
*   Collaborate with marketing and the customer team to support user acquisition, onboarding initiatives and go-to-market strategies
*   Contribute to the strategic planning process and ensure continuing alignment of the product strategy with the overall company strategy
*   Drive product innovation which extends beyond needs/wants analysis to understanding market trends and long term value
*   Establish success criteria for product usage and perform ongoing measurement and monitoring, using input to inform roadmap and designs

## About you

*   Minimum 3+ years relevant work experience as a Product Manager
*   Experience gathering product requirements, and contributing to the overall product strategy & roadmap in support of company wide goals and objectives  
    
*   Proven success working with development teams using agile/scrum methodology
*   Track record of being able to critically analyse data samples and produce sensible insights to fuel product initiatives
*   Ability to challenge expectations and question the status quo on a day-to-day basis  
    
*   User research and analysis experience will be highly favourable
*   Experience working on native mobile applications
*   While this is not a engineering role, a sound understanding of software development best practices and enthusiasm for technical principles is a must
*   Experience working in a SaaS environment (is a plus!)

## Company

Myagi is a VC-backed, high-growth tech startup that’s changing the way brands and retailers share knowledge.

Our business-to-business network connects everyone in the retail supply chain and our SaaS platform combines online training and communication to equip sales associates with targeted brand and product knowledge.

For brands, we enable them to connect and distribute content directly to the retailers that stock their products around the world. There's a direct communication channel to frontline staff and they get real time feedback through detailed analytics.

For retailers, we integrate a combination of external supplier content with internal "tribal knowledge" to ensure that frontline staff have access to the right information, at exactly the right time, to help drive sales and enhance the customer experience.

We have a fast-growing network global customers, including some of the world's leading retailers and brands such as Nike, Gibson Guitars, Bayer, Unilever, Giant Bikes and Garmin... to name a few.
