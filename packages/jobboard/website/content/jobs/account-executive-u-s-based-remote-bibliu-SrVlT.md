We have partnered with 2000 educational publishers including Pearson, McGraw and Wiley. We now have 43 institutional customers, including NYU and Oxford. ## Position Overview:

The Account Executive will manage BibliU’s sales to academic libraries in the assigned territory. We are looking for an experienced high achiever with 3-5 years software sales experience meeting and exceeding goals in a growing company with sales goals that will grow year over year in the assigned set of accounts or territory.

Necessary skills include the ability to prospect and secure new business as well as to upsell and grow accounts over time and ensure contract renewals. Experience in the Education Technology space (preferably Higher Ed) and knowledge of publishing is recommended. The Account Executive must establish BibliU in a sales territory (either defined set of accounts or region) and grow the business in that territory over time. This role is key to the company’s successful growth.

## Job requirements

## Required Experience:

*   Has 3-5 years high-achieving software sales experience meeting established goals
*   Has track record of effective prospecting with high conversion rate into qualified opportunities
*   Has demonstrable experience in efficiently managing opportunities through the funnel
*   Has demonstrable experience increasing deal velocity and deal size
*   Track record of meeting and exceeding assigned targets
*   Comfortable working in the C-suite and with executives
*   Excellent written and verbal communication skills

## Preferred Experience:

*   Background in Educational Technology (preferably Higher Ed) with publishing-related areas a plus (nice to have, not need to have)
*   Has been trained on and adopts Miller-Heiman Strategic Selling, and/or other sales training programs (_e.g._ SPIN Selling, Question-based selling)

## Our Ideal Candidate also has:

*   Strong understanding of technology - both BibliU and general EdTech product offerings and SaaS sales tools such as CRM-Salesforce.com, Google Docs, Sheets, Slides
*   Demonstrated utilization of sales methodology to develop and close opportunities - preferably Miller Heiman Strategic Selling and/or the Challenger Sale
*   Familiarity of the publishing and electronic publishing ecosystem
*   Solid grasp of market dynamics and players in higher education
*   Works well in teams
*   Ability to effectively demonstrate technology
*   Familiarity with and excels at enterprise sales
*   Ability to accommodate travel (20-50%)
*   Prior academic achievement

## Areas of Responsibility:

*   Create an annual and quarterly plan to achieve the assigned quota
*   Prospecting to target accounts and lead follow-up to build a strong sales pipeline that’s at least 40% of target
*   Build sales pipeline to 3x net quota in first year in conjunction with Growth Marketing
*   Maintain sales forecast so that it is current with close dates, sales stages, amounts and net amounts.
*   Create Strategic Selling plans for top 20% of opportunities
*   Develop upsell strategies to grow accounts as needed.
*   Renew existing accounts in the assigned sales territory
*   Work with Growth Marketing on sales campaign strategies for assigned market
*   Work with Customer Success to support successful enterprise implementations
*   Work with Content to match and maximize sell through of content at defined accounts
*   Identify and work regional and national conferences and association relationships that will support goals

## Measures of Success:

*   Meet and exceed annual sales quota
*   Expand and renew existing accounts in the defined territory
*   Close strategic opportunities that will expand the reach and visibility of BibliU
*   Develop executive level relationships within the prospect and customer organizations
*   Leverage the partner ecosystem for sales leads and market presence
*   Successfully employ the company’s sales methodology to close and grow enterprise opportunities
*   Work effectively with other company groups to maximize success in the territory
*   Sales forecasting timeliness and accuracy

We strongly encourage candidates of all different backgrounds, experiences and identities to apply. Each new hire is an opportunity for us to bring in a different perspective and BibliU is committed to building an inclusive and supportive workplace where everyone can do rewarding work.
