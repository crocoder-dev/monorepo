**Join us in changing an entire industry**

We need you to help us shape the future of air freight! At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) we believe that we can only win as a team, and we want you to make an impact with us!

The opportunity 

Join our growing marketing team as an Account-based Marketing Manager, focusing on our top airline accounts. Your role will be essential to growing the supply side of [cargo.one](http://cargo.one) by leading the strategy and execution of impactful highly targeted account-based marketing activities that meet our ambitious growth objectives. In strong collaboration with the business development team, your emphasis will be on pipeline generation and accelerating existing opportunities. You’ll develop a deep understanding of the market and our partners by working in a highly data-driven way, owning your metrics and constantly measuring and optimizing performance, to experiment with different channels and content formats (such as white papers, video, events etc.). Be ready to roll-up your sleeves while at the same time having our long-term goals always top of mind.

**⚠️This position is remote.** The caveat is that you must be based within certain countries to be able to work with us. **F****ind out which countries qualify [here](https://www.cargo.one/remote-working).**

## What you’ll be doing:

*   Develop an account-based marketing strategy and execution plan for our top airline accounts 
*   Own, implement and manage all our account-based marketing activities metrics and tools
*   Take ownership of personas, customer insights and journeys and define the value proposition and most effective key messages, channels as well as formats.
*   Work closely with the business development team to develop account focused playbooks including outbound prospecting and inbound lead follow up and nurture tactics. 
*   Develop all types of content (e.g. white papers, case studies, videos etc.), create sales enablement collateral, organize events (online/ offline) and more, together with the marketing/ content team and other external service providers.
*   Manage different internal as well as external stakeholders and build strong relationships with them.
*   Build and then measure the success of account based marketing metrics to ensure business impact measuredBe responsible for the database growth and accuracy for named accounts, account/person engagement scores and sales/partner utilization of programs;

## What you’ll need:

*   You have 5+ years of B2B marketing experience, ideally in account-based marketing roles in growth-oriented enterprise software or SaaS organisations 
*   You’re a creative problem solver and T-shaped marketer with a track record of successful key account programs and content marketing activities, backed up by measurable ROI
*   You have a proven ability to think strategically, are super organized and fast in execution.
*   You thrive in a fast-paced environment where you have to manage multiple projects to meet deadlines 
*   You’re a great communicator with strong convictions who can inspire others and easily build relationships, both internally and externally with key stakeholders 
*   You're experienced in creating efficient processes and demonstrate a good understanding of pipeline management and metrics
*   Going above and beyond to exceed expectations is natural to you

## What you can expect from cargo.one? We think you will enjoy working at cargo.one, here are just a few of the things that we think make it a great place to work:

*   Work within a virtual-first setup in a team that puts high emphasis on creating a great company culture
*   The opportunity to have a serious impact on a growing organization that’s changing an entire industry.
*   An extremely steep learning curve, working alongside an ambitious and international team.
*   MacBook or ThinkPad, the choice is yours – whatever helps you get your job done, we’ve got you covered.
