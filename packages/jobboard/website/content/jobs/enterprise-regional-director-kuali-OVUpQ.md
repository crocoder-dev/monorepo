## Description

Kuali is looking for an Enterprise Regional Director for our Kuali Build product team. Kuali Build is an unbelievable new “no-code” forms and workflow tool that allows functional people in higher ed to create forms and approval processes without writing any code.

**Who are we**

Kuali builds software solutions for higher education. We help our customers — colleges & universities — focus on providing a fantastic education to students by decreasing their administrative costs. We work in a competitive space, ripe for innovation, with users ready to be delighted.

Our Culture

As a company, we are guided by our [cultural values](https://www.kuali.co/jobs#values):

*   Iterate to evolve
*   Cultivate openness
*   Act with accountability

*   Assume the best
*   Practice humility
*   Deliver amazing experiences

We work remotely, and have for years. Distributed work is in our bones, with a history of institutions working across state lines for many years. Our employees each work in the environment where they’re happiest, from Massachusetts to Hawaii. We work consciously to create a collaborative and healthy remote work culture, and we travel to meet in person a few times each year.

**Everyone should love their work.**

Kuali has been voted a [top place to work](https://topworkplaces.com/publication/saltlake/kuali/) for 3 years in a row by the Salt Lake Tribune. We also made [Forbes' list of America's Best Startup Employers](https://www.forbes.com/americas-best-startup-employers/#1100e1e65275) for 2020. We believe that diversity contributes to a broader collective perspective and results in a better company and products. We are working hard to increase the diversity of our team and we encourage everyone to consider being a part of it."

## Requirements

**WHAT YOU'LL BE RESPONSIBLE FOR**

*   Intelligently prospect for >70% of your deals
*   Drive new bookings
*   Create and deliver effective sales presentations to articulate our compelling value proposition
*   Effectively understand and meet prospects’ complex business and technical requirements
*   Understanding the competition and how to beat them
*   Own and manage responses to RFPs, RFIs and all other types of prospect communications
*   Leverage internal product management, marketing, and engineering resources to solve prospect needs and issues in the spirit of true team selling

**About You**

We're looking for a curious, enthusiastic, empathetic team player to help us connect with potential community members, execute on ideas, advocate for the customer, and contribute to a team culture built on trust and mutual respect. We believe the best results are achieved by teams who represent a broad range of ideas and perspectives. We value employees with diverse backgrounds and experiences.

*   5+ years of experience selling SaaS products to large and complex organizations
*   Experience in education technology or SaaS ERP a huge plus
*   Full sales cycle skills from research and discovery to software demonstrations to pricing and negotiation
*   Ability and interest in sourcing your own leads with an Account Based Management (ABM) approach
*   Proven "hunter” track record of consistently over-performing quota and ranking in the top-tier of the sales force
*   Proven ability to cut through barriers to get to multiple decision makers and departments within an account
*   Strong VP, C-level, and board member level communication skills
*   Willingness to travel 20-30% (when it’s safe to do so)
*   Goal-oriented and self-motivated
*   Excellent business writing, oral communication and interpersonal skills
*   Strong working knowledge of Salesforce and LinkedIn is preferred

If you have some of these too we’ll be delighted

*   You have experience in research administration and/or research compliance functions at an institution of higher education.
