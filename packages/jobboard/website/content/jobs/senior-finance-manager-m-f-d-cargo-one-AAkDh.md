**Join us in changing an entire industry**

We need you to help us shape the future of air freight! At [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) we believe that we can only win as a team, and we want you to make an impact with us!

**🏆** **About the opportunity**

We're looking for our first Finance Manager to lead us towards fast growth and long-term financial stability. You will report directly to one of the managing directors and work closely with the management team to develop and set the strategic direction of [cargo.one.You](http://cargo.one.You) will be proactive in optimising our existing processes, and implementing new ones to ensure that we have full oversight of our spending and revenue and can make well-informed decisions.

**⚠️This position is remote.** The caveat is that you must be based within certain countries to be able to work with us. **F****ind out which countries qualify** [**here**](https://www.cargo.one/remote-working)**.**

## 🕵🏼‍♀️ What you’ll be doing

*   Develop and maintain ongoing financial reportings.
*   Manage our external accounts and tax advisors.
*   Lead our annual audit process and manage audit remediation plans.
*   Work with the management team to identify and measure key financial metrics.
*   Lead our budgeting process.
*   Maintain and track performance against our targets.
*   Take responsibility for key commercial contracts.
*   Manage our billing and invoicing processes with partners.
*   Support financial due diligence processes.
*   Develop the financial strategy to grow [cargo.one](http://cargo.one/) by an order of magnitude.

## 🙋🏽‍♀️ What you'll need

*   You are up for a challenge and are willing to roll up your sleeves in a remote-first global setup.
*   You have performed or managed financial planning and analysis, accounting and commercial functions.
*   You have experienced and flourished in dynamic, quickly evolving high-growth companies.
*   You have magical excel skills and use data to drive your decision- making process.
*   You have great communication skills and love working with people and technology.
*   You are organised, structured in your approach to work and have a strong attention to detail.
*   You are decisive and [fact-oriented.](http://fact-oriented.You)
*   You have a BSc/BA in accounting, finance or relevant field.
*   MSc/MA is a plus.
