## Description

**Why?**

With so many companies transitioning to remote work, what we offer is more relevant than ever, and we are growing fast! We need an experienced Senior Counsel who can work with our client base across 150 jurisdictions.

**What?**

Omnipresent is ramping up operations globally, including hiring a second senior lawyer to help manage our global operations and expanding the legal team more broadly so there couldn’t be a more exciting time to join us.

**Who?**

You will have expertise in employment law, including managing cross-border employment issues but will have the aptitude and desire to take ownership of a far broader range of work.

**The detail**

**About Omnipresent**

Remote work is one of the great equalisers of our time, allowing people to secure employment opportunities from companies around the world, no matter where they were born or where they live.

Yet, at a time when remote work is exploding, most companies are finding it too complex, costly and time consuming to employ workers internationally. At Omnipresent, we’re cutting this complexity and providing our clients, and their remote workers, with a simplified, cost effective and legally compliant employment experience. With a network of legal entities across the world, coupled with an automation-focused tech platform, we’re building the premier global employment-as-a-service offering on the market.

**Your role**

We're searching for a Senior Counsel with a strong operational mindset and an ability to resolve a wide range of complex matters across 150 jurisdictions, independently and by engaging local counsel.

*   Advise our Sales and Customer Success teams on a wide range of employment law queries across 150 jurisdictions
*   Establish a database of fully compliant template employment contracts and related documentation across our 150 countries
*   Horizon-scanning, especially for notable regulatory developments in Employment law
*   Obtain appropriate jurisdictionally-specific legal advice and manage external counsel where necessary
*   Advise on our internal ESOP and benefits plans from a legal perspective
*   Draft, review and negotiate a wide range of commercial contracts, including service agreements
*   Other general corporate advisory work
*   Solve unforeseeable issues, legal or otherwise, as they arise

**You’ve done some of these things..**

*   Have 7-10 PQE total:
    *   At least 5 PQE gained in a highly-regarded law firm advising clients both domestically and internationally
    *   2+ years PQE in-house or working specifically with startups
*   Gained deep experience advising C-Suite management on complex employment issues

**You are the sort of person that..**

*   Is comfortable running teams of international local counsel to obtain accurate legal data
*   Has a solid understanding of company and contract law
*   Has a good understanding of multi-jurisdictional compliance

*   Has excellent legal and commercial acumen

**What’s in it for you (apart from the most fun and challenging ride of your life!)**

*   Shared ownership - Being a part of our journey means you will own a piece of Omnipresent
*   Annual vacation entitlement: 33 days including local holidays
*   Equipment and infrastructure: We give you a budget for all the equipment you need to work effectively wherever you are
*   Company retreats: We are planning biannual company-wide retreats post COVID, and well as socials and other team building activities
*   Flexible working - Work from anywhere in the world - We don’t care where or when you work as long as the job gets done. We are genuinely as flexible as the work allows
