## The Role

Hopin is seeking a Brand Designer who is self-driven, passionate, compassionate and ambitious. You will be working closely with senior sales and marketing leadership to turn goals into strategic and remarkable creatives that result in increased awareness, growth, and engagement for the brand.

## Experience comes in many forms and passion goes a long way. If your background is close to what we are looking for, please consider applying even if you can’t check every box.

## About you

*   Consistently deliver world class visual craft
*   Possess a strong curiosity, ability to deal with ambiguity, work autonomously, and thrives in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment
*   Approach every project with 100%. No project is underneath you

## Responsibilities

*   Collaborate with Hopin marketers and another brand designer to focus on the business goals
*   Quickly brainstorm and execute on solutions, to get the team conversation started
*   Include empathy and emotion in your designs
*   Bring a broad skill set: all about static assets, animations, email templating, infographics, typography
*   Innovate proposing new formats and adventurous campaigns
*   Work closely with the Product Designers to keep the Hopin language consistent
*   Always advocate for the best emotional design

## Qualifications

*   6+ years of multi-channel brand experience, creating world-class visual assets that bring the Hopin brand to life (ad creative, one-pagers, whitepapers, social media content and campaigns, pitch decks)
*   You're great at talking about design in business terms
*   You’re up to date with the latest trend
*   You have experience working with in-house teams and external agencies
*   Your portfolio showcases amazing and exciting projects
*   Proficiency with current industry standard design and collaboration tools (Figma, Sketch, Protopie, Principle, Framer…)
*   You’re vocal about design values and process, and are willing to challenge the current establishment
*   Pride in your work, but you are flexible in making changes and hearing others thoughts. Humility is really important at Hopin
*   You’re proactive at seeking feedback and explaining designs to engineers, product managers and other designers
*   You’re willing to build Hopin Design Culture with us

## Skills

*   You have great visual and illustration skills
*   You have great animation skills

## Communication

*   Fluent in English (written and spoken)
*   Excellent communication skills and an ability to work closely with creative and business stakeholders

## The offer

*   Competitive salary
*   Fully remote team, work from anywhere in the United States
*   Flexible schedule
*   Work laptop - Macbook Pro or DELL XPS
*   $800 USD to improve your home-office set up
