## Job description

**Location:** Remote (Amsterdam)

**Duration:** 4-6 months (part-time)

**Want to get hands-on experience in finance and accountingoperations management in a fast-growing network-based start-up?**

Riverflex is a consultancy working shoulder to shoulder with organisations to meet the challenges of the digital world. Instead of applying old methods in a new world, we tap the power of an open-talent ecosystem to deliver expertise and value.

We work with a community of over 900+ highly skilled independent consultants and experts on a broad range of topics to solve the challenges of the digital world.

Riverflex is ready for the changing nature of the workforce: join us and be part of the consulting firm of the future!

**Are you our next Intern?**

As our intern, your responsibilities will sit mainly within the Finance domain. You will focus on:

*   Workingalongside the accounts team to gain thorough insight on key processes, requirements, and financial systems, utilized in recording periodic transactions at Riverflex
*   Assisting management in performing various ad-hoc projects or analysis such as financial performance impact analysis, financial models and reports, and variance analysis of actual and planned results
*   Supporting the HR & Finance Manager with funding matters, entering data, and defining and preparing annual budgeting plans
*   Assisting in the preparation and analysis of routine statements and external reports
*   Liaising with the people, marketing, and tech teams to provide financial and analytical support in coherence with budgeting plans
*   Assisting in the management of accounts payable and cash management processes
*   Reviewing journal entries and supporting documentation to ensure accurate reporting
*   Reviewing processes and propose recommendations for improvement
*   Conducting analyses of and helping to execute strategic profit improvement initiatives for business
*   Supporting the collection and payment of invoices and following up on due accounts
*   Supporting with the payroll function and managing external service providers

**What we offer:**

**Development:** You will take on a role in the finance daily operations and will be working directly with our HR & Finance Manager on a day to day basis receiving on the job training and coaching. Your development will be actively managed and you will gain a strong breadth of experience in this role working across the business and alongside or management and leadership teams.

**Experience:** We offer you an exceptional experience to work for an exciting start-up with a highly motivated and ambitious team of incredibly smart individuals from a beautiful co-working space in the heart of Amsterdam.

**Challenge:** Freedom, responsibility, the possibility to test new ideas in a hands-on environment - if this sounds like a challenge you are up for **\-[apply now.](https://riverflex.recruitee.com/o/finance-intern)**

This is a paid internship, with an allowance of 500€/ month (full-time rate). Please note this will be a part-time role (3-4 days).

## Job requirements

## For this internship, you must:

*   Have a graduated from or are currently enrolled in a bachelor’s or master’s program in Finance, Accounting, Business Administration, or a related field
*   Be available to work between 28-32 hours per week (hours are flexible and negotiable)
*   Possess knowledge of basic principles and practices in accounting and financial analysis
*   Be enthusiastic about digital and start-up life
*   Have an excellent command of English
*   Be eligible to work (or search for work) in the Netherlands
*   Be available for 4-6 months, preferably as soon as possible
*   Possess a highly analytical acumen and a mindset oriented on problem-solving
*   Be able to gather, evaluate, and interpret data
*   Be proactive and driven
*   Have excellent attention to detail

## Preferred qualifications:

*   Experience in MS Excel (e.g., sum, if, finance functions, lookup, pivot tables, etc.)
*   Good command of Dutch
