## Position Overview

The VP of  SW Program and Operations will be responsible for delivering all platform software to our product program. This individual will lead the software program office, the software quality assurance and validation teams as well as our build and release operations. In this position, you will organize and drive our SW delivery machine together with the development team and will guarantee quality and timeliness of deliverables. You will support our product definition effort together with our product team as well as with our internal and external customers. You will ensure that our OS and cloud backend are productizable and package-able for various configuration and business units.

Aside from the delivery responsibility, we are looking for an innovative leader that will take our operations to the next level. This individual will need to constantly promote best practices, strive to increase developer productivity and strive to improve product reliability, performance, and quality assurance.

As importantly, this leader will be expected to  radically modernize our approach, and aim at redefining new processes across the organization to increase the flexibility and efficiency of our delivery machine while maintaining a high bar on quality and predictability.  

## Essential Duties and Responsibilities 

*   Lead the SW Operations & Productization organization comprising of multiple program managers, individual contributors and deliver the SW and UX for the computing platform of the future
*   Drive all SW activity towards timely delivery and ensure the quality of all SW deliverables
*   Guarantee quality of deliverables by driving internal QA operations and coordinating the SW QA operations
*   Implement processes consistent with Project Management Professional (PMP) and AGILE best practices within the SW organization
*   Lead the planning and implementation of software milestones and facilitate the definition of scope, goals and deliverables
*   Capture overall SW program resource requirements and manage resource allocation and budget
*   Identify key performance indicators, implement meaningful metrics to measure the efficiency and performance of our SW and constantly monitor, track and report our progress on SW 
*   Coordinates SW effort and report/align into the product program office
*   Select and drive the implementation of Magic Leap SW tool chain. Organize and synchronize SW QA, integration and Release management
*   Provide visibility on SW program status, key risks and mitigations, and technical milestones
*   Proactively identify potential issues and propose mitigation plans or alternative solutions
*   Create operations that are future proof and can be scaled to business units
*   Work with product to derive sw requirements and coordinate work and scope with our internal stakeholders
*   Continuously improve operations processes, develop and present best practices, technical concepts and technologies for innovative SW solutions
