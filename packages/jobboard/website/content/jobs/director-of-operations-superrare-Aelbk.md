## About SuperRare

SuperRare is a well-funded and fast-moving startup on a mission to reinvent the art market for the digital generation. Our platform is a social marketplace for cutting edge digital / 3D / VR creators and a new generation of natively-digital art collectors.

We've built a vibrant online economy for digital art powered by the Ethereum blockchain, with over $50M earned by artists and collectors over the past two years.

We are now growing our team and looking for passionate, creative people to join us on our journey!

## Operations at SuperRare

At SuperRare, our mission is to create a global art economy fit for the digital age. We are looking for a Director of Operations who is excited about digital art and NFTs to join our team. Reporting to the CEO, the Director of Operations will be responsible for creating the infrastructure to help the business scale by setting up and managing legal, financial, and human resources.

## Responsibilities

*   Planning and monitoring the day-to-day running of business to ensure smooth operation
*   Reviewing financial information and adjusting operational budgets to promote growth
*   Ensuring that the company runs with legality and conformity to established regulation
*   Overseeing business development and growth
*   Identifying and targeting areas in which a business can improve operation
*   Ensuring financial health and monitoring cash flows
*   Researching and implementing new directives for business growth
*   Developing and implementing guidelines for employee evaluations, recruitment and promotion
*   Managing procurement processes and coordinating material and resources allocation

## Qualifications

*   Proven experience as Director of Operations or equivalent position
*   Excellent organizational and leadership abilities
*   Outstanding communication and people skills
*   Knowledge of art markets and crypto’s current and upcoming regulations
*   Knowledge of startup legal rules and guidelines
*   Understanding of marketplace dynamics
*   Working knowledge of data analysis and performance/operation metrics
*   BSc/BA in business administration or relevant field; MSc/MA will be a plus

## You are an exceptional candidate for this role if you have:

*   Curiosity and passion about our industry and the willingness to roll your sleeves up
*   A desire to work in a startup environment with a high degree of ownership and independence
*   Experience with crypto and art regulation
*   Prior experience scaling a platform or consumer product
