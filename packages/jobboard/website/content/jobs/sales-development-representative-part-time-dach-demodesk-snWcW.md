**Mission**

Help us scale Demodesk by setting up and providing our outbound channel in the DACH market. You will work closely with our sales, marketing, and customer success teams to build a sales pipeline that will enable our long-term growth. Hit your monthly targets and evaluate different approaches for efficiency and effectiveness to find the best strategic sales processes.

## Goals

*   **Research the market:** Work with the sales development team to identify relevant sales targets in DACH.
*   **Engage new customers:** Execute outbound prospecting campaigns (emails, hot calls, video-mails, social selling, events) to generate leads in well-identified niche markets. 
*   **Develop new customers:** Understand customer needs and buying cycle to gather key prospect information, generate interest and bring in successful customers.
*   **Deliver results:** Pursue both individual and team sales goals and consistently hit targets on a monthly basis.
*   **Co-create a successful go-to-market strategy:** Be the eyes and ears of Demodesk in the market - being in the frontline, your inputs are critical to grow to the next level.
*   **Grow to a world-class sales team:** Sharpen your sales skillset together with the sales team and become a member of a fast-growing, high performing team

## Your Profile

*   You already have experience in communicating with customers, preferably in the B2B area 
*   Strong analytical skills to quickly analyze problems and strong people skills to quickly built relationships
*   Understanding that selling is more effective over the phone vs other channels
*   Passion for helping people, digital products and a hunger to learn about driving change in organizations
*   Native German and fluent English both spoken and written
*   Part-Time for 25-30h per week & ability to work at least 50% in our Munich office or come to Munich for a two-month onboarding program
